## Main Purpose of the Guide

This Google Workspace Learning Center guide introduces users to **Gemini for Google Workspace** and explains what they can learn to do with Gemini inside Workspace apps.

The guide focuses on using Gemini to:

- **Draft new content quickly**
- **Improve and polish writing**
- **Summarize content**
- **Organize data**
- **Create custom graphics**
- **Illustrate presentations**
- **Enhance meeting experiences**

## Availability & Requirements

### Feature Availability

Gemini for Google Workspace is only available if the user’s organization supports it.

- If Gemini is unavailable, users should contact their **Google Workspace administrator**.
- The guide is intended for organizations using supported Google Workspace plans/features.

### Platform Scope

- The instructions in this guide are for **computer only**.
- Mobile instructions are not covered in this specific guide.

### Required Account

Users need a:

- **Google Workspace account**

- **14-day Google Workspace trial** available via the linked signup page.
