## Main Feature: Rewrite Content in Google Docs

Gemini can help rewrite selected text in a Google Doc. It can be used to:

- Shorten content
- Elaborate on content
- Adjust tone
- Transform text into another format, such as:
  - A poem
  - A script
  - A story
  - A blog post
  - Bullet points

---

## How to Rewrite Content with Gemini in Google Docs

1. On your computer, open a **Google Doc** file.
2. Click where you want to write in the document.
3. Select the content you want to rewrite.
4. On the left, click **Help me write**.
5. Choose a rewriting option, or enter a custom prompt.
6. Review Gemini’s response and refine it as needed.
7. When finished, choose one of the following:
   - **Insert** — Adds the new content below the original.
   - **Replace** — Swaps the original content with the new Gemini-generated version.
