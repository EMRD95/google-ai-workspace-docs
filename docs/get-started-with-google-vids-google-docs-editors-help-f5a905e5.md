## Overview

**Google Vids** is an AI-powered video creation app for work. It lets users **create, write, produce, edit, collaborate on, and share videos**.

> Google Vids is an AI-powered video creation app for work. Use Vids to create, write, produce, edit, collaborate, and share your videos.

You can start a new video here:

> [Click here to create a video](https://docs.google.com/videos/create)

Or enter this shortcut in your browser:

```text
vids.new
```

## Feature Availability & Limits

### Availability

- Google Vids requires an **eligible Google Workspace subscription**.
- Gemini/AI features depend on your plan and supported language availability.
- Some features are still rolling out and may not be available to all users yet.

> **Important:** New features for Vids are rolling out gradually, and may not be available to you yet.

### Video Length Limit

- Videos created with Google Vids currently have a **maximum duration of 30 minutes**.
- Limits may change over time.

> Videos created with Google Vids have a maximum duration of 30 minutes. Limits are subject to change.

## Browser Compatibility

Google Vids works on computers with the **two most recent versions** of:

- **Chrome**
- **Firefox**
- **Microsoft Edge** — Windows only

Other browsers may work, but not all features may be available.

### Safari Limitation

> **Tip:** Currently, you can’t edit or comment in Vids in Safari. You can use playback after a video is processed.

## Compatible Media & File Types

Google Vids supports uploading media from your **computer** or **Google Drive**.

| Media Type | Supported Content | Compatible Upload File Types |
|---|---|---|
| **Audio** | Pre-recorded audio or voice-over; voice-over/audio recorded in Flix; AI-generated voice-over; your own sounds/music; stock sound effects/music | `ogg`, `flac`, `m4a`, `mp3`, `mp4`, `mpeg`, `mpeg3`, `wav`, `x-flac`, `audio/x-m4a`, `audio/x-wav` |
| **Static images and GIFs** | Stickers; your own images; stock images, including photography and illustrations | `PNG`, `JPEG`, `GIF` |
| **Videos** | Your own clips; videos made using Vids recording studio; stock videos | `mp4`, `ogg`, `quicktime`, `webm` |

## Create a New Video

You can create a brand-new video or create a new version by copying and editing an existing Vids file.

### Steps

1. On your computer, open a new video in [Google Vids](https://docs.google.com/videos/create?usp=hc).
   - Or enter:

   ```text
   vids.new
   ```

2. In the start menu, choose a format:
   - **Landscape**
   - **Portrait**
   - **Square**

3. Select a creation option.

### Creation Options

| Option | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Veo: Generate video clips*** | Enter a prompt to generate custom video clips from your ideas. |
| **AI avatar*** | Narrate with an avatar to add personality to your video. |
| **Convert Slides** | Turn a Google Slides presentation into a video. Each slide becomes a scene, and speaker notes are imported as scripts. |
| **Record** | Record yourself, your screen, or both. Build a video using video, audio, or screen recording. |
| **Upload** | Upload your own clips and images. |
| **Templates** | Start from a designed template. |
| **Storyboard*** | Use AI in Google Vids to plan your video story. |
| **Blank Vid** | Start a new video from scratch. |

\* AI options only appear if your account has access to AI features in Google Vids.

> If your account doesn’t have access to AI features in Google Vids, these options won’t appear in the getting started screen.

## Copy a Video

You can copy an existing Google Vids video to create a new or updated version.

### Steps

1. On your computer, open a video in [Google Vids](https://docs.google.com/videos/u/0/).
2. From the video, click:

```text
File → Make a copy
```

### Tips

To make a copy from Google Drive:

1. Right-click the video file to open the menu.
2. Click **Make a copy**.

You can copy:

- The **full video**
- **Selected scenes**

## Edit & Format a Video

Google Vids supports editing and formatting content within each scene. You can add and customize:

- Text
- Images
- Videos
- Audio
- Shapes and lines
- Object timing
- Scene transitions

### Related Editing Actions

- **Upload & insert images**
- **Add audio**
- **Add stock media**
- **Add & customize text, shapes, and lines**
- **Control timing with object tracks**
- **Customize transitions between scenes**
