# Use Gemini for Sales — Google Workspace Learning Center Summary

**Source:** Google Workspace Learning Center

## Core Purpose

This guide shows sales professionals how to use **Gemini for Google Workspace** to speed up common sales workflows, including:

- Drafting customer emails
- Preparing customer/prospect research
- Brainstorming leadership questions
- Tracking customer outreach
- Writing social posts
- Summarizing technical information
- Planning webinars
- Outlining presentations
- Generating visuals for slides
- Creating custom Google Meet backgrounds

Gemini is positioned as a way to generate **first drafts, templates, agendas, trackers, summaries, and visuals** that users can then refine.

---

## Important Usage Notes & Warnings

Before using Gemini in Google Workspace:

- **Gemini feature suggestions don’t represent Google’s views** and should not be attributed to Google.
- **Do not rely on Gemini as professional advice**, including:
  - Medical
  - Legal
  - Financial
  - Other professional advice
- Gemini may suggest **inaccurate or inappropriate information**.
- User feedback helps improve Gemini’s helpfulness and safety.
- Enterprise end users may submit feedback about generative AI features, but:
  - They are informed beforehand that feedback should not contain personal, sensitive, or confidential information.
  - Feedback data may be read by humans.

**Tip from the guide:**

> If you don't like Gemini's first suggestion for any given prompt, ask it to try again before inserting its suggestion

# Sales Use Cases & Prompts

## 1. Save Time Writing Email

**Goal:** Draft a personalized customer email asking for a meeting.

**Where to use Gemini:** Gmail  
**Tool:** Help me Write in a Gmail compose window

**How it helps:**

Gemini can draft an email in seconds. Users can ask it to include a personal detail, such as the customer’s city or a shared interest, then refine and send the result.

### Original Prompt

> "Email my customer asking for a meeting. I saw on their public job profile that they live in Charleston, South Carolina, so make a personal reference to that city."

---

## 2. Create a Framework for Customer Research

**Goal:** Prepare for a customer or prospect meeting.

**Where to use Gemini:** Google Sheets  
**Tool:** Ask Gemini in a blank Sheet to build a new table

**How it helps:**

Gemini can generate a structured research framework, including:

- Questions to research
- Possible information sources
- Ideas for organizing meeting preparation

### Original Prompt

> "Create a framework for researching a sales prospect or their company, to prepare for a customer meeting."

---

## 3. Brainstorm Questions for Leadership

**Goal:** Quickly prepare thoughtful questions for an executive meeting.

**Where to use Gemini:** Google Docs  
**Tool:** Help me Write to create new text

**How it helps:**

Gemini can generate questions tailored to a specific leadership audience and topic, such as company strategy or product direction.

### Original Prompt

> "Give me 12 thoughtful questions to ask a Chief Marketing Officer on their strategy for 2024."

---

## 4. Create a Customer Contact Tracker

**Goal:** Organize sales outreach tasks.

**Where to use Gemini:** Google Sheets  
**Tool:** Ask Gemini in a blank Sheet to build a new table

**How it helps:**

Gemini can quickly create a table for tracking customers to call, using placeholder data that can be replaced with real customer details.

### Original Prompt

> "Create a tracker for all the customers I need to call this week."

---

## 5. Create Personalized Social Media Posts

**Goal:** Draft posts for customer-facing promotion, such as webinar invitations.

**Where to use Gemini:** Google Docs  
**Tool:** Help me Write to create new text

**How it helps:**

Users can briefly describe the audience and topic, and Gemini generates a first draft that can be refined before posting.

### Original Prompt

> "Create a social media post inviting my customers to a webinar on [ _webinar topic_]."
