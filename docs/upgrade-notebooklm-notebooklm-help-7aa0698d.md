# Upgrade NotebookLM — Summary

**Source:** [NotebookLM Help: Upgrade NotebookLM](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16213268?hl=en)

NotebookLM can be upgraded for **higher usage limits**, **premium features**, and **expanded enterprise/security capabilities** through:

- **Google AI Plans**
- **Google Cloud**
- **Qualifying Google Workspace / Workspace for Education plans**

---

## Key Excerpts

> “You can upgrade NotebookLM to get additional capabilities, including higher limits and more features through Google AI Plans, Google Cloud, or a qualifying Google Workspace plan.”

> “Sharing a notebook does not change the source limit for any collaborator.”

> “Daily quotas are reset after 24 hours; monthly quotas are reset after 30 days.”

> “Your data is protected, and is not used to train NotebookLM unless you provide feedback.”

> “Google Workspace or Google Workspace for Education users. Your uploads, queries and the model's responses in NotebookLM will not be reviewed by human reviewers even when you provide thumbs up or down feedback, and will not be used to train AI models.”

> “Your uploaded files will remain within your GCP project. Data regionalization is honored.”

# Upgrade Options

## 1. Google AI Plans

NotebookLM offers multiple tiers through Google AI Plans:

| Plan | How to Upgrade |
|---|---|
| **NotebookLM Standard** | Free with a Gmail account, or with a qualifying Workspace / Workspace for Education license |
| **NotebookLM in Plus** | Sign up for a **Google AI Plus** plan |
| **NotebookLM in Pro** | Sign up for a **Google AI Pro** plan, or use a qualifying Workspace / Workspace for Education license |
| **NotebookLM in Ultra — 20 TB Plan** | Sign up for a **Google AI Ultra** plan, or use a qualifying Workspace / Workspace for Education license |
| **NotebookLM in Ultra — 30 TB Plan** | Same Ultra path; higher limits than 20 TB plan |

Plan management references:

- Google One plan management
- Workspace plan management
