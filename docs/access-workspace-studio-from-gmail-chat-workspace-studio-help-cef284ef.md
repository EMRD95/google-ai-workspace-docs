# Access Workspace Studio from Gmail & Chat

You can use Workspace Studio inside Google Chat and Gmail to automate everyday tasks without leaving your conversation or inbox.

## Access Workspace Studio

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/z_aa8j3VlOn39D8kpuZ9w29DmGXaEOrd_GOWjuLsWU2hT8zC9QKHaYDyBgqSNi_ZfpU=w895)

1. **Open the side panel:** The Workspace Studio side panel helps you discover and manage your flows without leaving your conversation or your inbox. On the top right of Gmail or Google Chat, click the **Workspace Studio icon** to open it.
2. **Discover:** Browse recommended flows for your current app. The simplest way to create your first flow is to pick a pre-built template from the Discover tab.
3. **My Flows:** You can use this tab to quickly check which flows are active or turn them off. If a flow has an error, a red badge appears to notify you of the issue. Click **Show me** to investigate.
4. **Activity:** Check ”Activity” to confirm your flows ran successfully. You can also investigate any errors here.
5. **Do more in Studio:** To build more powerful flows from scratch or manage advanced settings, click **Do more in Studio** to open the full editor at [studio.workspace.google.com](https://studio.workspace.google.com/).
