# Your Data and Using AI Features on YouTube — Summary

**Source:** YouTube Help — “Your data and using AI features on YouTube”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/14772770

---

## Scope: Covered YouTube AI Features

This notice applies to use of YouTube AI features, including:

- **Veo and Gemini Omni in Shorts**
- **Nano Banana and Gemini Omni in YouTube Create**
- **Dream Track in YouTube Shorts**
- **Edit with AI**
- **Photo to video**
- **Make me move in Shorts**
- **AI Image and AI Video in Effect Maker**
- **Posts or AI Stickers**

Collectively, these are referred to as **“YouTube AI Feature(s)”**.

Feature availability may vary by **country** and **language**.

---

## Key Excerpts

> Your use of YouTube AI features [...] must comply with YouTube’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines, and shouldn’t violate the copyrights or privacy rights of others.

> You're responsible for the content that you interact with, create and publish.

> If the content that you interact with, create and publish violates Community Guidelines, it will be removed and you may get a strike.

> Don't include confidential or personal information when creating content or interacting with YouTube AI Features and don't rely on the feature for medical, financial or legal advice.

> Nevertheless, please don’t enter confidential information (or other data you wouldn’t want a reviewer to see) in prompts.

---

## User Responsibility and Policy Compliance

When using YouTube AI Features:

- You must follow:
  - **YouTube Terms of Service**
  - **YouTube Community Guidelines**
- You must not violate:
  - Others’ **copyrights**
  - Others’ **privacy rights**
- You are responsible for:
  - Content you **interact with**
  - Content you **create**
  - Content you **publish**
- Violating Community Guidelines can result in:
  - Content removal
  - A YouTube **strike**

YouTube also warns users not to rely on these AI features for **medical, financial, or legal advice**.

## C2PA Manifest and Content Provenance

AI-generated content may include a **valid, signed C2PA manifest**.

If you create content by selecting the referenced AI/content provenance icon and then reuse or share that content on an external platform, the external platform may be able to access the embedded metadata.

### C2PA Standard

> This technology is based on an open standard for content origin and authenticity developed by the Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity (C2PA).

The C2PA standard helps users understand the **authenticity** and **origin** of media, including:

- Images
- Videos
- Audio

## Privacy Notice: Who Provides YouTube AI Features

YouTube AI Features are provided by:

- **Google Ireland Limited** in the **European Economic Area and Switzerland**
- **Google LLC** everywhere else

The article refers to both entities collectively as **Google**.

This privacy notice works together with Google’s broader **Privacy Policy**.

---

## What Data Google Collects

Google may collect the following when you use YouTube AI Features:

- **Prompts**
- **Outputs**
- Content you share with AI features, such as:
  - Images
  - Videos
- **Related content**
- **Feedback**

---

## How Google Uses the Data

Google may use collected AI Feature data to:

- Provide Google products and services
- Improve Google products and services
- Develop Google products and services
- Improve and develop **machine-learning technologies**

---

## Reviewing and Deleting AI Feature Activity

You can review and delete past AI Feature data in **My Activity**, including:

- Prompts
- Outputs
- Related content

If certain content cannot be managed through My Activity:

- It is handled according to Google’s Privacy Policy
- It is deleted in **up to 60 days**

---

## YouTube AI Feature Activity Retention

**YouTube AI Feature Activity is on by default.**

### Default Retention

YouTube stores AI Feature data with your YouTube Account for:

- **Up to 18 months** for:
  - Prompts
  - Outputs
  - Related content

Prompts and outputs **not connected to your Google Account** are stored for:

- **Up to 3 years**

### Data Collected Before May 8, 2025

For YouTube AI Feature prompts, outputs, and related content collected by Google **before May 8, 2025**:

- Data connected with your Google Account is saved for:
  - **Up to 60 days**
- Outputs not connected to your Google Account are stored for:
  - **Up to 1 year**

## Avatar Data: Additional Collection, Use, and Storage Details

YouTube provides extra details for avatar-related AI features involving recordings of your **face** and **voice**.

### What Avatar Data Includes

Avatar-related data may include:

- Recordings of your face and voice, referred to as **“your recordings”**
- Data derived from your recordings
- Data generated from your recordings

Some face and voice data, including derived or generated data, may be considered **biometric data** in certain jurisdictions.
