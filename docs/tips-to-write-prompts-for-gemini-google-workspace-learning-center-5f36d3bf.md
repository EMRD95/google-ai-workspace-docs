# Tips to Write Prompts for Gemini  
**Source:** Google Workspace Learning Center — “Tips to write prompts for Gemini”  
**Context:** Gemini for Google Workspace productivity guide

---

## Core Purpose

To get better results from **Gemini for Google Workspace**, users should follow basic prompt-writing best practices. Good prompts should be natural, specific, contextual, and broken into manageable tasks when needed.

---

## Key Prompt-Writing Best Practices

### 1. Use Natural Language

Write prompts conversationally, as if speaking to a friend or colleague.

| Instead of... | Try this... |
|---|---|
| `"Training plan."` | `"Write a training plan for the sales team for the launch of a brand new product."` |

**Takeaway:** Avoid short, vague fragments. Ask in complete, natural sentences.

---

### 2. Be Clear and Concise

Gemini can follow precise instructions, so avoid ambiguity.

| Instead of... | Try this... |
|---|---|
| `"Marketing talking points."` | `"Give me 12 thoughtful questions to ask a Chief Marketing Officer on their strategy for 2024."` |

**Takeaway:** Specify the exact output you want, including quantity, audience, and topic.

---

### 3. Provide Context

The more context you provide, the more useful Gemini’s response can be.

| Instead of... | Try this... |
|---|---|
| `"Write about a sales job."` | `"Write a job description for a _[job title]_, including the required skills and experience, as well as a summary of _[company name]_ and the position."` |

**Takeaway:** Include relevant background such as role, company, audience, goal, or constraints.

---

### 4. Use Specific and Relevant Keywords

Gemini can understand individual words and phrases, so using relevant keywords helps it generate more accurate and informative responses.

| Instead of... | Try this... |
|---|---|
| `"Create project plan."` | `"Create a project plan for the launch of a brand new product. The timeframe should be from now until June 2024."` |

**Takeaway:** Include keywords tied to the task, deliverable, subject, and timeline.

---

### 5. Break Down Complex Tasks into Separate Prompts

If a task involves several related actions, split it into multiple prompts.

**Reason:** This helps Gemini better understand each step and provide more useful responses.

**Example reference:** Google links to an example showing how to use Gemini to recruit and hire for a job opening.

## Actionable Prompt Formula

A strong Gemini prompt should usually include:

- **Task:** What you want Gemini to do  
- **Context:** Background information Gemini needs  
- **Audience:** Who the output is for  
- **Format:** Desired structure or output type  
- **Constraints:** Timeline, length, tone, number of items, or required details  

### Example Structure

```text
Write/Create/Summarize [specific deliverable] for [audience/purpose].
Include [required details].
Use [tone/format].
The timeframe/constraint is [specific requirement].
```

---

## Quick Do / Don’t Summary

| Do | Don’t |
|---|---|
| Use conversational, natural language | Use vague fragments |
| Be specific about the desired output | Assume Gemini knows your intent |
| Provide background context | Ask broad, context-free questions |
| Include relevant keywords | Use generic wording |
| Split complex tasks into smaller prompts | Combine many tasks into one unclear prompt |
| Review outputs carefully | Treat Gemini responses as guaranteed accurate |
