## What AI Overviews Are

**AI Overviews** are AI-generated summaries shown in Google Search results when Google’s systems determine that generative AI may be especially helpful.

They are intended to help users:

- Find information **faster and more easily**
- Get a **snapshot of key information**
- Access **links to supporting sources** for deeper research
- Quickly understand information gathered from a range of sources

Google is gradually expanding AI Overviews to more users, languages, and regions.

## AI Overviews with Image Search and Circle to Search

AI Overviews may also appear when users:

- **Search with an image**
- Use **Circle to Search**

This applies in countries, territories, and languages where AI Overviews are supported.

---

## Can You Turn Off AI Overviews?

No. Google states that AI Overviews are a **core Search feature**, similar to knowledge panels, and **cannot be turned off**.

However, users can choose the **Web filter** after performing a search.

### Web Filter

The **Web filter** displays only text-based links and removes Search features such as:

- AI Overviews
- Other enhanced Search result features

---

## How AI Overviews Work

AI Overviews use **generative AI**, a type of artificial intelligence that learns patterns and structures from training data and uses them to generate new content.

Google emphasizes that this technology is still evolving and may produce:

- Inaccurate information
- Offensive information
- Mistakes or misleading responses

---

## How to Double-Check AI Overview Responses

Google recommends users verify important information rather than relying solely on AI Overviews.

### Recommended Checks

- **Check important information in more than one place**
  - Open the supporting links shown in the AI Overview
  - Compare with other Google Search results
- **Ask multiple versions of your question**
  - Try rephrasing
  - Ask for different opinions
  - Ask for more detail
- **Send feedback**
  - Report anything that appears incorrect, biased, unhelpful, or problematic

## Continue from AI Overview into AI Mode

Users can transition from an AI Overview into a deeper conversational experience in **AI Mode**.

### Availability

This feature is available on **mobile devices** in regions and languages where both:

- AI Overviews
- AI Mode
