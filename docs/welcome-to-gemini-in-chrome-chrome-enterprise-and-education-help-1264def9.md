## Get started with Gemini in Chrome

# Welcome to Gemini in Chrome

[Previous: Gemini in Chrome Learning Center](https://support.google.com/chrome/a/answer/17034491?hl=en&ref_topic=17031489)[Next: What you'll need](https://support.google.com/chrome/a/answer/17031593?hl=en&ref_topic=17031489)

![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/87a2a960-8c17-4849-922e-e991ef3766f9)

Gemini in Chrome provides AI assistance directly in your Chrome browser. Based on the context of your open tabs, Gemini in Chrome can summarize content, extract insights from videos, help you troubleshoot issues in real time, and much more. With auto browse, Gemini in Chrome can help you complete complex multi-step tasks.

**Note:** The instructions in this guide are for computers—MacOS, Windows, or Chromebook Plus devices.

## Features

![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/mxcfaH9F3EV18pEMyhFNrn5CDdRDL6OkQBr6)

#### Gemini in Chrome (Side panel)

Gemini in Chrome lives in a side panel within Chrome browser and provides context-aware help, based on your open tabs. It also connects with your Google Workspace—Gmail, Calendar, Drive, and so on.

**Useful for:** Summarizing long complex articles, summarizing trends from log data across up to 10 open tabs/reports, and drafting emails or documents using information from the page that you’re currently viewing.

![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/4gByRAmJildHjKTxmOR3fDlMKrNHuwEa2j4Q)

#### Auto browse

Auto browse lets Gemini in Chrome directly navigate and interact with websites to carry out complex multi-step tasks. Gemini in Chrome can click buttons, fill forms, and navigate websites on your behalf.

**Useful for:** Conducting consumer research, finding and booking flights and accommodation for a conference, finding and saving trip expense receipts from your email as PDFs in a specific Drive folder.
