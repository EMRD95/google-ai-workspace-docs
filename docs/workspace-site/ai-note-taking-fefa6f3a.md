## Take notes for me

With "Take notes for me" in [Google Meet](https://workspace.google.com/products/meet/), you can focus entirely on the conversation while AI captures meeting details in real time. The feature organizes the notes in a [Google Doc](https://workspace.google.com/products/docs/) and saves it directly to your [Drive](https://workspace.google.com/products/drive/), which frees you up to fully participate in the discussion. Try this and more in [Google Workspace with Gemini](https://workspace.google.com/solutions/ai/).

## Tips for smarter meeting notes

Get the best results from your AI meeting note taker by:

- Having clear audio and eliminating outside noise
- Prepare and follow an agenda for the meeting
- Reviewing and editing AI-generated notes for accuracy
