Senior Director, Customer Marketing, Google Workspace

#### Six sessions not to miss at our premier digital event

##### Google Workspace Newsletter

Keep up with the evolving future of work and collaboration with insights, trends, and product news.

[SIGN UP](https://workspace.google.com/?source=gafb-blog_article-body-en#newsletter)

On September 24th, we're thrilled to present **Gemini at Work**, our first-ever digital event dedicated to exploring the power of AI in the workplace. We'll showcase how customers are using Gemini to drive real business outcomes and achieve more at work every day with the help of AI. We'll also unveil new AI innovations, share proven best practices, deliver a masterclass in prompting, and share proven best practices on using Gemini for Workspace, the AI-powered assistant across Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and more.

I'm particularly excited to hear from our customers about how **Gemini** is changing how they work. For example, Randstad, a global talent solutions provider, is using Gemini to streamline job description creation and communication in multiple languages and across 39 offices. Join us to hear more from Randstad, Trimble, Avery Denison, Click Therapeutics, and more.

### Don't miss the ‘Working with AI’ breakout session track after the keynote that includes the following sessions:

**1. Spotlight: AI innovations in Gemini for Workspace:** Join Aparna Pappu, VP and GM of Google Workspace, as we dive into how Gemini can unlock your productivity and creativity — all while keeping your privacy and security a top priority. [Learn more](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24?talk=t3_sptl_achievemorewithgemini).

**2. Beyond the AI Hype:** How organizations are driving impact with Gemini for Workspace In this session, we'll share best practices and adoption strategies from real enterprise customers, Trimble and Avery Dennison, who are using Gemini to deliver measurable value across their organizations. [Learn more](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24?talk=t3_s1_beyondtheaihype).

**3. Master the art of prompting:** Our prompting “masterclass” will teach you how to craft effective prompts with Gemini, saving you time, enhancing your creativity, and maximize the use of AI in your workday. [Learn more](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24?talk=t3_s2_promptingmasterclass).

**4. Work safely and securely in the AI era:** Learn how Gemini for Workspace manages data privacy and compliance, grounding AI responses in your documents and emails, for a safer way to collaborate and remain compliant with regulations such as GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 1/2/3 and more. [Learn more](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24?talk=t3_s3_keepyourorganizationsdataprivate).

**5. Cut through the noise at work with Google Vids:**Learn more about new AI-powered video creation app, Google Vids, and how organizations can tell engaging stories with video to captivate their audiences.. [Learn more](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24?talk=t3_s4_breakthroughthenoise).

**6. Improve workflows through seamless integrations.**Discover how Google Workspace integrates with your existing business applications, helping to automate or eliminate repetitive tasks and dramatically improve workflows to increase productivity. [Learn more](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24?talk=t3_s5_achievedramaticproductivity).

Don't miss this opportunity to learn how Gemini can help you and your organization achieve more at work. Whether you're just at the beginning of exploring new AI capabilities, or ready to pilot and scale AI solutions, this event will equip you with the knowledge and tools to succeed. [Register today](https://cloudonair.withgoogle.com/events/gemini-at-work-24)
