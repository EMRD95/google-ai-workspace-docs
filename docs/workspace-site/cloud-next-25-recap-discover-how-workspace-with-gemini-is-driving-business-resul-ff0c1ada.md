Last week at Google Cloud Next in Las Vegas, we heard from dozens of Workspace customers across virtually every industry. In addition to [bringing new AI-powered workflows and even more Gemini capabilities into the Workspace tools millions of businesses rely on every day](https://workspace.google.com/blog/product-announcements/new-ai-drives-business-results?e=48754805), we held more than 30 sessions, panels, and spotlights, covering everything from driving innovation and unlocking employee productivity to human expertise and empathy in the AI world. Along the way, we heard how global industry leaders, nonprofits, and local small businesses are using Workspace with Gemini to build the future of work — today.

We heard how Workspace with Gemini helps brands like **Etsy**, **DocuSign**, **Motorola Solutions**, **ServiceNow**, **Rivian**, **Verizon** and many more transform the way they work — from slashing the time it takes to gain customer insights to improve service to boosting any-time collaboration to generating code so developers can focus on higher-value tasks.
