## Overview

Google Photos offers generative AI creation tools on Android, mainly:

- **Remix** — Reimagines photos using AI templates.
- **Photo to video** — Turns a still photo into a short dynamic video using effects or text prompts.

These tools are found in the **Create** tab of the Google Photos app and require eligible accounts, backed-up photos, and an internet connection.

---

## Availability & Usage Limits

- Features are **not available in all regions**.
- Functionality may vary depending on location.
- Some features may have extra eligibility requirements.
- Photos must be **backed up** before editing.
- A **network connection** is required.
- Users can generate only a **limited number of photos or videos per day**.
- Buying a **Google AI plan** increases the number of available generations.

---

## Eligibility Requirements

To use these AI creation tools:

- You must be **at least 13 years old**.
- You need a **personal Google Account**.
- These features are **not available** for:
  - Google Workspace accounts
  - School accounts

Additional age restrictions:

- **Remix** is available only to users **18 years old and over**.
- The **text prompt** feature in Photo to video is initially available only to users **18 years old and over**.

# Remix Your Photos

## What Remix Does

**Remix** is a tool in the Google Photos **Create** tab that uses AI templates to turn photos into unique, shareable creations.

It allows users to:

- Browse featured or full template selections
- Apply creative styles or visual effects
- Generate personalized or template-based AI creations
- Save, share, regenerate, or send feedback on results

---

## How to Use Remix on Android

1. Open the **Google Photos** app on your Android device.
2. At the bottom, tap **Create**.
3. Open **Remix** in one of two ways:
   - Under the **“Remix your photos”** header, select a template.
   - Tap **Remix** to view available templates.
4. If using the feature for the first time, an acknowledgement screen appears.
   - Tap **Try now** to continue.
5. Choose a template.
6. Tap **Generate**.
7. After generation, you can:
   - Tap **Save** to save the result.
   - Tap **Share** to share it.
   - Tap **Regenerate** to get a different result using the same template.
   - Tap **Send feedback** to share your experience and help improve the feature.

---

## Browsing Remix Templates

You can start from featured templates or browse all options.

### Start with a Featured Template

1. Under **“Remix your photos”**, find the featured template cards.
2. Scroll through available templates.

### Browse All Templates

1. Tap **View all**.
2. Scroll through every template option.

### Filter Templates

1. Tap specific category labels.
2. Scroll through the filtered template options.
3. To try another creative direction, tap a new category at any time.

---

## Remix Template Types

### Personalized Templates

Personalized templates are marked with a **badge**.

Key details:

- They use insights from your life to create unique and relevant outputs.
- Some personalized templates do **not require uploading an image**.
- For some templates, generation starts immediately after selection.
- To use badged templates, you must meet the **eligibility requirements for Gemini features in Photos**.

### Other Templates

Other templates apply specific creative styles or visual effects to generate a photo of you.

These templates usually require a **reference photo** before generation.

#### Upload a Reference Photo

To upload from your photo library:

1. Tap **Upload your own reference photo**.
2. Tap **Add photo**.
3. Choose from suggested photos or scroll through your library.
4. To change the uploaded photo, tap **Edit**
