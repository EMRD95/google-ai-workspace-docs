# Learn about availability of Gemini features in Google Vids — Summary

**Source:** Google Docs Editors Help — <https://support.google.com/docs/answer/15609411>  
**Topic:** Which Google account types can use Gemini features in **Google Vids**, and what usage limits apply.

---

## Key Excerpts / Important Notes

> **Google Vids is an AI-powered video creation app. Use Vids to create, write, produce, edit, collaborate, and share your videos.**

> **For work or school accounts, your administrator controls access to Google Vids.**

> **Important:**
>
> - For work or school accounts, your admin controls access to Google Vids and can turn off access to generative AI features.
> - Make sure you sign in with the correct Google Account that can access the feature.
> - If your account doesn’t have access to a Google Vids feature, the associated option won’t display in the “Getting started” screen.

---

## Core Availability Rules

You can use **basic Google Vids features** with a Google Account.  
To use **Gemini features in Google Vids**, availability depends on account type and plan.

Account categories covered:

- **Work / Business accounts**
- **Education accounts**
- **Personal Google Accounts**
- **Google AI accounts**
- **Workspace add-ons**
- **Workspace Individual**
- **Workspace for Nonprofits**

---

## Default Monthly Usage Limits

Unless a table specifies otherwise, Gemini feature limits in Google Vids are:

| Feature | Default limit |
|---|---:|
| **AI avatars** | Up to **25 AI avatar generations/month** |
| **AI video clips** | Up to **50 video clip generations/month** |
| **Help me create** | **Unlimited** |
| **AI voice-over** | **Unlimited** |
| **Image generation & editing** | **30/month*** |
| **Background removal** | **Unlimited** |
| **Slides to Vids with AI voiceovers** | **Unlimited** |
| **Music generation** | Account-type specific |
| **Slides to Vids with AI Avatars** | **10/month** |

\* Image generation/editing limits apply **across Workspace products**, including **Google Slides** and **Google Docs**.
