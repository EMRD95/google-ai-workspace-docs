## What AI Overview in Gmail Search Does

Gmail can generate an **AI Overview** when you search your inbox using **natural language questions** or **key phrases**, such as:

- `"When is my flight to Hawaii?"`
- `"Find the latest Cymbal Team Meeting notes."`

Based on relevant emails, Gmail can:

- Provide a **key fact or summary** directly above search results.
- **Synthesize information across multiple emails** into one answer.
- Save time by reducing the need to open and read individual messages.

AI Overviews work best with **descriptive natural language queries**. If no overview appears, try asking a more specific question or using a more descriptive phrase.

## Eligibility & Requirements

### Availability

- Available only in the **United States**
- Available only in **English**
- Gradually rolling out, so it may not appear for all eligible users yet

### Personal Google Accounts

You need one of these eligible Google AI plans:

- [Google AI Plus](https://support.google.com/googleone/answer/16882689)
- [Google AI Pro](https://support.google.com/googleone/answer/14534406)

You need an eligible **Google Workspace plan**.

- [Learn more about Gemini features and plans](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13952129#zippy=%2Cgoogle-workspace-business-plans)

### Smart Features Requirement

You must turn on smart features:

- Smart features for **Workspace**
- Separate smart features setting for **Gmail, Chat, and Meet**

Related settings page:

- [Smart features in your account](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/15604322#change_smart_feature_settings)

### Gmail App Limitation

- AI Overviews in the **Gmail app** are available only with a **Google AI Plus, Pro, or Ultra** plan.
- For **work or school accounts**, the feature is limited to the **web**.

## How to Get an AI Overview in Gmail Search

1. On your computer, open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/).
2. In the search box at the top, enter a **natural language question** or **key phrase**.
3. Press **Enter**.

### Search Operators Are Not Supported

AI Overview is not available if your search uses operators such as:

```text
is:unread
from:
```

To use AI Overview, ask a natural language question instead of using Gmail search operators.

## How Gmail Generates AI Overview Answers

When you search:

1. Gmail finds the **most relevant emails**.
2. Gmail generates an AI Overview using information from those emails.
3. The result is shown as a **direct summary or answer** above search results.

The feature is designed to help you avoid manually reviewing individual messages.

---

## Feedback on AI Overviews

You can rate an AI Overview directly next to the result by selecting:

- **Good suggestion**
- **Bad suggestion**

This sends feedback about the quality of the AI-generated overview.

## Privacy & Data Controls

To control use of your data for AI-powered features, update your **Google Workspace smart feature settings**.
