## Overview

Gemini in Chrome can be customized to better match your style, preferences, and needs. Customization options include:

- Saving and reusing prompts as **Skills**
- Connecting Gemini to supported Google apps
- Using **Personal Intelligence** for more personalized responses
- Changing keyboard shortcuts
- Changing the voice used by **Gemini Live**

# Skills in Gemini in Chrome

## What Skills Do

Skills let you save useful AI prompts and reuse them with one click on any page.

> A skill works directly with the page you currently see. When you use a skill, Gemini in Chrome reads the content on your active tab and applies your saved instructions to it.

You can:

- Save custom prompts
- Run saved prompts on the active tab
- Browse built-in skills
- Remix ready-to-use skills from the built-in library

### Example Skills

- **Protein max:** Change a standard recipe by inserting high-protein substitutes wherever possible.
- **Check job fit:** Analyze a job description against your background and resume to determine fit and highlight relevant experience.
- **Explain simply:** Translate complicated text into simpler terms and analogies understandable to a kindergartener.

---

## Requirements for Using Skills

To use Skills in Gemini in Chrome, you must:

- Use a **Chromebook Plus**, **Mac**, or **Windows** computer
- Use the latest version of Chrome
- Have Gemini in Chrome turned on
- Sign in with a **personal Google Account**
- Use Gemini in Chrome with Chrome language set to **English (United States)**
- Be **18 years or older**

> **Note**: Skills are not currently available for managed or Enterprise accounts unless your organization's administrator has enabled the feature.

## Manually Enable Skills During Rollout

Skills are rolling out gradually. If you do not see them yet, you can try enabling them manually:

1. Check that you're on Chrome version 147 or higher by typing **chrome://version** into your address bar.
2. Go to chrome://flags and search for "Skills." Enable the Skills flag.
3. Relaunch Chrome.
4. Open the Gemini side panel and type / to confirm skills are available.

Important Chrome URLs:

```text
chrome://version
chrome://flags
```

## Browse Recommended Skills

To browse built-in skills:

1. Open Chrome on your computer.
2. Click **Ask Gemini** at the top of the browser.
3. In the text box, type a forward slash:

```text
/
```

4. Scroll through available skills.
   - Tip: Hover over a skill to learn what it does.
5. Click the skill you want.
6. Press **Enter** or click **Send**.

For a fuller list of available skills, enter:

```text
chrome://skills/browse
```

You can filter by skill type or search by name.

---

## Create Your Own Skills

You can create unlimited custom skills.

Steps:

1. Open Chrome.
2. Click **Ask Gemini**.
3. Type `/` in the text box to open the Skills menu.
4. Click **Add Skill +**.
5. Enter a name and prompt for the skill.
6. Click **Save**.

To reuse a custom skill later:

```text
/skill name
```

Type `/` followed by the custom skill’s name.

---

## Manage Your Skills

You can edit, create, delete, or copy skill instructions.

Steps:

1. Open Chrome.
2. Click **Ask Gemini**.
3. Type `/` to open the Skills menu.
4. At the bottom, click **Browse Skills**.
5. Choose an action:
   - **Change an existing skill:** click **Edit**
   - **Create a new skill:** click **+ Add**
   - **Delete a skill:** click **More > Delete**

Direct link to your skills page:

```text
chrome://skills/yourSkills
```

Additional tips:

- To reuse a skill’s text, click **More > Copy Instructions**
- In the Edit skill popup, click the pencil icon to make instructions more robust

# Connected Apps in Gemini in Chrome

## What Connected Apps Do

Connected Apps allow Gemini in Chrome to work with supported Google services.

> **Important:** Only some Connected Apps are available in Gemini in Chrome.

If Google Workspace is connected, Gemini in Chrome can help you:

- Summarize emails in Gmail
- Add items to Google Tasks
- Create events in Google Calendar

---

## Google Apps Gemini in Chrome Can Connect To

> **Note:** In some countries, Connected Apps do not use Personal Intelligence to tailor your answer.

Gemini in Chrome currently supports connections to:

- **Google Workspace**, including:
  - Gmail
  - Google Calendar
  - Google Docs
  - Google Drive
  - Google Keep
  - Google Tasks
- Google Photos
- YouTube
- Search services
