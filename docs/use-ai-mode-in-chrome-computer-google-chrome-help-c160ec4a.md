# Use AI Mode in Chrome — Summary

**Source:** Google Chrome Help — “Use AI Mode in Chrome”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/16704170

## Overview

AI Mode in Chrome lets users ask questions, receive AI-powered answers, ask follow-up questions, and get helpful links directly from Chrome. It improves search relevance by breaking a query into subtopics and searching for them simultaneously.

> “When you use AI Mode, it divides your questions into subtopics, then searches for each one simultaneously. This way, AI Mode finds more relevant content that matches your query.”

**Important caveat:**

> “AI responses may include mistakes.”

Google links to additional information about how data helps develop generative AI in Search.

---

## Availability & Requirements

### Availability

**Important:** AI Mode in Chrome is not available to everyone or on every device yet.

> “This feature is gradually coming to people outside the US and to non-English languages, so it might not be available to you right now.”

### Recommended setting

For the best experience, Google recommends enabling **Web & App Activity** in **My Activity**:

- Go to **My Activity**
- Turn on **Web & App Activity**

If this setting is off:

- You can still use AI Mode.
- You **can’t pick up where you left off** with previous searches.

## What AI Mode Can Do in Chrome

From Chrome’s search box or address bar, you can:

- Enter detailed AI-powered search queries
- Ask follow-up questions
- Access AI Mode results
- Add recent tabs
- Upload an image or file
- Use AI tools such as:
  - **Canvas**
  - **Create Images**
  - **Deep Search**
- Choose a model for your question
- Use voice input
- Ask about the current page you’re browsing
- View AI Mode history

Google also links to a related feature for using AI to search and chat about tabs.

## Get Started with AI Mode in Chrome

### From the New Tab Page

1. Open **Chrome** on your computer.
2. Open a **New Tab** page.
3. On the right side of the search box, select **AI Mode**.
   - Or use the keyboard shortcut:

     > **Tab** + **Enter**

4. On the left side of the search box, select the “add” / tools option. You can:
   - Add recent tabs
   - Upload an image or file
   - Apply tools, including:
     - Canvas
     - Create Images
     - Deep Search
   - Choose a model for your question

5. To select from previous activity, choose **View your AI Mode history**.
6. Enter a question. Chrome directs you to the **AI Mode results page**.

---

### From the Address Bar

1. Open **Chrome** on your computer.
2. On the right side of the address bar, select **AI Mode**.
   - Or use the keyboard shortcut:

     > **Tab** + **Enter**

3. Enter your question.

Optional actions:

- To use your voice, select the **Microphone** icon.
- To add media, tools, or models to your question, select the add/tools option.
- To ask about the current page you’re browsing, select the add/tools option.

### Address Bar Tips

- When you ask about the current page, the result opens in the **AI Mode side panel**.
- To hide **AI Mode** and **Add tabs and more** from the address bar:
  1. Right-click the address bar.
  2. Unselect **Always show AI Mode**.
- To check AI Mode history from the address bar, select **View your AI Mode history**.

## Delete AI Mode History Saved in Your Account

To delete AI Mode history:

1. Open **Chrome** on your computer.
2. Open a **New Tab** page.
3. On the right side of the search box, select **AI Mode**.
4. On the left, select **AI Mode history** to find past searches saved in your account.
5. Choose what to delete:

### Delete a Specific AI Mode Search

Next to the AI Mode history item:

1. Select **More**.
2. Select **Delete**.

### Delete All AI Mode History

1. Select **More**.
2. Select **Delete all**.

---

## Delete AI Mode Activity from Your Account

Google also notes that AI Mode activity saved in your account can be deleted through Search history:

1. Go to **Search history**: https://myactivity.google.com/product/search
2. Select **Delete**.

---

## What Happens When You Delete AI Mode History

Deleting AI Mode history does **not always immediately remove all related activity everywhere**.

Key points:

- If you delete searches from AI Mode history, they may still appear in **My Activity** for some time.
- These items are **auto-deleted within 24 hours**.
- You can also manually delete them from **My Activity**.

> “If you delete searches from AI Mode history, they may still appear in the ‘My Activity’ page for some time. These items are auto-deleted within 24 hours.”

### AI Overview to AI Mode interactions

If your search starts with an **AI Overview** and you select **“Dive deeper in AI Mode,”** you may see **2 entries** in Search history:

1. The original query
2. The transition to AI Mode for follow-up questions
