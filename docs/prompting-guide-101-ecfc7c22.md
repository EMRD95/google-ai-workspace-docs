#### Human resources

As an HR professional, you're the backbone of your organisation, and you deal with a large volume of confidential and sensitive information. You shape company culture, find and nurture talent and ensure a positive employee experience. These are no small feats.

This section provides you with simple ways to integrate prompts in your day-to-day tasks.

###### Getting started

First, review the general prompt-writing tips in the [How to write effective prompts](/intl/en_uk/resources/ai/writing-effective-prompts/) section.

Each prompt below is presented with an accompanying scenario to serve as inspiration for how you can collaborate with Gemini for Google Workspace. The prompt iteration example shows how you could write follow-up prompts to build on the initial generated response.
