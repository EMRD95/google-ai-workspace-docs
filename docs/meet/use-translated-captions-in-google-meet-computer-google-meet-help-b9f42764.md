## How to Turn On Translated Captions
> *"If you need subtitles in your meetings, learn how to use captions in Google Meet."*

1. On your computer, go to [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com/).
2. In the meeting, click **More options** (`⋮`) → **Settings** (`⚙`) → **Captions**.
3. Select **Language of the meeting**.
4. Turn on **Translated captions** (`🌐`).
5. Choose the language you want captions translated **into**.

**Tip:** If recording a meeting, select **Record captions** to embed the captions in the recording.

---

## Customize Captions Appearance
1. Join a meeting → **More options** → **Settings** → **Captions**.
2. Under **“Customize your captions,”** adjust:
   - Font
   - Font size
   - Font color
   - Background color
3. Click **Close** (settings are saved automatically).

---

## View Past Translated Captions
While captions are turned on:
- Scroll **up or down** to review captions from earlier in the conversation.
- Translates only the portions of the conversation **where you were in the meeting and had captions enabled**.
