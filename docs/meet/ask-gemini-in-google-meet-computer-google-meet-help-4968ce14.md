# Ask Gemini in Google Meet — Summary

**Source:** Google Meet Help — “Ask Gemini in Google Meet - Computer”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/meet/answer/16024610?hl=en

---

## Feature Availability

### Requirements

- Requires an **eligible Google Workspace subscription**.
- Organizations can get immediate access by joining **Google Workspace with Gemini Alpha**.
- Ask Gemini in Meet is available by default **unless disabled by an organization admin**.

### Supported Languages

Ask Gemini in Meet supports **one language at a time** during a meeting. Multiple languages in the same meeting are **not currently supported**.

Supported languages include:

- English
- French
- German
- Italian
- Japanese
- Korean
- Portuguese
- Spanish

> “This feature supports one language at a time. Multiple languages spoken in the same meeting aren't currently supported.”

## What Ask Gemini in Google Meet Does

Ask Gemini brings Gemini capabilities directly into active Google Meet meetings. It helps users privately catch up, summarize, and extract useful meeting information.

### You can use Ask Gemini to:

- Summarize ongoing discussions
- Get a recap of what someone said
- List:
  - Key takeaways
  - Decisions
  - Action items
- Catch up on missed discussion if you joined late, **only if “take notes for me” was enabled**

> “Ask Gemini in Meet brings the power of Gemini into your organization’s meetings. It can help track action items and summarize discussions.”

---

## How Ask Gemini Works

Ask Gemini generates responses using:

- Live meeting captions
- Google Workspace resources the user has permission to view, such as:
  - Docs
  - Sheets
  - Slides
  - Gmail
  - Calendar
- Google Search and public websites

### Important Limitations

- Ask Gemini interactions and responses are **private to each user**.
- Caption data is **not stored after the meeting ends**.
- Ask Gemini **does not create a meeting recording**.
- It cannot answer questions about discussion that happened before the user joined unless **“take notes for me”** had already been turned on.
- Ask Gemini is **not available in breakout rooms**.
- Gemini can make mistakes, including about people, so users should review its output.

> “No caption data will be stored after the meeting ends. Ask Gemini in Meet does not create a meeting recording.”

> “Gemini in Workspace can make mistakes, including about people, so users should review its output.”

## Getting Started with Ask Gemini

Ask Gemini starts automatically after **two participants join** a meeting. The meeting host can also turn it on.

### Use Ask Gemini During a Meeting

1. In the meeting, go to the **bottom left**.
2. Click the **Ask Gemini** text box.
3. Ask questions about the meeting by:
   - Typing a question
   - Selecting suggested questions
   - Clicking the Ask Gemini icon for more suggestions

### If Ask Gemini Doesn’t Turn On

- Hover over the **Ask Gemini turned off** icon in the bottom left.
- Click **Start Now**.
- If it still does not turn on, check troubleshooting causes.

## Remove Your Ask Gemini Conversation History

Your Ask Gemini conversation history is automatically lost when:

- You reload your browser
- You close and reopen the meeting
- Your computer goes offline

### Delete Individual Conversations

1. On your computer, in the top left of the Ask Gemini text box, click **More options**.
2. Click **View all**.
3. Hover over a conversation.
4. Click **More** next to the conversation.
5. Tap **Delete**.

## Turning Ask Gemini On or Off

Ask Gemini can be controlled at multiple levels:

### Admin Controls

Admins can configure Ask Gemini in Meet at the:

- Domain level
- Organizational unit level
- Group level

Path in Admin console:

> **Google Workspace > Google Meet > Gemini Settings**

Ask Gemini is **ON by default** unless the admin changes it.

---

### Meeting Host and Co-host Controls

Meeting hosts can turn Ask Gemini **off for all participants**.

They can do this:

- From the Calendar event before the meeting
- During the meeting by:
  - Hovering over the Gemini icon and using the three-dot menu
  - Going to **Host controls > Gemini**

### Turn Ask Gemini On or Off During a Meeting

1. Open **Host controls**.
2. Under **Gemini**, turn **Gemini** on or off.

---

### Turn Ask Gemini On or Off Before a Meeting

1. Go to [calendar.google.com](https://calendar.google.com/).
2. Click the event or meeting.
3. Click **Edit event**.
4. If the event has no meeting attached, click **Add Google Meet video conferencing**.
5. Click **Video call options > Gemini**.
6. To prevent attendees from using Gemini, turn off:

> **Meeting attendees can use Ask Gemini**
