# Use & Manage Connected Apps in Gemini — Summary

**Source:** Google Gemini Apps Help — “Use & manage Connected Apps in Gemini”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13695044  
**Platform focus:** Computer / Gemini web app, with notes for Android, iOS, and other devices.

## Core Idea

Gemini Apps can connect to other apps and services to complete user requests and provide more useful, personalized responses. With permission, Gemini can access information and content from connected apps, such as Gmail, Google Calendar, Spotify, Google Photos, WhatsApp, smart home tools, and more.

> “Gemini Apps can connect to other apps to complete your request and give you more helpful responses. With your permission, Gemini can also help you with your info and content in other apps.”

---

## What You Can Ask Gemini to Do with Connected Apps

Gemini can help across several categories:

### Productivity

- Summarize emails from Gmail.
- Create events in Google Calendar or third-party calendar apps.
- Help with code from GitHub.
- Interact with Google Workspace apps such as Docs, Drive, Keep, and Tasks.

### Media

- Play music from YouTube Music or Spotify.
- Search Google Photos or Samsung Gallery.

### Communication

- Make calls and send messages using default Android apps.
- Send messages through WhatsApp on Android devices.
- Find and manage details about people using Contacts.

### Device Control

- Manage smart home devices through Google Home.
- Use Android Utilities to:
  - Set alarms and timers.
  - Open websites, apps, and settings.
  - Check and control device features.

## Important Account and Availability Notes

### Personal vs. Work or School Accounts

This help page is for users accessing Gemini Apps with a **personal Google Account**.

> **Important for users with a work or school account:** The info below is for users who access Gemini Apps with a personal account. If you’re using a work or school account, learn how to use Connected Apps in Gemini with a work and school Google Account.

### Google Messages Limitation

> **Important:** For now, Gemini can’t use Connected Apps in Gemini in Google Messages.

### Availability Varies

Connected Apps availability depends on:

- Location
- Language
- Device
- Gemini app or platform
- Country
- Specific app support

> **Important:** Availability of Connected Apps vary by location, language, device, and Gemini app.

Apps available only on Android may not appear in Connected Apps settings on iOS or the Gemini web app.

## Getting Started with Connected Apps

To use Connected Apps:

1. **Sign in to Gemini Apps.**
   - You must be signed in to use Connected Apps.

2. **Check your Keep Activity setting.**
   - Connected Apps depend heavily on whether Gemini’s **Keep Activity** setting is on.

3. **Choose which apps Gemini connects to.**
   - Apps can be connected or disconnected anytime in Connected Apps settings.

### Keep Activity Behavior

> “When Keep Activity is off, Connected Apps won’t be available on gemini.google.com, iOS devices, or smart watches.”

On Android devices, if Keep Activity is off, only these apps remain available:

- Utilities
- Phone
- Messages
- WhatsApp

## How to Use Connected Apps on Computer

To use Connected Apps in Gemini web:

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Enter your question or prompt in the text box.
3. To specify an app, type:

```text
@
```

Then select the app.

4. If the selected app is not connected:
   - Gemini may connect it automatically, or
   - Gemini may ask for permission.
5. Click Submit.
6. Follow any on-screen instructions.

> “If an app is available, Gemini automatically uses it.”

## Browsing Available Connected Apps

To view available apps:

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. At the bottom, click:
   - **Settings & help**
   - **Connected Apps**

If “Connected Apps” does not appear:

- Click **Personal Intelligence**
- Then click **Connected Apps**

You can also browse directly:

- [Browse Connected Apps](https://gemini.google.com/apps)

---

## Managing Which Apps Gemini Works With

You can connect or disconnect apps anytime.

### Steps on Computer

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click:
   - **Settings & help**
   - **Connected Apps**

   If not visible:
   - Click **Personal Intelligence**
   - Then **Connected Apps**

3. Find the app you want to manage.
4. Toggle the app:
   - Turn it on to connect.
   - Turn it off to disconnect.
5. Follow any on-screen instructions.

### Data and Privacy Notes

Google points users to the Gemini Apps Privacy Hub for:

- What happens when an app is disconnected.
- How data is handled when using Connected Apps.
