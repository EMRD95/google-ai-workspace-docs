## Main Role-Specific Gemini Use Cases

### Sales

**Guide:** [Use Gemini for sales](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/14190391)

Gemini can help sales teams:

- Speed up writing **email** and **social media posts**
- Prepare for **meetings** and **customer visits**
- Organize **events** and the **customer pipeline**
- Generate **customer one-pagers**

---

### Marketing

**Guide:** [Use Gemini for marketing](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/14196644)

Gemini can help marketing teams:

- Write **blog posts**, **press releases**, and **company announcements**
- Outline and create visuals for **presentations**
- Organize **marketing campaigns**
- Generate **executive summaries**

---

### Human Resources

**Guide:** [Use Gemini for human resources](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/14171678)

Gemini can help HR teams:

- Recruit and hire for a **job opening**
- Onboard **new employees**
- Speed up drafting **employee communications**

---

### Nonprofits

**Guide:** [Use Gemini for nonprofits](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/14571258)

Gemini can help nonprofit organizations:

- Brainstorm **marketing** and **fundraising campaigns**
- Write **grant applications** and **proposals**
- Manage and engage **donors**
