### Summarize video meetings

Using AI in video conferencing, ask Gemini in [Google Meet](https://workspace.google.com/products/meet) to "[take notes for me](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/14754931)", allowing you to give your full attention to the discussion. Gemini in Meet summarizes your video call and automatically sends you meeting notes in [Gmail](https://workspace.google.com/products/gmail/).

![Gemini in Meet summarizing video meeting notes and delivering them to a Gmail inbox. ](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/9v5eZkZ_wkUqWDz6HCbtwULzIJjN8_HXSHAtSini1XmxNysbujayZ8tEfS2EV6_bMaVMaeCI3_yb_0T2CXU75py6Duk3v7JrCtTMNA=e365-pa-nu-rw-w527)

### Break down language barriers

Gemini in Meet makes video conferencing more accessible for everyone, using AI for translated captions. Gemini in Meet helps remove language barriers, translating speech in real-time with [translated captions](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/10964115) for 60+ languages. In addition, people who are hearing-impaired or who prefer to follow the conversation in a visual format can [turn on live captions](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/10964115).

![Gemini in Meet translating a meeting from Korean to English. ](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/AHB8iuJR0iFZLEl85pt9UOeUDBR9mxuxxAHaSR5cKrvxoHSBe8WTOK8xm7kD6NN5QfeQbDSx3aafs8EuZmslpuCt3uQUTo0mM7-s8w=e365-pa-nu-rw-w527)

### Create ad hoc meeting spaces

[Adaptive audio in Google Meet](https://workspace.google.com/resources/echo-cancellation/#adaptive-audio) automatically detects multiple laptops in a room, synchronizes microphones and speakers, and [prevents awkward echoes and audio feedback](https://workspaceupdates.googleblog.com/2024/05/google-meet-adaptive-audio.html#:~:text=With%20%E2%80%9Cadaptive%20audio%2C%E2%80%9D%20you,for%20dedicated%20conference%20room%20equipment.). This flexibility allows teams to create ad-hoc meeting spaces anywhere with just their laptops.
