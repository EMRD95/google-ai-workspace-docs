# 1. Recruit & Hire for a Job Opening

Gemini can support the full hiring pipeline, including:

- Drafting job descriptions
- Creating job postings
- Writing candidate outreach emails
- Preparing interview questions
- Tracking candidates in Sheets

---

## 1.1 Draft a Job Description

Use Gemini in **Google Docs** to draft a job description from scratch based on a job title. The generated draft can then be reviewed and refined collaboratively with the hiring team.

**Setup:**

- Open a **Google Doc**
- Use **Help me Write** to generate new text

**Prompt:**

> "Write a job description for a \[ _job title_\], including the required skills and experience, as well as a summary of \[ _company name_\] and the position."

**Example job titles:**

- “Senior Product Manager”
- “Regional Sales Representative”

---

## 1.2 Draft a Job Posting

After creating a job description, Gemini can generate multiple job post options and hashtags tailored to a specific target audience.

**Setup:**

- In a **Google Doc**, select the job description
- Use **Help me Write** to ask Gemini about the selected text

**Prompt:**

> "Using this job description, provide 2 options for a job post directed at \[ _target audience_\]. Include 3 hashtags for each option."

**Example target audiences:**

- “mid-level manager”
- “someone with international experience”

**Recommended use:** Refine the generated posting and publish it to a job listing site.

---

## 1.3 Send an Outreach Email to a Candidate

Gemini can draft personalized recruiting emails in **Gmail**, starting from a job title and optionally referencing a candidate’s project or achievement.

**Setup:**

- Open a **Gmail compose window**
- Use **Help me Write** to draft a new email

**Prompt:**

> "Write an email to a job candidate about a job opening for \[ _insert job title_\]. Mention something about \[ _specific project or achievement_\]."

**Personalization source:**

> To find a _specific project or achievement_ for that candidate, refer to their resume or public job profile.

---

## 1.4 Prepare Interview Questions

Gemini can create interview questions tailored to a specific role and audience, helping interviewers avoid starting from scratch.

**Setup:**

- Open a **Google Doc**
- Use **Help me Write** to create new text

**Prompt:**

> "Create a list of interview questions for the job role \[ _job role_\]. Tailor the questions for a \[ _audience_\]."

**Example audiences:**

- “VP”
- “CFO”
- “individual contributor”
- “manager”

---

## 1.5 Track the Interview Pipeline

Gemini in **Google Sheets** can generate a table to track, monitor, and report on candidate interview status in one central spreadsheet.

**Setup:**

- Open a blank **Google Sheet**
- Use **Ask Gemini** to build a new table

**Prompt:**

> "Create a table for tracking interview candidates for a job position we're trying to fill."
