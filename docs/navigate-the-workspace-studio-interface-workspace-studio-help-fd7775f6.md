# Navigate the Workspace Studio interface

Explore the pages you can use to create and manage flows.

## Explore the Discover page

On your computer, go to [studio.workspace.google.com](https://studio.workspace.google.com/). This is the home page where you start. From here, you can:

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/Rbm4KgVi9LqoOkSXxCoimg6x2gtDEYLEtrTvb_MzpS-fwbAcRDCwYveKdibCYOeB6oc=w895)

- **Create a flow with AI:** Describe the automation you want in plain language.
- **Use flow templates:** Browse pre-built flows by app or use case. Template recommendations adjust based on the available features of your account type.
- **Create from scratch:** Start with a blank canvas to build a custom flow.
- **Discover:** Click the icon to return to the Discover page at any time.
- **Open your flows:** Get a list of all the flows you've created.

## Use the flow editor

Once you start your creation of a flow, you arrive at the editor. You can use it to:

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/z01KC3EuTEr2Y9mkdjQBWXCaJK6fgBPQi96UoEduSc2u2rETr2ZlxMsw9NTufTNY3A=w895)

- **Go back:** Return to your list of flows.
- **Rename your flow:** Click the name to edit it.
- **Check activity:** Get a history of your flow runs.
- **Share:**Get a link to share your flow with others.

  - To share a flow, you need a work or school account. If you have a personal account, you can’t share flows.
- **Copy or delete:** Make a copy or permanently delete the flow.
- **Add a variable:** Use data from a previous step.
- **Manage a step:** Delete or change the order of a step.
- **Add a step:** Add another step to your flow.
- **Test run and turn it on:** Test and activate the flow so it can run.

## Review your flow's activity

After you create a flow, you can review its activity on the activity page. To go to the activity page, click **My flows**![and then](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/3_l97rr0GvhSP2XV5OoCkV2ZDTIisAOczrSdzNCBxhIKWrjXjHucxNwocghoUa39gw=h36) **Activity**. From here, you can:

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/NFlmmfwi38_WjwWuQqHl7ywWrx-ZZz128tOY70ZCxpLohVOardkXaQHd6Zh1mRPb_kY=w895)

- **Filter runs:** Find only flows that are completed, in progress, or have errors.
- **Refresh the list:** Get the most up-to-date activity.
- **Collapse details:** Hide the individual steps of a run.
- **Get the start time:** Know when the flow starts.
- **Open the editor:** Make changes to the flow.
- **Expand details:** Show all the steps of a specific run.
