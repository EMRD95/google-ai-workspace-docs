# Collaborate with Gemini in Google Slides — Summary

**Source:** Google Docs Editors Help  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/docs/answer/14207419?hl=en

---

## Overview

Gemini in Google Slides lets users collaborate with AI directly inside Slides to create, edit, summarize, and enhance presentations.

With Gemini in Google Slides, you can:

- Generate and edit new slides.
- Generate images.
- Use, implement, or reference the style of a current presentation.
- Reference your Drive files or Gmail emails as you write or create a new slide.
- Summarize a presentation.
- Write or rewrite content.

---

## Feature Availability

Gemini in Slides is not universally available to all users.

**Requirements and availability:**

- Requires an eligible **Google Workspace** or **Google AI plan**.
- Supported languages depend on Google Workspace with Gemini language availability.
- Available in **Google Workspace Experiments**, a trusted tester program for users with personal accounts.
- To check Workspace Experiments access:
  - Open a new presentation in Google Slides.
  - Look for a bottom bar indicating access.

## Getting Started with Gemini in Slides

### Open Gemini

1. On your computer, open a presentation in [Google Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation).
2. At the top right, click **Ask Gemini**.
3. In the side panel, either:
   - Select a suggested prompt, or
   - Write your own prompt.
4. Press **Enter**.

### Use a Suggested Prompt

1. Select from the list of suggestions.
   - **Tip:** Select **More suggestions** to see additional options.
2. Replace the example text in the prompt box with your own information.
3. Press **Enter**.

### Enter Your Own Prompt

1. At the bottom of the side panel, type a prompt.
2. Press **Enter**.

### Clear Gemini History

To remove Gemini history:

1. Click **More options**.
2. Select **Clear history**.

**Important tip:**

> To avoid losing your history, insert a suggestion into your presentation.

You lose conversation history when:

- You refresh your browser.
- You close and reopen the presentation.
- Your computer goes offline.

## Generate Images Based on Slide Content

Gemini can suggest or create images for your slides.

### Steps

1. Open a presentation in Google Slides.
2. Click **Ask Gemini**.
3. In the Gemini side panel, choose one of the following:
   - Enter:

     > “Generate an image”

     or

     > “Suggest images for this slide.”

   - Select a suggested prompt based on your slide content.
   - Write your own image prompt, such as:

     > “Generate an image of a dog with glasses.”

4. Press **Enter**.
5. Hover over an image and click **Insert** to add it to the slide.

### Additional Options

You can also:

- Click **Edit Prompt** to revise the image prompt.
- Click **Show more** to generate more image options.
- Send feedback with **Good suggestion** or **Bad suggestion**.

## Generate a Slide

Gemini can create a new slide from scratch or reuse the visual style of an existing presentation.

### Steps

1. Open a presentation in Google Slides.
2. Click **Ask Gemini**.
3. Enter a prompt describing the slide you want to create.

### Optional: Match Another Presentation’s Style

To use the visual style of another presentation:

1. Click **Match a presentation style**.
2. Select **Add from Drive**.
3. Choose a presentation.
4. Click **Add**.

To avoid using previous conversation context:

1. Click **More options**.
