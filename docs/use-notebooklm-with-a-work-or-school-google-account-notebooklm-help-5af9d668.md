# Use NotebookLM with a Work or School Google Account — Summary

**Source:** [NotebookLM Help](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16337734?hl=en)

## Overview

Most users with a **work or school Google Account** can access NotebookLM. However, **features, usage limits, security/privacy protections, and data handling depend on the organization’s Google Workspace license**.

> For Workspace and Workspace for Education users, NotebookLM is now a **core service** — similar to Gmail, Google Calendar, and Docs.

---

## How to Upgrade NotebookLM Access

To get **higher usage limits** and **additional features** with a work or school account:

- Your organization must upgrade to a **Google Workspace plan that includes higher NotebookLM limits**.
- Users should contact their **Google Workspace administrator** to request an upgraded license.
- Google Workspace admins can learn how to upgrade their organization’s license via Google’s admin help documentation.

## NotebookLM Access Levels by Workspace Edition

NotebookLM availability and limits vary by edition.

### Standard Access to Features

NotebookLM is available as an **additional service** with:

- Business Base
- Essentials Starter

- Android Enterprise

NotebookLM is available as a **core service** with:

- Frontline Standard
- Frontline Plus
- Nonprofits

### More Access with Enterprise-Grade Security & Privacy

NotebookLM is available as a **core service** with:

- Education Plus

### Higher Level Access with Enterprise-Grade Security & Privacy

NotebookLM is available as a **core service** with:

- Business Plus
- Business Standard
- Enterprise Plus

NotebookLM is available as an **add-on** with:

NotebookLM is available as an **add-on** with:

## Data Protections

### Standard Access

> Your data is protected, and is not used to train NotebookLM unless you provide feedback.

If users submit feedback:

> When you share feedback to help us improve, we may review the full context of that interaction, including your queries, uploads, and the model’s responses.

Google may also process data:

> To ensure safety and reliability, we may process your data to prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues.

### Enterprise-Grade Security & Privacy

For editions with enterprise-grade protections:

> Your uploaded files, chats and model outputs won’t be reviewed by human reviewers or used to improve generative AI models.

Additional privacy references:

- **Workspace:** Google Cloud Privacy Policy describes how Google Cloud handles NotebookLM data.
- **Education:** Google Workspace for Education Privacy Notice and Privacy Center describe how Google Cloud handles NotebookLM data.
