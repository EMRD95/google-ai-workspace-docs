# Plan your video with AI in Google Vids – Summary

**Source:** [Google Docs Editors Help](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/15067819)

- Requires an **eligible Google Workspace subscription** with Gemini features.  
  → [Learn about Gemini features and plans](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13952129)  
  → [Supported languages for Google Workspace with Gemini](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/14925782)

  → [Storage, object & version history limits](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/16286470)

## “Help me create” – Overview

Gemini in Google Vids creates a **first draft video** from your prompt and attached documents.  
When you submit, Vids generates:

- Suggested outline with scenes populated by generated text
- Media from stock and/or files included in the prompt (images, videos, music)
- A script and a voiceover
- Video placeholders for your own recordings

---

## How to Create a Draft Video

1. **Open** [Google Vids](https://docs.google.com/videos/create?usp=hc) and select:
   - **Landscape** → **Storyboard** aspect ratio.
2. **Enter a prompt** or click a suggested one.
   - **Attach files:** Type `@` and select a Drive file (PDFs, Slides).  
     *When you add a Google Slides file:*
     - Visuals (images, GIFs) are analysed and reused.
     - Specific slides may be imported (including shapes and GIFs).
   - **Get new prompts:** Click the **Shuffle** icon below the text prompts.
3. Click **Next**.
4. **Edit the generated outline** (tiles for each scene):
   - **Edit scene prompt:** Click the title text of a tile.
   - **Delete a tile:** Click the Delete (trash) icon.
   - **Add a new tile below:** Click the Add (plus) icon.
   - **Rewrite prompt:** Enter a new prompt and click **Try again** for a fresh outline.
5. **Choose a design:**
   - Hover over a design to preview.
   - Click to select it.
   - Click **Create the draft video**.
6. After creation, continue editing or customising.

| Strategy            | Best practice                                                                 |
|---------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

## Editing the AI-Generated Video

### Scripts & Voiceovers

- Click **Voiceover** to open the side panel and view/edit the generated script.  
- Each scene gets a script and voiceover. To change them:
  1. Delete the voiceover audio track for that scene.
  2. Add a new voiceover **manually** or **with AI** (uses the updated Voiceover panel content).  
     → [How to create scripts and voiceovers](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/15070345)

### Media Placeholders

- **Large placeholders** show an “Add your media” tag.  
- Click the **Select a video** icon to open a drop-down with options:
  - Upload
  - Generate an image
  - Generate a video
  - My media
  - Stock media
- Use **Recording** ![](icon) to add from the recording studio.
- To keep the original media, click **Keep video** ![](icon).
