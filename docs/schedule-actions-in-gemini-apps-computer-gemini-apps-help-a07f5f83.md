# Schedule Actions in Gemini Apps — Summary

**Source:** [Google Gemini Apps Help — Schedule actions in Gemini Apps](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16316416)  
**Platform covered:** Computer / web app

## Overview

Gemini Apps can schedule recurring actions as part of daily, weekly, or monthly routines. Scheduled actions let Gemini generate content automatically at a chosen time, such as news digests, creative inspiration, skill-learning suggestions, calendar summaries, weather forecasts, and more.

Gemini prepares scheduled content in the background so it is ready by the delivery time.

---

## Key Excerpts

> “You can make Gemini a part of your daily, weekly, and monthly routines by scheduling recurring actions.”

> “You can set a delivery time that works for your schedule. Gemini will prepare your content in the background so it’s ready by the scheduled time.”

> **Important:** You can have up to 10 active scheduled actions at a time.

> “Gemini will prepare your content within the hour leading up to your delivery time to help ensure you get the latest information.”

> “Responses for scheduled actions are prepared in advance to guarantee timely delivery. As a result, rapidly changing data, like stock prices, won’t be the latest when you receive them.”

> “Scheduled actions work best for daily summaries and wrap-ups.”

## Requirements

To schedule actions in Gemini Apps, you must meet **both** of the following requirements:

### 1. Sign in with an eligible account

You need one of these account types:

- A **personal Google Account** with a **Google AI Pro or Ultra subscription**
- A **work or school Google Account** with access to a **qualifying edition of Google Workspace**

Relevant links from the article:

- [Learn how to upgrade](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/14517446)
- [Qualifying Google Workspace editions](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/14620100)
- [Learn how to sign in to Gemini Apps](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13278668)

### 2. Keep Activity must be on

- **Keep Activity** must be enabled.
- Scheduled actions are **not available** when Keep Activity is off.

Link:

- [Keep Activity](https://myactivity.google.com/product/gemini?utm_source=help)

## How to Schedule an Action

### Steps

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. In the text box at the bottom, enter your prompt.
   - Include details about **when** and **how often** Gemini should run the action.
3. Click **Submit**.
4. Gemini replies with a summary of the scheduled action.

### Prompting tip

When creating a scheduled action, explicitly state the schedule and instructions, such as:

- Daily at a specific time
- Weekly on a certain day
- Monthly on a recurring date
- What information Gemini should include
- Whether it should use connected apps like Gmail, Calendar, or Docs

---

## Active Scheduled Action Limit

You can have:

- **Up to 10 active scheduled actions at a time**

Paused actions may remain saved, but only active ones count as running scheduled actions.

---

## Timing and Data Freshness

Gemini prepares scheduled content **within the hour before the delivery time**.

Important limitation:

- Scheduled actions are prepared in advance to ensure they arrive on time.
- Because of this, rapidly changing data may not be fully current when delivered.

Examples of data that may not be latest:

- Stock prices
- Cryptocurrency prices
- Other fast-changing market or live data

Best use cases:

- Daily summaries
- Daily wrap-ups
- News digests
- Calendar or email summaries
- Recurring learning prompts
- Weather and planning assistance

## Editing a Scheduled Action

You can update a scheduled action in two ways:

### Option 1: Ask Gemini in the scheduled action chat

In the chat associated with the scheduled action, ask Gemini to change the schedule or instructions. Changes are saved for future runs.

### Option 2: Use Gemini settings

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Settings & help**.
3. Select **Scheduled actions**.
4. Find the scheduled action.
5. Click **More** → **Edit**.
6. Update the scheduled action.
7. Click **Save**.

---

## Deleting a Scheduled Action

To delete a scheduled action:

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Settings & help**.
3. Select **Scheduled actions**.
4. Find the scheduled action.
5. Click **More** → **Delete**.

---

## Pausing or Resuming a Scheduled Action

You can pause an action without deleting it.

### What happens when paused

If an action is paused:

- Gemini will not run the prompt.
- The scheduled action remains saved.
- You can resume it later.

### Steps to pause or resume

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Settings & help**.
3. Select **Scheduled actions**.
4. Find the scheduled action.
5. Toggle it:
   - Turn it **off** to pause.
   - Turn it **on** to resume.
