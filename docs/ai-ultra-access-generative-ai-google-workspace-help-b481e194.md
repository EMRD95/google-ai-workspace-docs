# AI Ultra Access — Google Workspace Help Summary

**Source:** [Google Workspace Admin Help](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/workspace-with-gemini/ai-ultra-access)  
**Last updated:** 2026-06-11 UTC  
**Context:** The AI Ultra Access add‑on is being **discontinued** and replaced with AI Expanded Access.

---

## Key Changes & Transition

### What’s happening
Google is removing the AI Ultra Access add‑on for Workspace. The table below summarises what will happen to your licences.

| Plan type | Flexible plan | Annual/fixed‑term plan | Alternative / recommendation |
|-----------|---------------|------------------------|-------------------------------|
| **Workspace Business, Enterprise, G Suite Legacy** | Automatically transitioned to **AI Expanded Access** on **July 7, 2026** | Automatically transitioned to **AI Expanded Access** at the **next subscription renewal after July 7, 2026** | Consider [Gemini Enterprise](https://cloud.google.com/gemini-enterprise) for Antigravity, Gemini Code Assist, Gemini CLI |
| **Workspace for Education** | Subscriptions **cancelled** on July 7, 2026 | Subscriptions **cancelled** at the next renewal after July 7, 2026 | Consider [Google AI Pro for Education](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/workspace-with-gemini/ai-pro-for-education) |
| **Workspace purchased with @gmail.com** | Subscriptions **cancelled** on July 7, 2026 | Subscriptions **cancelled** at the next renewal after July 7, 2026 | Consider Google AI Ultra for [personal accounts](https://one.google.com/intl/en/about/google-ai-plans/) |

**Critical note for offline agreements:**  
No changes will be made to existing contracts. Work with your Account Manager before renewal, or the add‑on will be cancelled at the end of the current term.

### Products you will lose
After transition, Workspace Business, Enterprise, Education, and legacy customers will no longer have access to:
- **Google Antigravity**, **Gemini CLI**, **Gemini Code Assist** → use [Gemini Enterprise](https://cloud.google.com/gemini-enterprise) to keep them.
- **Google Flow** → video generation remains available in the [Gemini app](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16126339) and [Google Vids](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/16143507).

---

## Action plan for admins
- **Do nothing** – We’ll move licenses to AI Expanded Access automatically.
- **Transition early** – Move users to AI Expanded Access and cancel AI Ultra Access before:
  - 7 July 2026 (flexible plan)
  - Your next renewal (annual plan)
- **Cancel now** – [Cancel the add‑on](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/workspace-with-gemini/cancel-a-gemini-add-on-subscription) at any time; final bill is prorated.

## Frequently Asked Questions (Highlights)

- **Credit overages** – Settings remain unchanged; admins can still adjust overage for AI Ultra Access users.
- **Audit & usage logs** – Remain available for **180 days** after licence removal.
- **Cancelling an annual/fixed‑term plan** – Possible without penalty if purchased via Admin console; prepaid balances are refunded (excluding past months). Contact your Google Account Manager or partner if you bought through them.
- **Google AI Ultra subscriptions unaffected** – These changes apply only to the Workspace add‑on.
- **AI Expanded Access update** – Starting July 7, 2026, AI Expanded Access no longer includes Google Flow.
- **Data export options** –  
  - Gemini app, NotebookLM, Workspace data: export with [data export tool](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/migrate/export-all-your-organizations-data).  
  - Google Flow data: email `ai-test-kitchen-data-export@google.com`.  
  - Gemini CLI: use `share` sub‑command.  
  - Antigravity: stored locally in `~/.gemini/antigravity/`.

## What AI Ultra Access includes (for reference)

> **Important:** The information below applies to customers who purchased AI Ultra Access **before May 5, 2026**.

### Core benefits
- **Advanced image generation** – More images using **Nano Banana Pro** in Slides, NotebookLM, Gemini app.
- **Video generation** – Scale video production with **Veo 3.1** in Google Vids (including AI avatars) and the Gemini app.
- **Gemini app** – Highest access to deep reasoning with models like **Gemini 3 Pro** (Deep Think).
- **NotebookLM** – Highest limits for Audio Overviews, sources, notebooks, Mind Maps, infographics, custom styles, and sharing analytics.
- **Workspace Studio*** – Execute more automations; limits enforced from **July 1, 2026**.
- **Speech translation**** – Higher limits for real‑time translation in Meet; currently promotional, updates will be communicated before limits change.
- **Access to most Gemini features in Workspace** (see full list below).
