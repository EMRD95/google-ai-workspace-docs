# Use Enhanced Smart Fill with Gemini in Google Sheets — Summary

**Source:** Google Docs Editors Help  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/docs/answer/14226603  
**Feature availability:** Desktop Google Sheets only

---

## Key Excerpts

> **Important:** This feature requires an eligible Google Workspace or Google AI plan.

> This feature is also available through our early access testing program, **Google Workspace Experiments**.

> On Google Sheets, enhanced Smart Fill suggestions makes manual text processing tasks easier. It automatically detects the relationship between incomplete column pairs. It also uses AI to predict the remaining values, which allows them to be instantly filled.

> **Important:** The outline indicates the range Sheets can predict and you can hover over the icon to see the fill suggestions.

> Enhanced Smart Fill can only detect and support **English values**.

> To trigger enhanced Smart Fill detection, a table must have at least **3 example rows** with **2 distinct column values** to be filled with suggested values.

> Enhanced Smart Fill can only be triggered on **text columns**. It will not run on **numeric and date columns**.

---

## What Enhanced Smart Fill Does

Enhanced Smart Fill with Gemini in Google Sheets uses AI to help automate manual text-processing tasks. It detects relationships between incomplete column pairs and predicts missing values so users can fill them instantly.

It is designed for spreadsheet tasks where Google Sheets can infer a pattern from examples, especially text-based transformations, classifications, or extractions.

## How Enhanced Smart Fill Suggestions Appear

Enhanced Smart Fill suggestions can appear in two ways:

1. **After an edit is made** in the file.
2. **Live while entering data manually**, once Sheets detects that it has a prediction available.

When suggestions appear:

- Google Sheets outlines the predicted fill range.
- Users can hover over the Smart Fill icon to preview fill suggestions.

---

## How to Accept or Reject Suggestions

When enhanced Smart Fill suggestions appear, you can:

1. **Accept the suggestion**
   - Click the suggestion bubble.

2. **Reject the suggestion**
   - Click the cancel icon on the expanded suggestion pill.
   - This rejects the suggestion for the selected range.

## Triggering Enhanced Smart Fill

To trigger Enhanced Smart Fill detection:

- The table must include at least:
  - **3 example rows**
  - **2 distinct column values** to be filled with suggested values
- The columns must contain:
  - **Text data**
- The feature will not trigger on:
  - Numeric columns
  - Date columns

## Giving Feedback on Generated Suggestions

Google notes that Workspace with Gemini is still learning and may not always support a request correctly.

If a suggestion is inaccurate, unsafe, or otherwise problematic, users can submit feedback to help improve Gemini-powered Workspace features and Google’s broader AI efforts.

### Submit Feedback on a Specific Suggestion

1. Click the **three-dot menu** in any suggestion.
2. Click **Send feedback**.

### Submit General Feedback on the Feature

In Google Sheets:

1. Go to **Help**.
2. Select **Help Sheets improve**.

### Report a Legal Issue

To report a legal issue, users can either:

- Create a request through Google Legal Help, or
- Go to **Send feedback** → **Report an issue** or **Suggest an idea** → click **Legal Help** at the bottom of the sidebar.
