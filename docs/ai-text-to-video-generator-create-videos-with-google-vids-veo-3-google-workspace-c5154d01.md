## What Google Vids Offers

Google Vids is positioned as an AI-powered video creation tool for Workspace users. It enables teams to create video content quickly without specialized editing skills.

### Main capabilities

- Generate AI video clips from:
  - **Text prompts**
  - **Images**
- Use **Veo 3** inside Google Vids without leaving the app
- Add or edit:
  - Animations
  - Voiceovers
  - Music
  - Storyboards
  - Scripts
  - Scenes
- Produce high-definition video with:
  - Realistic motion
  - Sound
  - Native audio generation
  - Multiple visual styles
- Use cinematic prompt language such as:
  - `"timelapse"`
  - `"aerial shot"`

## Veo 3 Integration

Google Vids includes access to **Veo 3**, described as Google’s latest state-of-the-art video generation model.

### Veo 3 benefits

- Generates high-quality clips directly in Google Vids
- Supports both **text-to-video** and **image-to-video**
- Understands nuanced and cinematic prompts
- Offers greater creative control over visual style, motion, and composition
- Supports native audio generation for more dynamic outputs
