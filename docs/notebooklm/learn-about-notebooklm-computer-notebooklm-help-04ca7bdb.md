## Overview

**NotebookLM** is an **AI-powered research assistant** designed to help users refine, organize, and transform ideas using uploaded or discovered source materials.

### Key excerpt

> NotebookLM is an AI-powered research assistant designed to help you refine and organize your ideas.

With NotebookLM, users can:

- Upload or discover sources, including:
  - PDFs
  - Websites
  - YouTube videos
  - Audio files
  - Google Docs
  - Google Slides
- Use **Gemini’s advanced reasoning and interaction capabilities** across:
  - Text
  - Graphs
  - Images
  - Audio
  - Multiple languages
- Chat with a notebook to receive **grounded answers based on uploaded sources**
- Get **clear inline citations** for accuracy, transparency, and trust
- Transform sources into formats such as:
  - Study guides
  - Briefings
  - Audio overviews
  - Mind maps
  - Other approachable summaries and outputs

## Availability

NotebookLM is available through a browser for eligible users in supported countries and territories.

### Languages

- NotebookLM currently supports **80+ languages**.

### User eligibility

NotebookLM is available to users who:

- Are above the **age of consent**, or the applicable minimum age in their country
- Are in a **supported country or territory**
- Have verified their age in their Google Account when required

### Important age-related note

> NotebookLM has stricter content policies that help prevent potentially inappropriate or harmful responses for users under 18. Certain features may only be available to users over 18.

### Google Workspace and Education availability

- Most users with a **business or school Google Account** have access to NotebookLM.
- For **Workspace for Education** users, NotebookLM is available to users of **all ages**.
- If using a school or work-managed account, the organization’s admin may need to enable NotebookLM access.

## Why NotebookLM May Be Unable to Answer Questions

NotebookLM may fail to answer or may produce limited answers for several reasons.

### 1. Safety flags

Source content may trigger a safety filter.

This can happen with sensitive topics such as:

- Violence
- Sexuality
- Obscenity

This may occur even when the material is presented in a historical or academic context.

### 2. Unclear phrasing

When a notebook contains many sources, NotebookLM first retrieves the most relevant information based on the user’s question, then generates an answer from that information.

If the question is vague, the system may not retrieve the right source material.

**Actionable guidance:**

- Rephrase the question
- Make the question clearer
- Ask more specific questions

### 3. Information is not in the sources

NotebookLM is designed to answer based on the information provided in uploaded or available sources.

If the answer is not present in the sources, NotebookLM may not be able to answer.

### Key excerpt

> NotebookLM is designed to answer questions based on the information provided in your uploaded sources.
