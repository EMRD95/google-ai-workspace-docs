# Explore Inspiration Tab on YouTube — Summary

**Source:** YouTube Help — “Explore Inspiration tab on YouTube”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/15575509

## Overview

The **Inspiration tab** in YouTube Studio helps creators brainstorm and generate content suggestions using AI. It can assist with:

- **Video ideas**
- **Titles**
- **Thumbnails**
- **Hooks**
- **Outlines**
- **Related video/topic research**

It uses channel data and audience-related signals to suggest ideas and help creators evaluate whether a topic may be worth pursuing.

## Availability & Important Notes

- Available **globally**, but **desktop only**.
- Suggestions are currently **English-only**.
- If the **Inspiration** tab does not appear on the **Content** page, the creator does not have access to the feature.
- Creators who saved ideas in previous versions of Inspiration can export them using **Google Takeout**.
- AI outputs may be:
  - Inaccurate
  - Inappropriate
  - Variable in quality
- AI-generated ideas **do not represent YouTube’s views**.
- Users should avoid entering **personal information**.
- Outputs should not be relied on for **professional advice**.

## How to Get Started

1. Sign in to [YouTube Studio](https://studio.youtube.com/).
2. In the left menu, select **Content**.
3. From the top menu, click the **Inspiration** tab.
4. Begin brainstorming topics for your next video.

---

## Brainstorming Features

When opening the Inspiration tab:

- You will see **nine suggested ideas** based on your channel’s data.
- You can refine ideas or brainstorm new ones by:
  - Entering prompts in the input box
  - Clicking suggested topic chips
  - Selecting previously published videos from the **+ menu**
- To explore variations:
  - Click **More like this** on an idea card.
  - Click **Show more** at the bottom of the page to load **9 similar but new ideas**.
- You can navigate between iterations using **back and forward arrows**.
  - Ideas explored during the session are retained until the page is reloaded.
- To save an idea for later, click the **bookmark** icon on the idea card.

## Image Preferences

Creators can adjust how AI-generated images appear:

- Open settings via the **setting icon**.
- Choose an image style:
  - **Digital painting**
  - **Sketch**
- Or disable images entirely if preferred.

Signals may include:

- **Aggregated comment insights**
- **Prediction signals**

### Channel Alignment

The tool helps creators understand:

- How an idea fits their existing channel content
- What unique angle they can bring
- How the idea connects to previous videos

### Related Interest

Creators can evaluate whether an idea is worth pursuing based on recent topic interest, including:

- Related videos
- Creator names
- Views
