# Let Gemini in Google Calendar find times to meet

**Important:** This feature requires an eligible Google Workspace plan. [Learn about Gemini features and plans](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13952129).

To save you time, you can use Gemini in Google Calendar to search for everyone's availability and suggest times to meet that work best for everyone.

## Get suggested times when you create an event

1. On your computer, open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/).
2. At the top left, click **Create**![and then](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/3_l97rr0GvhSP2XV5OoCkV2ZDTIisAOczrSdzNCBxhIKWrjXjHucxNwocghoUa39gw=h36) **Event**.
3. Add guests to your event.
4. Click **Suggested times**.
5. Select a suggested time.
   - If you want to see other times, click **More suggestions**.

     - Or, you can directly edit on the grid.
     - If you edit on the grid, you can click Filter and view ![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/2pLQFgB9rT4D5TjsXS5cO4dCviGcLT1dRbfi). This lets you toggle rooms and filter guests by required or optional status.

**Tip:** To collapse or expand the suggestions, on the left, click **Suggested times**.

## Get suggested times when you reschedule an event

1. On your computer, open [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/).
2. Click the event you want to reschedule.
3. Click Edit event ![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/YL1HyKGQPj5qXFcApz66xPy140gy7cwsF4iH).
4. Click **Suggested times**.

**Tip:** If you're the event organizer and several guests decline, a banner might appear at the top of the event with alternative schedule suggestions.

- To accept the suggestion, click **Reschedule to \[date and time\]**.
- To manually find a different time, click **More times**.

## Learn why you don't get suggested times

You can't get suggested times if:

- You use the Calendar app for Android, iPhone, or iPad.
- The event is longer than 8 hours.
- The meeting has too many attendees.
- The date range you are viewing is in the past.
