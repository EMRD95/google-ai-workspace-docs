# Get started with Google Workspace with Gemini — Business/Enterprise

**Source:** [Google Calendar Help](https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/13952129)  
**Topic:** AI‑assisted features in Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Chat, Docs, Drive, Forms, Meet, Sheets, Slides, Vids, Workspace Studio) available through Workspace editions or personal Google AI plans.

---

## Requirements

- **Age:** 18 or older.
- **Eligible Account:**
  - **Work/school account:** requires an eligible Google Workspace plan.
  - **Personal account:** access through a Google AI plan:
    - [Google AI Plus](https://support.google.com/googleone/answer/16882689)
    - [Google AI Pro](https://support.google.com/googleone/answer/14534406)
    - [Google AI Ultra](https://support.google.com/googleone/answer/16286513)

- **Additional notes:**
  - Some features may not be available in your language; you can switch to a supported language. [Supported languages](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/14925782).
  - **Personal accounts in the US** get these Gmail features at no charge:
    - Help me write
    - AI Overviews for email threads
    - Suggested Replies
