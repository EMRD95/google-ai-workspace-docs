# Use Gemini While You Navigate — Android (Google Maps Help)

**Source:** Google Maps Help — “Use Gemini while you navigate”  
**Platform covered:** Android phones/tablets

## Overview

Gemini can be used inside Google Maps during navigation to help you get information and complete tasks while **driving, walking, or riding a two-wheeler**. You can ask questions or give commands without leaving navigation, helping you stay focused on the road.

## Important Notes

- **Availability:** Gemini is currently available in many countries and languages.
  - Related help: *Gemini mobile app availability*
- **Language and voice settings:** Google Maps uses the **Gemini language and voice preferences** for the signed-in Google account.
  - Related help: *Change Gemini’s language*
- **Location-based answers:** Gemini in Google Maps provides answers based on your **precise location preferences**.
  - Related help: *Manage precise location preferences*

---

## How to Talk to Gemini in Google Maps on Android

1. Open the **Google Maps app** on your Android phone or tablet.
   - If you haven’t used Gemini before, first get started with the **Gemini mobile app**.

2. While in **navigation mode**, activate Gemini by saying:

   > “Hey Google”

   Or tap the **Gemini** icon in Maps.

3. Ask Gemini for help with anything supported in Maps or connected apps.

### If “Hey Google” Doesn’t Work

Check that **“Hey Google” and Voice Match**, powered by Gemini, are turned on.

---

## What You Can Ask Gemini to Do in Maps

## 1. Get Help with Google Maps

### Basic Navigation Controls

Use Gemini to control navigation with commands such as:

> "Navigate home"  
> "Stop navigating"  
> "Add stop"  
> "Mute guidance"  
> "Avoid tolls"  
> "Show alternative routes"

### Basic Route Answers

Ask for information about your current route, including:

> "What's my next turn?"  
> "When will I arrive?"  
> “What’s the weather like at my destination?”  
> "What's my next stop?"

### Report Route Issues

Submit reports about conditions on your route, such as:

> "There’s traffic on the road."  
> "There’s construction up ahead."  
> "There's a car breakdown that’s causing congestion."

---

## 2. Search and Get Information About Places

Gemini can help you search for places while navigating.

### Search Capabilities

You can:

- Search along your route
- Find places near you
- Find places near your destination
- Refine search results
- Add stops to your route

Example:

> “Find me a cafe near my destination that serves tiramisu.”

### Place Information

Gemini can answer questions about destinations, search results, specific places, and landmarks.

Examples:

> “What's the most popular dish on the menu?”  
> "When does {Place Name} close?"

---

## 3. Call or Text Someone

You can ask Gemini to place calls or send messages:

> "Call Mom."  
> "Send a text to Larry”  
> "Send an SMS to Sergey.”

---

## 4. Play Music

Gemini can control music playback with commands like:

> "Play some jazz"  
> “Stop playing music.”

Related help: *Learn how to listen to music*

---

## 5. Use Other Apps with Gemini

**Important:** To use other apps with Gemini in Google Maps, the apps must be connected to the **same account signed into Google Maps**.

Related help: *Use and manage connected apps in Gemini*

Examples of supported requests:

> "Summarize my latest emails."  
> "When’s my next meeting?"  
> "Play music with Spotify."
