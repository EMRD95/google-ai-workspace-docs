# Ask Questions in Google Maps — Summary

**Source:** [Google Maps Help: Ask questions in Google Maps](https://support.google.com/maps/answer/16842041)  
**Platform covered:** **Computer**  
**Feature:** Ask Maps / Gemini in Google Maps

---

## Overview

Google Maps includes an AI-powered feature, **Ask Maps**, powered by **Gemini**, that lets users ask questions and get recommendations or details about destinations directly inside Google Maps.

> “You can get recommendations and answers from Gemini in Google Maps. This AI-powered tool helps you find the best places to go and provides specific details about your destination.”

---

## Important Requirement: WebGL

Ask Maps requires **WebGL** to work.

> **Important:** WebGL is required for Ask Maps to function. Some browsers block the WebGL technology used to make 3D images.

Useful links from the article:

- [Learn more about which browsers might cause problems](https://www.khronos.org/webgl/wiki/BlacklistsAndWhitelists)
- [Check if your web browser can use WebGL](https://get.webgl.org/)

## How to Ask Questions in Google Maps

1. On your computer, open [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps).
2. Click **Menu** → **Ask Maps**.
3. In the prompt box, enter a question.
4. Tap **Send**.
5. You can continue asking follow-up questions in the same conversation.

---

## Examples of Questions You Can Ask

The article provides examples of practical, multi-step, and location-based questions:

> “Any fun things to do this weekend within a 15-minute drive?”

> ”I need to pick up my friend from the airport. What's the fastest way to get there? When should I leave if I need to be there by 6:00 PM? Where can I get flowers on the way?”

> ”My phone is about to run out of battery. Where can I buy a charger? Are there any charging stations available at no charge without having to order a coffee?”

These examples show that Ask Maps can help with:

- Local recommendations
- Route planning
- Timing and departure planning
- Finding stops along a route
- Locating specific amenities or services
- Follow-up questions

## Find Past Conversations in Chat History

To access previous Ask Maps conversations:

1. On your computer, open [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps).
2. Click **Menu** → **Ask Maps**.
3. Tap **History** to view past conversations.
4. Tap a past conversation to load it.

---

## Delete Conversations from Chat History

To delete a conversation:

1. On your computer, open [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps).
2. Click **Menu** → **Ask Maps**.
3. Tap **History**.
4. Next to the conversation you want to delete, tap **Delete**.

> **Important:** You can only delete the full conversation, not just a part of it.

---

## Report a Problem with an Ask Maps Response

To report an issue with a response:

1. Under the response, tap **Thumbs-down**.
2. Select a reason for the feedback.
3. In the text box, describe the issue in detail.
4. Tap **Submit**.

For positive feedback or feature suggestions:

> **Tip:** To leave positive feedback or suggest features, tap Thumbs-up and fill out the form.
