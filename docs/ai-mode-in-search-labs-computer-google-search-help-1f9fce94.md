## What AI Mode in Search Labs Is

**AI Mode** is an opt-in Search Labs experiment that lets users try additional generative AI capabilities in Google Search before they are broadly available in AI Mode.

Users can:

- Ask questions using **text or voice**
- Receive **AI-powered responses**
- Ask **follow-up questions**
- Continue prior interactions through **AI Mode history**
- Provide feedback to help Google improve the experiment

---

## Requirements

To use the **AI Mode** experiment in Search Labs, you need:

- To be **13 or older**
- A **personal Google Account** that you manage yourself
  - Search Labs and experiments are **not currently available** to:
    - Google Workspace accounts
    - Google Workspace for Education accounts
- Availability in a supported language
- For best experience:
  - Enable **Search history**
  - Enable **personalized recommendations**

Without history/personalization enabled, you can still access AI Mode, but:

- You **cannot pick up where you left off** with previous searches

## Search Personalization

AI Mode in Labs can personalize responses for:

- Users **18+**
- Located in the **United States**
- With **Web & App Activity** enabled
- With **Search personalization** enabled

### Manage Search Personalization

1. Go to the [Search personalization setting](https://www.google.com/search-personalization/?utm_source=google-account&hl=en&utm_medium=web&continue=https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy).
2. Turn **Personalize Search** on or off.

---

## How to Get Started with AI Mode in Search Labs

1. On your computer, open any browser.
2. Make sure you’re signed in to your Google Account.
3. Turn **Incognito mode off**.
4. Enter a question in the Search bar on [Google](https://google.com/).
5. Click or tap **AI Mode** below the Search bar.
   - You can also go directly to [google.com/ai](https://google.com/ai).
6. Ask questions using text or voice.
7. Ask follow-up questions on the same topic.
8. Use **AI Mode history** to pick up where you left off.

---

## Agentic Capabilities for Google AI Pro / AI Ultra Subscribers

If you are a **Google AI Pro** or **Google AI Ultra** subscriber in the **US**, you may access the **“Agentic capabilities in AI Mode”** experiment.

This can help with tasks such as:

- Finding restaurant reservations
- Finding event tickets
- Booking beauty and wellness appointments

### Example Prompts

> Find me a dinner reservation for \[desired date, time, location, preferences, requirements, group size etc\]

> Find me 2 tickets to see \[artist in city\]. prefer seats with a good view that are still under $150 each

> Find me a good deal on a back massage in \[city\] on Sunday afternoon

---

## Turn AI Mode On or Off in Search Labs

1. On your computer, open any browser.
2. Make sure you’re signed in to your Google Account.
3. Open a new tab.
4. At the top of the page, click **Search Labs**.
5. Click **Manage**.
6. Toggle **AI Mode** on or off.
