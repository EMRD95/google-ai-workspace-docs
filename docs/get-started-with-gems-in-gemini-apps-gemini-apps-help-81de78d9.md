# Get started with Gems in Gemini Apps

![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/zY0QaDiBw6WBTWdpCduEnUxFkrkLwFvpc405)

Welcome to the getting started guide for Gems. Here you'll find tips to customize Gemini Apps responses with Gems.

#### What are Gems?

Gems are customized versions of Gemini that help you tackle repetitive tasks or get deep expertise in new areas. When you chat with a Gem, Gemini will tailor responses to save time on repeat goals and guidelines in your Gemini web app and Gemini mobile app prompts.

#### What you'll need

To create and use Gems with a personal Google Account, you must also be 13 (or [the applicable age in your country](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/1350409)) or over.

**Note**: Some features are not yet available in the Gemini mobile app. Requirements will be listed in each section of the Gems guide.
