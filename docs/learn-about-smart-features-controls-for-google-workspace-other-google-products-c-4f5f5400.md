# Summary: Smart Features & Controls for Google Workspace and Other Google Products

**Source:** Google Workspace Studio Help — “Learn about smart features & controls for Google Workspace & other Google products”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/workspace-studio/answer/15604322

---

## Key Takeaways

Google updated its **smart feature settings for Google Workspace** to give users more granular control over how their Workspace data is used. The previous control has been split into **two distinct settings** for different product experiences, while Gmail/Chat/Meet also retain their own smart feature setting.

> “These updates also give you more choice and control over your data, and do not alter our underlying data handling practices or our robust privacy commitments.”

Smart features can enable experiences such as:

- Using **Gemini in Google Workspace** to reference Drive files from Gmail
- Automatically adding events from Gmail, such as flights, to Calendar
- Compiling loyalty cards and tickets from Gmail into Google Wallet
- Tracking orders using Gmail summary cards

---

## Smart Feature Settings Overview

Each smart feature setting works independently and controls different types of personalization.

| Smart feature setting | Where to find it | What it controls | Example experiences |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Smart features in Gmail, Chat, and Meet** | Gmail | Use of Gmail, Chat, and Meet data to provide smart features within those products | Smart Compose in Gmail; summary cards |
| **Smart features in Google Workspace** | Gmail, Calendar*, Chat*, Drive*, Meet* | Use of Workspace data to provide smart features across Workspace products | Events from Gmail in Calendar; Google Workspace with Gemini |
| **Smart features in other Google products** | Gmail, Calendar*, Chat*, Drive*, Meet* | Use of Workspace data to provide smart features in non-Workspace Google products | Maps reservations; Wallet tickets, passes, loyalty cards |

\* These settings can only be turned on from a web browser.

## 1. Smart Features in Gmail, Chat & Meet

When enabled, Gmail, Chat, and Meet can use your content and activity in those products to personalize your experience within those apps.

### Experiences Enabled

- [Automatic email filtering/categorization](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/3094499), such as:
  - Primary
  - Social
  - Promotions
- [Smart Compose](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/9116836) in Gmail
- [Smart Reply](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/6585) in Gmail
- [Summary cards above emails](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/15195630), such as:
  - Travel
  - Package tracking
  - Other contextual cards

### Important Notes

- This setting controls **app-level smart features** in Gmail, Chat, and Meet.
- It does **not** affect the separate **Smart features in Google Workspace** setting.
- If turned off, the listed Gmail/Chat/Meet experiences become unavailable until the setting is turned back on.
- Some smart features with their own separate controls may still remain available, including:
  - [Conversation summaries in Chat](https://support.google.com/chat/answer/12918975)
  - [Smart Reply in Chat](https://support.google.com/chat/answer/7654374)

## 2. Smart Features in Google Workspace

When enabled, Google Workspace can use your Workspace content and activity to personalize your experience across Workspace apps.

Workspace includes apps such as:

- Calendar
- Gmail
- Chat
- Meet
- Drive
- Other business and school Workspace apps

### Experiences Enabled

- Showing events from Gmail in Calendar, such as:
  - Flight itineraries
  - Invitations
- Personalized search in products like Drive, including:
  - Keyword suggestions
  - File suggestions
  - More relevant results
- Google Workspace with Gemini features, such as asking Gemini to:
  - Summarize content
  - Create drafts
  - Find key information

### Important Notes

- Turning this setting off disables the listed Workspace-wide experiences.
- To use them again, the setting must be turned back on.
- This setting does **not** affect the separate Gmail/Chat/Meet smart features control.

## 3. Smart Features in Other Google Products

When enabled, Google can use your Workspace content and activity to personalize your experience in Google products outside Workspace.

### Experiences Enabled

For **personal accounts only**, examples include:

- [Restaurant reservations and to-go orders in Maps](https://support.google.com/maps/answer/3503748)
- [Suggested tickets, passes, and loyalty cards in Wallet](https://support.google.com/wallet/answer/12060038#zippy=%2Cadd-items-from-gmail)
- [Personal Intelligence in the Gemini app](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16598406)
- [Personal Intelligence in Search services](https://support.google.com/websearch/answer/16859283)
