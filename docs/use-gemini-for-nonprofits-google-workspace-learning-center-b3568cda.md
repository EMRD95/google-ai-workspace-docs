# 1. Brainstorm Marketing & Fundraising Campaigns

Gemini can help nonprofits create marketing materials, fundraising messages, visuals, and event plans.

---

## Draft Social Media & Blog Posts That Drive Empathy

Use Gemini in **Google Docs** to draft compelling marketing content that raises awareness and encourages donations.

**Recommended uses:**

- Social media posts
- Blog posts
- Email newsletters
- Ad copy
- Website content

You can target messaging by audience interests, demographics, or geography, then revise Gemini’s output for tone and accuracy.

**Setup:**  
In a **Google Doc**, use **Help me Write** to write new text.

**Prompt:**

> "Write a blog post that generates empathy about \[ _your cause_\] and encourages donations to \[ _your organization_\]. Target your language at \[ _audience_\]."

**Audience examples:**

- “outdoor enthusiasts”
- “parent”
- “people in your community”

---

## Create Attention-Grabbing Email Campaigns

Use Gemini in **Gmail** to draft donation request emails and subject lines.

**Recommended uses:**

- Donation request emails
- Personalized donor outreach
- Calls to action
- Subject line brainstorming

**Setup:**  
In a **Gmail compose window**, use **Help me Write** to write a new email.

**Prompts:**

> "Write 3 compelling subject lines for an email requesting donations to help \[ _your cause_\]."

> "Write an email requesting a donation from a person who has contributed to your cause in the past. Add a personal touch by mentioning how \[ _something personal about the person_\] is relevant to \[ _your cause_\]. Emphasize how to make the donation."

**Personalization examples:**

- Profession
- Hobby
- Location
- Other public-profile details

---

## Create Visuals That Raise Awareness

Use Gemini in **Google Slides** to generate custom images for nonprofit outreach.

**Recommended uses:**

- Newsletter images
- Website graphics
- Stakeholder presentations
- Educational outreach materials

**Setup:**  
In **Google Slides**, use **Create image** to generate new artwork.

**Prompts:**

> "Create an image for a newsletter that raises awareness about \[ _the problem your nonprofit is trying to solve_\]."

> "Create a conceptual image for a presentation to board members of a nonprofit that shows effective money management. The nonprofit supports \[ _your cause_\]."

---

## Plan a Fundraising Campaign or Event

Use Gemini in **Google Sheets** to create a marketing plan and budget table.

Gemini can generate a table with placeholder values, activities, timelines, and budget categories that your team can later customize.

**Setup:**  
In a blank **Google Sheet**, use **Ask Gemini** to build a new table.

**Prompt:**

> "Create a table outlining a marketing plan and budget for a fundraising campaign for \[ _your cause or event_\]. Include a 2 month timeline. There will be live music and lunch from a local caterer."
