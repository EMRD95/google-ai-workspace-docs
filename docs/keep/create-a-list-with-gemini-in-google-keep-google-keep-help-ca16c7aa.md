# Create a List with Gemini in Google Keep — Summary

**Source:** Google Keep Help  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/keep/answer/15222789?hl=en

---

## Overview

Google Keep lets users create AI-generated lists using Gemini through the **“Help me create a list”** prompt.

### Availability

- Requires a **Google AI plan** to create lists with Gemini in Google Keep.
- The feature is available on **Pixel devices**.
- Usage may be subject to **Gemini usage limits**.
- To continue using Gemini in Google Keep, users may need to sign up for a Google AI plan.

---

## Key Excerpts

> You can use AI to create a new list in Google Keep when you select the "Help me create a list" prompt.

> You can create lists with Gemini in Google Keep with a Google AI plan.

> You can use “Help me create a list” on Pixel devices. Your usage may be subject to Gemini usage limits.

**Tip:**

> For better results, write specific instructions. For example: "Groceries for a week for a vegetarian family of 3."

---

## How to Use Gemini to Create a List

1. Open the **Google Keep app** on your device.
2. Tap **Create**.
3. At the bottom right, tap **Help me create a list**.
4. Enter a prompt.

   Example prompts include:

   - `"Packing list for a camping trip with 2 kids in summer"`
   - `"Classic movies to watch on Halloween"`
   - `"Spring cleaning checklist for a 2-bedroom apartment"`

5. Tap **Create**.
6. Optionally, after the list is generated:
   - Give feedback:
     - Tap **Good suggestion**
     - Or tap **Bad suggestion**
   - Edit the prompt:
     1. Tap the prompt at the top of the window.
     2. Edit your prompt.
     3. Tap **Refresh**.
7. Tap **Insert** to add the generated list to Google Keep.

---

## Prompting Guidance

For best results, Google recommends using **specific instructions** rather than vague prompts.

Example of a more specific prompt:

> "Groceries for a week for a vegetarian family of 3."

Specific prompts help Gemini generate more useful and relevant lists.

## Giving Feedback on a Generated List

Gemini for Google Workspace:

- Constantly learns
- May not support every request at this time

If a generated list is inaccurate, unsafe, or otherwise problematic, users can submit feedback. Google may use this feedback to improve AI-assisted Workspace features and broader AI efforts.

### Submit Feedback on a Suggested List

1. Below the generated list, select:
   - **Good suggestion**
   - Or **Bad suggestion**
2. If selecting **Bad suggestion**, choose the issue found and optionally enter additional feedback.
3. To include the generated output with feedback, check **“Output.”**
4. Select **Submit**.

### Provide General Feature Feedback

1. Open the **Menu**.
2. Select **Help and feedback**.

### Report Legal Issues

To report a legal issue, users should create a request through Google’s legal support page.

## Important Notes About Gemini Suggestions

Google provides several cautions about Gemini-generated content:

- Gemini feature suggestions **do not represent Google’s views** and should not be attributed to Google.
- Users should **not rely on Gemini** for:
  - Medical advice
  - Legal advice
  - Financial advice
  - Other professional advice
