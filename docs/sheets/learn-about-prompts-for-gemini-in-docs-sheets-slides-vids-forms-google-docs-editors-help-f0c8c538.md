# Learn about prompts for Gemini in Docs, Sheets, Slides, Vids & Forms

**Source:** [Google Docs Editors Help](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/15013615)

This guide provides tips and sample prompts for using Gemini in Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, Vids, and Forms to boost productivity.

- [Supported languages for Google Workspace with Gemini](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/14925782).

## Tips for Writing Prompts for Gemini

Four key areas make an effective prompt. You don’t need all four, but combining them yields better results:

| Area | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Persona** | Tell Gemini what role to play for context on behavior and tone. | *I am a customer service representative.* |
| **Task** | Specify what to do using an action verb (summarize, write, create, etc.). | *Help me craft an empathetic email response.* |
| **Context** | Provide relevant background information. Attach files using `@` followed by the file name to include more details. | *The customer ordered a pair of headphones that arrived damaged.* |
| **Format** | Be explicit about the desired structure (bullets, paragraphs, tables, etc.). | *Include a paragraph that acknowledges their frustration and 3 bullet points with potential resolutions.* |

**Combined prompt from the table:**

> I am a customer service representative. Help me craft an empathetic email response. The customer ordered a pair of headphones that arrived damaged. Include a paragraph that acknowledges their frustration and 3 bullet points with potential resolutions.

### Additional Best Practices

- **Use natural language:** Write as you speak, in full sentences.
- **Be specific and iterate:**
  - Provide as much context as possible.
  - Attach relevant documents by typing `@` to select from suggestions or type a file name.
  - Use descriptive prompts to clarify audience, purpose, and goals.
- **Make it a conversation:** Refine results with follow-up prompts. Build on previous outputs and use an iterative process of review and refinement.

## Sample Prompts by Application

Copy, paste, and customize these prompts to fit your needs. They are meant for illustration.

### Google Docs

- “Draft templates for 3 different types of customer communication. Create a template for apology emails, order confirmation messages, and thank you notes for loyal customers. Keep each template to one paragraph and use a friendly tone.”
- “Craft a list of customer communication best practices that can be used to train new team members. Outline 3 sections that include how to handle happy customer inquiries, neutral customer inquiries, and dissatisfied customer inquiries.”
- “Summarize this content to write a clear and concise product return policy.”
- “Draft an email template to a customer that highlights self-service resources [blog link] for [support issues]. Thank the customer for their business and assure them of our commitment to meet their needs.”
- “Write 3 engaging social media posts about [product or service or topic] that would appeal to [target audience]. Keep each social media post to 2 sentences and include a call to action to visit [our website].”

### Google Sheets

- “Create a table to track the progress and impact of different customer experience improvement tactics with relevant metrics that include support ticket volume and priority level (high, medium, low).”
- “Create a table that tracks weekly staffing. Create columns for date, name, shift (AM or PM), and notes.”
- ”Create a table that outlines a new employee’s first-week schedule, with key meetings, training sessions, and introductions. Provide a column for key contacts and priority level (low, medium, high) for each activity.”

### Google Slides

- “Create an image of a plane that flies above the clouds over mountains and rivers during sunrise that I can use in the marketing campaign to promote my travel company.”
- “Create a slide that outlines the next steps from @_[File name]_ and @_[File name]_.”
- “Create a slide about how to optimally train for a marathon.”
- ”Summarize this presentation in 3 sentences.”

### Google Vids

- “Explain our Q4 product roadmap for executives with @_[File name]_. This video informs company leadership of upcoming plans and strategy to achieve end of year goals.”
- “Remind employees to complete their annual self-assessments as explained in @_[File name]_. This process is important so this video will be shared with everyone in the company.”
- “Pitch video shared with internal customer service teams to use our latest product update with @_[File name]_. This video should convince potential customers of our product to try new features.”
