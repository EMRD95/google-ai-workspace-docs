# Use enhanced Smart Fill with AI in Google Sheets (Workspace Experiments)

> **Important:** This feature is part of the Google Workspace Experiments trusted tester program, currently available only to selected users in US English. Availability may vary by country. To check access, open a new sheet and look for the **`Help me organize`** sidebar on the right.

## How It Works

Enhanced Smart Fill uses AI to detect relationships between partially filled column pairs and instantly predicts the remaining values. It works with text columns only (not numeric/date columns) and requires:

- At least **3 example rows** with **2 distinct column values** to be filled.
- **English** text values.

### Triggering Suggestions

- Suggestions appear after an edit or **live while you type** (as soon as a prediction is available).
- A colored outline shows the range that Sheets can predict; hover over the suggestion icon to see the fill preview.

### Accepting or Rejecting

- **Accept:** Click the suggestion bubble.
- **Reject:** Click the cancel icon on the expanded suggestion pill.

## Example Use Cases

- Categorize feedback by theme
- Organize news articles by topic
- Convert addresses to a consistent format
- Extract phone numbers from text fields

## Giving Feedback

1. Click the three-dot menu on any suggestion.
2. Select **`Send feedback`**.

For general feedback: go to **Help** → **`Help Sheets improve`**.  
For legal issues: use [this form](https://support.google.com/legal/answer/3110420) or **`Send feedback`** → **`Report an issue`** / **`Suggest an idea`** → click **`Legal Help`** at the bottom of the sidebar.

## Turning Off Enhanced Smart Fill

You can disable only the AI-enhanced suggestions while keeping standard Smart Fill active.

### From a cell suggestion
1. Click the three-dot menu on any suggestion.
2. Uncheck **`Enable enhanced Smart Fill suggestions`**.

### From the menu
1. Go to **Tools** → **`Suggestion controls`**.
2. Uncheck **`Enable enhanced Smart Fill suggestions`**.

To re-enable, follow the same path and re-check the option.

> **Note:** When turned off, all undecided suggestions are removed from all sheets. This **does not** disable standard [Smart Fill](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/9914525) (e.g., extracting first names from full names).  
> Exiting the Labs program permanently revokes access to all Labs features and cannot be reversed. Learn about [exiting Labs](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13447104#labs-opt-out).

## Important Limitations & Data Practices

- **Only English** text values are supported.
- Suggestions are not professional advice and may be inaccurate or inappropriate.
- Google processes data according to the [Workspace Experiments Privacy Notice & Terms](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/13447401). Data may be read by human reviewers, but if Google-selected input is used to generate output, the content is **aggregated/pseudonymized** before review (unless provided directly via feedback).
- Avoid entering personal, confidential, or sensitive information.
- Your feedback helps improve the feature and broader AI efforts.
