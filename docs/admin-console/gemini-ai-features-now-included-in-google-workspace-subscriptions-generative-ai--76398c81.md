# Gemini AI Features Now Included in Google Workspace Subscriptions

**Source:** Google Workspace Help — “Gemini AI features now included in Google Workspace subscriptions”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/a/answer/15756885?hl=en  
**Last updated:** **2026-06-11 UTC**

---

## Overview

Google began including new Gemini AI features directly in **Google Workspace Business and Enterprise editions** starting in **January 2025**. These features were previously available through separate **Google Workspace with Gemini add-ons**.

The goal is to improve productivity and innovation across organizations by bringing Gemini-powered AI into Workspace apps and services.

---

## Key Excerpts

> In January 2025 we started adding new AI features designed to improve productivity and innovation in organizations with Google Workspace Business and Enterprise editions. Previously these features were included in Google Workspace with Gemini add-ons.

> Your existing Google Workspace data protections are automatically applied to AI features.

> Generative AI doesn't change these commitments — it reaffirms their importance.

> **Your interactions with Gemini stay within your organization**. Gemini does not share your content outside your organization without your permission.

> **Your existing Google Workspace protections are automatically applied.** Gemini brings the same enterprise-grade security as the rest of Google Workspace.

> **Your content is not used for any other customers.** Your content is not human reviewed or used for Generative AI model training outside your domain without permission.

---

## Add-ons No Longer Available for Purchase

As part of the transition, the following add-ons are no longer available for purchase:

- **Gemini Business–Legacy**
- **Gemini Enterprise–Legacy**
- **AI Meetings and Messaging**
- **AI Security**

### Education Exception

The **Google AI Pro for Education add-on** remains available to educational organizations.

Previously, this was known as:

- **Gemini Education**
- **Gemini Education Premium**

Google recommends reviewing the related [Google Workspace blog announcement](https://workspace.google.com/blog/product-announcements/empowering-businesses-with-AI) for more details.

## AI Features Coming to Organizations

Available AI features depend on the organization’s **Google Workspace edition**.

### Highlighted Gemini and AI Features

- **Gemini in the side panel**
  - Available in:
    - Gmail
    - Docs
    - Sheets
    - Slides
    - Drive
    - Chat
  - Provides AI assistance directly in the flow of work.
  - Related help: [Gemini in the side panel](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/15146419)

- **Help me write in Gmail and Docs**
  - Helps users quickly compose professional emails and documents.
  - Related help: [Help me write](https://support.google.com/a/users/answer/14747862)

- **Take notes for me in Meet**
  - Automatically captures meeting notes so users can focus on the conversation.
  - Related help: [Take notes for me in Meet](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/14754931)

- **Gemini app**
  - Lets users chat with Google’s next-generation AI for:
    - Learning
    - Brainstorming
    - Planning
    - Coding
    - Deep research
    - Data analysis
  - Available on laptop or mobile device.
  - Includes **Gems**, which can be used to build a team of AI experts for repeatable tasks or complex projects.
  - Related admin help: [Gemini app settings](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/gemini-app/turn-the-gemini-app-on-or-off#advanced)

- **NotebookLM**
  - Helps accelerate team knowledge sharing.
  - Users can upload sources to generate:
    - Instant insights
    - Podcast-like **Audio Overviews**
  - Enhanced version includes:
    - Notebook customization
    - Advanced sharing
    - Usage analytics
    - Increased Audio Overviews
    - Increased queries
    - Increased notebooks
    - Increased sources
  - Related product page: [NotebookLM Plus](https://notebooklm.google/plus)

- **Gemini in AppSheet**
  - Includes:
    - **Gemini for App Creation**
      - Build apps by describing a business process or idea in natural language.
      - Related help: [Gemini for App Creation](https://support.google.com/appsheet/answer/14699210)
    - **Gemini in AppSheet Solutions**
      - Add AI tasks in automations to extract and categorize information from images and documents.
      - Related help: [Gemini in AppSheet Solutions](https://support.google.com/appsheet/answer/16045284)
