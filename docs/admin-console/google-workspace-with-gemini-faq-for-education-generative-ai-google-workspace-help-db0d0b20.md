# Google Workspace with Gemini FAQ for Education – Summary

## What is the Gemini App?
- **Components**: Web app (`gemini.google.com`), mobile apps (Android/iOS), Gemini in Chrome, Gemini on Mac.
- Sometimes called “Gemini” or “Gemini Apps”.
- For work or school accounts, data handling terms may differ.

## Eligibility & Core Service
- **All Education editions** – Fundamentals, Standard, Plus – can use the Gemini app as a **core service** with enterprise-grade data protections.
- **Google AI Pro for Education** is the add-on edition that includes Gemini (latest models, priority features).

> “Gemini includes everything in the Gemini app with enterprise-grade data protections as well as priority access to new features and access to Google’s most capable AI models.”

- Chats and uploaded files are **not** reviewed by humans and **not** used to train generative AI models.  
  Subject to the [Google Workspace for Education Terms of Service](https://workspace.google.com/terms/education_terms).

## Licensing: Who Needs a License?
- **All Google Workspace for Education users** get the Gemini app as a core service without an additional add-on.
- **Google AI Pro for Education add-on license** unlocks Gemini (advanced models and features) for the user.  
  Admins assign licenses to grant access.

## Usage Limits
- With the Google AI Pro for Education add-on, each user can use Gemini app and Workspace AI features **1,000 times per month**.
- Limits reset on the **first day of each month**.

## Getting Started Without Current Workspace
- Educational institutions without Google Workspace for Education can [submit a request](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/getting-started/editions/get-started-with-google-workspace-for-education) for an Education edition, then contact sales for the Google AI Pro for Education add-on.

## Data Protection & Privacy
- Same robust security as all Google Workspace services.
- **With a Google AI Pro license**: Submissions **not used to train models**, never reviewed by humans. For users without a license, see [details](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/gemini-app/turn-the-gemini-app-on-or-off).
- Interactions stay **within your organization**; prompts and generated content are stored alongside your Workspace content and not shared outside.
- Existing protections (data-regions policies, DLP) are automatically applied.
- “None of your content is used for model training outside of your domain without permission.”
- Learn more: [Generative AI in Google Workspace Privacy Hub](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/generative-ai-in-google-workspace-privacy-hub).

## Security Against Gen AI Threats
- Gemini **automatically analyses content** for malicious URLs or prompt injections before summarising it.
- If a threat is detected, the user is notified and the content is excluded from the response.  
  [Learn more](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/security/how-google-helps-protect-gemini-users-from-malicious-content-and-prompt-injections)
