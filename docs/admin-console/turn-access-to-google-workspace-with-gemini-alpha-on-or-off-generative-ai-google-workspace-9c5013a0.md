# Summary: Turn Access to Google Workspace with Gemini Alpha On or Off

As a Google Workspace administrator, you can enable Gemini alpha features so users can try them before general availability.

---

## Important Notes About the Alpha Program

- **Default state**: Off by default; only admins can enable.
- **Scope**: Can be turned on for all users or specific organizational units/groups.
- **Data protection**: Same robust standards as all Workspace services.
- **Usage limits**: Alpha features may be subject to limits when they reach general availability.
- **Edition availability**: Not all alpha features are available in all Workspace editions (see features table below).
- **Feature list changes**: Google periodically adds or removes alpha features; this page reflects current supported features.

---

## Terms That Apply

Enabling Gemini alpha features means your organization agrees to the [Google Workspace Service Specific Terms](https://workspace.google.com/terms/service-terms/):

- **Business & Enterprise** and **Education** both reference:
  - Pre-General Availability Offering Terms (excluding Section 6.1(b))
  - Generative AI Services sections of the Service Specific Terms
- Data protection and processing obligations under the Cloud Data Processing Addendum (CDPA) apply.
