# Gemini AI Features Now Included in Google Workspace Subscriptions

## Overview
Beginning in **January 2025**, Google started integrating new AI features directly into **Google Workspace Business and Enterprise editions**. These capabilities were previously exclusive to paid Gemini add‑ons. As part of this transition, several legacy add‑ons are no longer sold.

> **Key excerpt:**  
> “In January 2025 we started adding new AI features designed to improve productivity and innovation in organizations with Google Workspace Business and Enterprise editions.”

## Discontinued Add‑ons
The following are **no longer available for purchase**:

- Gemini Business–Legacy  
- Gemini Enterprise–Legacy  
- AI Meetings and Messaging  
- AI Security  

**Education exception:** The **Google AI Pro for Education** add‑on (formerly Gemini Education and Gemini Education Premium) **remains available** for educational organizations.

---

## AI Features Being Added
The features your organization receives depend on the Workspace edition. Highlights include:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Gemini in the side panel** | AI assistance directly in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Drive, and Chat. |
| **Help me write** | Quickly compose professional emails and documents in Gmail and Docs. |
| **Take notes for me** | Automatically capture meeting notes in Google Meet. |
| **Gemini app (gemini.google.com)** | Chat with next‑gen AI for learning, brainstorming, and planning. Use **Gems** to create a team of AI experts for repeatable tasks, coding, deep research, and data analysis. |
| **NotebookLM** | Upload sources to get instant insights and podcast‑style **Audio Overviews**. Enhanced version includes notebook customisation, advanced sharing, usage analytics, and higher limits for overviews, queries, notebooks, and sources. |
| **Gemini in AppSheet** | • **Gemini for App Creation** – build apps by describing a process in natural language.<br>• **Gemini in AppSheet Solutions** – add AI tasks in automations to extract and categorise information from images and documents. |

For complete feature lists by edition:

- [Enterprise editions](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/getting-started/editions/compare-enterprise-editions#gemini)

## Control & Management Options
Admins and end users have multiple ways to adjust AI settings:

### Admin Controls
- **Manage Gemini features in Workspace services** – Enterprise editions only.
- **Gemini app (gemini.google.com)** – can be turned on/off (does not affect other AI features in Workspace services).
- **NotebookLM** – turn on/off as an [additional service](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/users/advanced/turn-on-or-off-additional-google-services).
- **Google Vids** – [turn access on or off](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/users/access/turn-vids-on-or-off-for-users).
- **AI note‑taking in Meet** – control if users can [let Google AI take notes](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/meet/let-google-meet-ai-take-notes-for-my-users).
- **Smart features** – turn [Google Workspace smart features](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/security/manage-google-workspace-smart-features-for-your-users) on/off.
- **Gemini in AppSheet** – separately toggle [Gemini for App Creation](https://support.google.com/appsheet/answer/11918568#gemini-for-app-creation) and [Gemini in AppSheet Solutions](https://support.google.com/appsheet/answer/16106849).
- **Workspace Studio** – [turn on/off](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/users/access/turn-google-workspace-studio-on-or-off-for-your-organization) or block Gemini access via [API controls](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/studio/manage-access-to-steps-and-starters-in-workspace-studio#service).

### End‑User Controls
- Users can [manage smart features in Google products](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/10079371).

> **Automated protections:**  
> “Your existing Google Workspace data protections are automatically applied to AI features.”

## Privacy & Data Protections
Google emphasizes that generative AI **does not alter** its foundational privacy commitments.

> **Key privacy statements:**
> - *“Your interactions with Gemini stay within your organization. Gemini does not share your content outside your organization without your permission.”*
> - *“Your existing Google Workspace protections are automatically applied. Gemini brings the same enterprise‑grade security as the rest of Google Workspace.”*
> - *“Your content is not used for any other customers. Your content is not human reviewed or used for Generative AI model training outside your domain without permission.”*

This means data handling remains consistent with the broader [Google Workspace privacy commitments](https://cloud.google.com/privacy).
