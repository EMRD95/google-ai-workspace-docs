# Supported Languages for Google Workspace with Gemini

This help page outlines the language availability for various Gemini-powered features across Google Workspace apps.

> **Key Note:** Features not listed below default to English only. To use those, set your Google Account language to English.

---

## Gmail

### Help Me Write
- English
- French
- German
- Italian
- Japanese
- Korean
- Portuguese
- Spanish

> **Restriction:** For personal Google Accounts, this feature is available **only in the US** (English).

### Gemini Side Panel
- Arabic, Catalan, Chinese, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Finnish, French\*, German\*, Greek, Hebrew, Hungarian, Indonesian, Italian\*, Japanese\*, Korean\*, Malay, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese\*, Romanian, Russian, Spanish\*, Swedish, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Vietnamese

> **\*** Languages that also support **Gems** in the side panel (French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Spanish).

### AI Overview Conversation Summaries
- English, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Spanish
