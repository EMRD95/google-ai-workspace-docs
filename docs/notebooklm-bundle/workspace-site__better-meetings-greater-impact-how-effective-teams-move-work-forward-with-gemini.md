### It’s time for a better way to collaborate.

Are your days filled with back-to-back meetings that end without clear next steps? You’re not alone. [Harvard Business Review](https://hbr.org/2024/03/how-to-improve-a-meeting-when-youre-not-in-charge) reports that 71% of senior managers feel meetings are “inefficient and unproductive.”

What if you could cut through meeting friction, freeing your team to focus on productive, effective collaboration?
