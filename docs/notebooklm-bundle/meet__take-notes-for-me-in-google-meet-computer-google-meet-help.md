# Google Meet Help: “Take notes for me” in Google Meet — Summary

**Source:** [Google Meet Help](https://support.google.com/meet/answer/14754931?hl=en)  
**Feature:** Gemini-powered AI meeting notes in Google Meet

---

## Key Excerpts & Important Facts

> **Important:**  
> - This feature requires an eligible Google Workspace subscription.  
> - This feature is available in:
>   - English
>   - French
>   - German
>   - Italian
>   - Japanese
>   - Korean
>   - Portuguese
>   - Spanish

> **Tip:** This feature supports one language at a time. Multiple languages spoken in the same meeting aren't currently supported.

> **Important:** The feature is recommended for meetings of **15 minutes to a maximum of 8 hours**.

> If Host Management is turned on in Meet, **only meeting hosts can turn "take notes for me" on or off**.

> The meeting notes document is generated shortly after the meeting ends and is saved in the meeting organizer’s Drive folder.

---

## What “Take notes for me” Does

In Google Meet, Gemini can automatically capture and organize meeting notes into a Google Docs document.

With this feature, users can:

- Automatically generate meeting notes in Google Docs.
- Share notes with the team.
- Use **“Summary so far”** to catch up if joining late.
- Receive an email recap shortly after the meeting ends.
- Access the recap link from the associated Google Calendar event.

---

## Availability & Requirements

### Subscription Requirement

- Requires an eligible **Google Workspace subscription** that includes Gemini features.

### Supported Spoken Languages

Meetings must be conducted in one of the following spoken languages:

- English
- French
- German
- Italian
- Japanese
- Korean
- Portuguese
- Spanish

### Language Limitation

- Only **one language at a time** is supported.
- Meetings with multiple spoken languages are not currently supported.

## Consent Requirements

Administrators may require participants to provide explicit consent before certain meeting features can be used, including:

- **“take notes for me”**
- **Record**
- **Transcribe**

This admin setting is **off by default**, but can be enabled.

### Consent Actions

- To start a feature and consent: click **Start**
- To join a meeting with an active feature: click **Join**
- To stay after a feature is enabled: click **Continue**
- To exit instead: click **Leave**

### Quick Stop

If the admin has enabled **Quick Stop** and host controls are off:

- An internal user can click **Stop** to end capture for all participants.

## How to Use “Take notes for me”

You can turn on the feature from:

- Google Calendar
- The pre-meeting Google Meet greenroom
- Inside the Google Meet call
- In-person or on-the-go meetings via the related “take notes for me anywhere” feature

> Depending on your Admin’s settings, "take notes for me" may be automatically turned on for certain meetings.

---

## Turn It On from Google Calendar

A meeting organizer can enable note-taking when creating or editing a Calendar event that already includes Google Meet.

### Steps

1. Go to [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/).
2. Select your event or meeting.
3. Click **Edit event**.
4. Add Google Meet video conferencing if needed.
5. Select **Video call options**.
6. In the left column, click **Meeting records**.
7. Check or uncheck the box next to **“Take notes with Gemini.”**

---

## Turn It On During a Google Meet Call

1. Go to [meet.google.com](https://meet.google.com/).
2. Select a meeting.
3. After joining, click the **“take notes for me”** icon at the top right of the screen.
4. Optionally adjust settings before starting, such as:
   - Who receives notes
   - Meeting notes language
   - Notes length
   - Section customization
5. Click **Start taking notes**.

---

## Customizable Settings

Some settings are available before or after starting note-taking.

> **Important:** Sections customization and screenshots of presented content are only available to Alpha users.

### Send Notes To

Only hosts and co-hosts can see this menu.

Sharing options include:

- **All invited guests, including those outside of my organization**
  - Shares notes with everyone invited.
- **Invited guests in my organization**
  - Shares notes only with internal invitees.
- **Hosts and co-hosts only**
  - Shares notes only with hosts and co-hosts.

### Important Sharing Notes

- Recipients receive an email with access to the generated notes doc after the meeting.
- “Invited guests” means guests on the Calendar invite, **not necessarily attendees**.
- If guests are added through a group email, group members may need to request individual access.
- For instant meetings, notes are shared with:
  - Hosts
  - Whoever initiated “take notes for me”

---

## Notes Length Options

Under **“Notes length,”** users can choose:

- **Standard**
  - Provides a concise summary of the meeting.
- **Longer**
  - Includes more details and comprehensive discussion points.
