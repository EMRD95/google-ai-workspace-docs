# Generate Video Overviews in NotebookLM — Summary

**Source:** [NotebookLM Help](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16454555?hl=en)  
**Topic:** How to generate, customize, play, manage, share, download, and set languages for Video Overviews in NotebookLM.

---

## Key Excerpts & Important Notes

> **Important:** At this time, the NotebookLM mobile app may have limitations to this feature.

> Video Overviews transform the sources in your notebook into an engaging video. They distill complex information into clear, digestible content, providing a comprehensive and engaging visual deep dive of your material.

> To generate or delete a Video Overview, you need edit access to a notebook.

> Video Overviews, including voices and visuals, are AI-generated and may contain inaccuracies or audio glitches.

> NotebookLM may take a while to generate the Video Overview — sometimes more than 30 minutes.

> NotebookLM uses the preferred language from your Google Account settings as your default output language.

# What Are Video Overviews?

**Video Overviews** are AI-generated videos created from the sources inside a NotebookLM notebook. They are designed to turn complex material into a more digestible, visual explanation.

They can include:

- Narration
- Visuals
- Storytelling or structured explanation
- Source-based summaries
- Custom focus areas based on user instructions

---

# Generate a Video Overview

## Requirements

- You must open an existing notebook or create a new one.
- You must upload sources.
- You need **edit access** to generate or delete a Video Overview.

## Steps

1. Open an existing notebook or create a new notebook and upload sources.
2. In the **Studio** panel, select **Video Overview**.
3. Optionally customize the video before generation.
4. Select **Generate**.

## Background Generation

Video generation runs in the background, so you can:

- Generate other artifacts at the same time
- Navigate to other screens
- Return to the notebook later

Generation may take a while, sometimes **more than 30 minutes**.

# Optional Customization Settings

Before generating a Video Overview, you can customize several options.

## Format

Choose the type of video:

### Cinematic

- Available only to users **18+**
- Currently supports **English only**
- Designed as a rich, immersive experience
- Uses visuals and storytelling to unpack complex ideas

### Explainer

- A structured, comprehensive overview
- Connects ideas across your sources

### Brief

- A shorter, bite-sized video
- Helps quickly communicate the core ideas of a document

---

## Language

You can choose the narration language from NotebookLM’s supported languages.

NotebookLM supports Video Overviews in **80+ languages**.

---

## Visual Style

Available for **18+ users**, excluding Cinematic Video Overviews.

Example visual styles include:

- **Classic**
- **Whiteboard**
- **Watercolor**
- **Retro Print**
- **Heritage**
- **Paper-craft**
- **Kawaii**
- **Anime**

You can also:

- Let NotebookLM **auto-select** a visual style
- Choose **Custom** and enter a description to generate a custom visual style

---

## Steering Prompt

Use a steering prompt to guide NotebookLM’s focus.

Examples of what you can specify:

- Important sources
- Specific topics
- Desired emphasis
- Areas to prioritize or avoid

---

# Manage a Video Overview

After a Video Overview is generated, you can manage playback and feedback.

Available controls:

- Change playback speed from **“1x”**
- Use the playback slider to move forward or backward
- Tap **rewind** or **skip forward**
- Enter full-screen mode
- Send feedback by selecting:
  - **Good video**
  - **Bad video**

---

# Play a Previously Generated Video Overview

> **Important:** This only applies to notebooks that already have generated Video Overviews.

## Steps

1. Open an existing notebook.
2. In the **Studio** panel, select the title of the generated Video Overview.
3. When the video player opens, press **Play**.

## Tip

To view the prompt used to generate the overview:

- Open the three-dot menu next to the artifact in the Studio panel
- Select **View custom prompt**

# Share a Video Overview

There are three sharing options.

---

## Option 1: Share a Link

### Steps

1. Generate a Video Overview.
2. In the video player, select **Share**.
3. Confirm that:
   - The notebook is shared with the recipient, or
   - The notebook is public and open to **Anyone with a link**
   - Viewers have access to the **full notebook**
4. Copy the Video Overview link.
5. Save to apply changes.

### Important Sharing Notes

- Public notebook sharing is only enabled for **consumer accounts**.
- Public sharing is currently disabled for:
  - Workspace Enterprise accounts
  - Education accounts
- Only notebook **owners and editors** can make a generated video publicly available and manage access.
- Owners or editors can turn off public access by changing the notebook access level in the sharing panel.
