# Use Suggested Replies in Gmail (Workspace Experiments) – Summary

**Important context:** This feature is part of **Google Workspace Experiments**, a trusted tester program for new AI features. It uses Gemini to suggest personalized email replies. Access may be limited; check by replying to an email and looking for suggestions at the bottom. [See where Workspace Experiments features are available](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/13607340).

---

## Requirements

- **Workspace smart features** must be turned on in Gmail settings. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/15604322).
- **Google Account language** must be set to English. [Change language](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32047).
- Even if requirements are met, suggested replies are not always available.

---

## How to Use

1. Open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/) on a computer.
2. Open an email.
3. At the bottom of the email, click the **suggested reply**.
   - You can edit the text before sending.
4. Click **Send**.

> **Note:** The feature is currently available on Computer, Android, iPhone & iPad (device switcher present).

---

## Personalization Details

> *“Suggested Replies with personalization are available in English.”*

- Gemini draws details from your **emails and Drive files** (e.g., flight times, hotel booking codes, soccer schedules) to craft a relevant response.
- The response matches your **tone and writing style**.
- Click **“Sources”** above a suggestion to see which emails/docs were used. You cannot edit the source list.

---

## Giving Feedback

Google Workspace with Gemini is still learning; feedback helps improve it. **Do not submit personal, confidential, or sensitive information** because feedback may be read by humans.

- **Quick feedback:** Click **Send feedback** (icon) at the top of a suggested reply, write your comment, and **Submit**.
- **Feedback with screenshots:** Click **Help** (question mark icon) → **Send feedback to Google**. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/9212652).
- **Legal issues:** [Create a request](https://support.google.com/legal/answer/3110420).

---

## Turning Off Workspace Experiments

- To disable these AI features, you must **exit Workspace Experiments entirely**.
- **This action is permanent** – you lose access to all Workspace Experiments features and cannot rejoin.
- [How to exit](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/13447104#labs-opt-out&exit-labs-docs&exit-labs-gmail).

---

## Important Disclaimers

> *“Workspace Experiments feature suggestions don’t represent Google’s views, and should not be attributed to Google.”*

- **Not professional advice** – don’t rely on it for medical, legal, financial, or other professional matters.
- Suggestions may be **inaccurate or inappropriate**.
- **Don’t include personal, confidential, or sensitive information** in prompts.
- Google uses Workspace Experiments data to **improve products, services, and machine learning technologies**.
- Your data may be **read, rated, annotated, or reviewed by humans**. Where Google uses Google-selected input, the content is aggregated/pseudonymized before human review, unless provided as feedback.

---

## Data Collected

When you select a suggested reply, Google stores:

- The **email thread**
- The **selected reply**
- Any **feedback** you submit

Review the full [Google Workspace Experiments Privacy Notice and Terms for Personal Accounts](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/13447401).
