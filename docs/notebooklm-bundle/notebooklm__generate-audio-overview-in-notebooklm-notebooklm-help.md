Source: [NotebookLM Help](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16212820?hl=en)

## Key Excerpts & Important Notes

> **Important:** At this time, the NotebookLM mobile app may have limitations to this feature.

> Audio Overviews are deep-dive discussions between AI hosts that provide in-depth summaries of the key topics in your uploaded sources. These overviews are designed to be an objective reflection of your source content, rather than subjective opinions from the AI hosts.

> **Important:**
>
> - Audio Overviews, including voices, are AI-generated and may contain inaccuracies or audio glitches.
> - NotebookLM may take a couple of minutes to generate the Audio Overview.

> **Tip:** You can also listen to Audio Overviews while continuing to work within NotebookLM. You can explore relevant quotes, get citations, and query sources while listening to an Audio Overview in the background.

> **Important:** The interactive mode experience is currently available only in English.

> You can generate Audio Overviews in 80+ languages.

## What Audio Overviews Are

**Audio Overviews** are AI-generated audio discussions in NotebookLM based on uploaded notebook sources. They summarize and explain key topics from the sources, typically through AI-hosted conversations.

They are intended to be:

- **Source-grounded**
- **Objective reflections** of the uploaded material
- Useful for listening while continuing to work in NotebookLM

However, they may include:

- Inaccuracies
- Audio glitches
- AI-generated voice artifacts

---

## Generate an Audio Overview

### Requirements

To generate or delete an Audio Overview, you need **edit access** to the notebook.

### Steps

1. Open an existing notebook or create a new notebook.
2. Upload sources.
3. In the **Studio** panel, select **Audio Overview**.
4. NotebookLM generates the audio in the background.
   - You can continue generating other artifacts.
   - You can navigate to other screens while it processes.
5. Wait a couple of minutes for generation to complete.

---

## Customize an Audio Overview

Within the generation panel, you can set preferences before generating the audio.

### Format Options

- **Deep Dive — default**
  - An in-depth conversation.
  - Two hosts unpack and connect topics from your sources in a lively discussion.

- **The Brief**
  - A quick overview.
  - A single speaker delivers key takeaways in under two minutes.

- **The Critique**
  - Critical feedback.
  - Two hosts provide a constructive evaluation of material such as an essay or design document.

- **The Debate**
  - Multiple perspectives.
  - Two hosts engage in a formal back-and-forth debate on the topic.

### Other Customization Options

- Choose a **language**.
- Tailor the **length**:
  - Shorter
  - Default
  - Longer  
  - **English only**
- Add a **custom prompt** to:
  - Focus on specific topics
  - Adjust the expertise level
  - Provide other instructions

## Manage an Audio Overview

### Playback & Feedback

- Change playback speed:
  - Select **More**
  - Choose **Change playback speed**

- Send feedback:
  - Tap **Thumbs up**
  - Tap **Thumbs down**

### Work While Listening

You can continue using NotebookLM while the Audio Overview plays in the background, including:

- Exploring relevant quotes
- Getting citations
- Querying sources

## Load a Previous Audio Overview

> **Important:** This is only applicable to notebooks that have previously generated Audio Overviews.

### Steps

1. Open an existing notebook.
2. In the **Studio** panel, find the previously generated audio.
3. Select **Load**.
4. Wait for the overview to load.

### View the Original Prompt

To see the prompt used to generate an Audio Overview:

1. Open the three-dot menu next to the artifact in the **Studio** panel.
2. Select **“View custom prompt”**.

## Share an Audio Overview

There are three ways to share an Audio Overview.

---

### Option 1: Share a Link

#### Steps

1. Generate an Audio Overview.
2. In the audio player, select **Share**.
3. Confirm that:
   - The notebook is shared with the recipient, **or**
   - The notebook is public/open to **“Anyone with a link”**
   - Viewers have access to the **“full notebook”**
4. Copy the Audio Overview link.
5. Share the link with the recipient.
6. Save to apply changes.

#### Important Sharing Notes

- Public notebook sharing is only enabled for **consumer accounts**.
- Public sharing is currently disabled for:
  - Workspace Enterprise accounts
  - Education accounts
- Only notebook **owners and editors** can:
  - Make generated audio publicly available
  - Manage access
- Owners/editors can turn off public access by changing the notebook access level in the sharing panel.
- If an audio is deleted, previously generated share links stop working.
