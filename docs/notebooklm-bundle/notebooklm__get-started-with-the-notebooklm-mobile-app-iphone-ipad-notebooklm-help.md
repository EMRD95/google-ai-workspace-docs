# Get started with the NotebookLM mobile app – iPhone & iPad

**Source:** [NotebookLM Help](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16296687?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DiOS)

## Key Excerpts

> **"Our early version of the NotebookLM mobile app includes many core features of NotebookLM and is designed to help you understand anything, anywhere."**

**Core feature list from the app:**
- Ask questions about your sources
- Listen to Audio Overviews (on‑the‑go, with offline download)
- Review material via flashcards or quizzes
- Visualize key insights with infographics and slide decks
- Share content directly from websites, PDFs, or YouTube videos into NotebookLM

**Device Requirement:** iOS 17 and higher.

## How to Download

1. Open the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/google-notebooklm/id6737527615) on iPhone or iPad, or search “NotebookLM”.
2. Select the official NotebookLM app.
3. Tap **Get**.

> **Note:** Learn how to manage iOS app downloads [from Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-ph/guide/app-store/fir9b2ea074e/mac).

## How to Use the Mobile App

### Basic Workflow
1. Open the app, open or create a notebook.
   - Tapping ![New tab](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/FcuQd8H08YOV7Pep2qW6b1FI9Cnp4XEblzGDdnZzXUqPRoY4pXY_rfW8S6SQ0y7QuA=w36-h36) **Create New**.
   - Or select an existing notebook from Recent, Shared, Title, or Downloaded.
2. Add a source: PDF, Website, YouTube, Audio File, or Copied Text.
   - While browsing, use the share icon → select NotebookLM to add a source directly.
   - Find Sources from the web by entering a query in the prompt box.
3. **Chat panel:** Displays a generated summary of all sources. Ask questions, use source selector to include/exclude sources. Chat history is retained unless you “Delete Chat History”.
4. **Studio panel:** For deeper engagement – Audio Overviews, Video Overviews, flashcards, quizzes, infographics, slide decks.

> **Important:** Chat responses, audio, videos, flashcards, quizzes are AI-generated and may contain inaccuracies or audio glitches. Generation may take a couple of minutes.

### Fast Research
- Use the prompt box on the home panel (or in a notebook’s source adder) to search the web for relevant sources.
- Review results: title, brief description of relevance, link to full webpage.
- Select one or multiple results to import directly into the notebook.

### Audio Overviews
- Generate or play in the **Studio** panel.
- Customize via pencil icon: language, length, source selection, custom prompts.
- Playback controls: play/pause, skip, speed, background listening.
- **Download** (icon ![](https://storage.googleapis.com/support-kms-prod/KBhZb2G131WdFqiuUWsj6y0xpWdDZp6im2ct)) for offline access; found in the Downloaded section. Swipe left in studio to download/delete.
- **Interactive Mode (Beta, English Only):** Join conversation with AI hosts. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16212820#interactive_mode).

### Video Overviews
- Generate or play in **Studio**. Customize format, sources, visual style, prompts.
- Play/pause, full screen, skip, speed, rename. Swipe left to delete. **Not downloadable** at this time.

### Flashcards & Quizzes
- Use to memorize key terms, dates, concepts.
- Generate in **Studio**, customize language, number, difficulty, sources, prompts.
- **Flashcard controls:** Tap to flip, shuffle, delete. Mark “Got it!” (✓) or “Missed it!” (✕). After completing, review results, retake whole deck or only missed cards. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16958963).
