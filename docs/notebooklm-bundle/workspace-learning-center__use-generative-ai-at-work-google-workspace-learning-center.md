# 1. Write & Respond to Email in Gmail

Use **Help me write** in Gmail to draft emails quickly, such as meeting requests. Gemini can generate a draft in seconds, which you can then refine and send.

## Steps

### Step 1: Open Help me write in Gmail

1. Open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/) on your computer.
2. Click **Compose**.
3. In the bottom-left of the compose window, click **Help me write**.

### Step 2: Enter a Prompt

Example prompt:

```text
Write an email asking for a meeting about [project name]. Include a bulleted list of discussion points including milestones, task status, and next steps.
```

Then click **Create**.

### Step 3: Review, Refine & Insert

1. Click **Refine**.
2. Choose how you want Gemini to change the content.
3. When finished, click **Insert**.

# 2. Speed Up & Polish Your Writing in Docs

Use Gemini in Google Docs to:

- Start from a blank page
- Create a first draft quickly
- Shorten existing text
- Elaborate on text
- Summarize content
- Adjust tone
- Transform text into another form, such as a **poem** or **script**

---

## Create a First Draft

### Step 1: Open Help me write in Docs

1. Open a [Google Docs file](https://docs.google.com/document/).
2. Click where you want to write.
3. On the left, click **Help me write**.

### Step 2: Tell Gemini What to Write

Example prompt:

```text
Write an outline of [project x]. Specify 4 phases: Research, Design, Testing, and Production. The project will kick off Q2 this year and wrap up in Q2 next year.
```

Then click **Create**.

### Step 3: Review, Refine & Insert

1. Review the response.
2. Click **Refine**.
3. Choose how Gemini should change the content.
4. Click **Insert** when finished.

---

## Polish Existing Writing

1. Open a [Google Docs file](https://docs.google.com/document/).
2. Select the text you want to polish.
3. On the left, click **Help me write**.
4. Choose how Gemini should change the content.
5. Click **Insert** when finished.

# 3. Brainstorm for New Ideas in the Gemini App

Use the Gemini app for brainstorming, writing, learning, and related tasks.

## Step 1: Start a Conversation

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Sign in to your Google Account if needed.
3. Enter a question or prompt in the text box.

Example prompt:

```text
[Product name] is a product that will [describe what product will do]. Give me 10 things I can do to get customers excited about the launch of [product name].
```

4. Click **Submit**.

---

## Step 2: Review and Continue the Conversation

After Gemini responds, you can:

### View Other Drafts

For some prompts:

1. Click **View other drafts** or **Show drafts** above the response.
2. Select the draft you want to review.

### Revise Your Prompt

1. Click **Edit text** to the right of your prompt.
2. Edit the prompt.
3. Click **Update**.

### Modify the Response

Click **Modify response**, then choose an option:

- **Shorter** — reduce length
- **Longer** — expand the response
- **Simpler** — simplify language
- **More casual** — make tone more casual
- **More professional** — make tone more professional
