## Frequently asked questions

Take a look at our FAQs to learn more.

Expand all

- NotebookLM supports various source types including Google Docs, Google Slides, PDFs, text and markdown files, web URLs, copy-pasted text, public YouTube video URLs, and audio files. Each source can contain up to 500,000 words or up to 200MB for uploaded files.
- NotebookLM prioritizes privacy and security. The sources you upload stay private unless you choose to share a Notebook. NotebookLM never trains models on your uploaded data.
- NotebookLM is available to anyone with a Google Workspace account. Users with a qualifying Workspace plan get access to [higher limits and additional features](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16213268?hl=en-CA) of NotebookLM.
- [Advanced chat settings](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/14276569?hl=en-CA) in NotebookLM let you customize response style, including longer or shorter output lengths. Users can also create custom response styles (such as an academic or analytical style) based on specific needs.
- NotebookLM does not currently keep a history of questions and responses in chat. You can save notes by pinning the response.
