# How Google Workspace with Gemini Billing Works – Summary

**Source:** [Google Workspace Help](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/workspace-with-gemini/how-google-workspace-with-gemini-billing-works)

---

## Billing Cycle

- After signing up, the **primary payment method is charged at the start of the next month**.
- Example: If service starts in May, charge occurs at the beginning of June.
- Billing continues automatically each month thereafter.

> *Note:* For customers with no prior Google payments, a **$100 early payment** may be required before purchasing AI Ultra Access.

---

## Billing Plans

### Flexible Plan
- Pay **per user per month**.
- Licenses can be added or removed anytime.
- **Prorated billing** for partial months.
  - *Example:* License added April 1, removed April 15 → charged for 15 days (half a month).
- No advance commitment required.

### Annual/Fixed-Term Plan
- Pay each month for a **monthly portion of the committed licenses**.
- Licenses can be **added anytime**, increasing the monthly charge.
- **Reducing licenses is only possible at renewal** (end of contract term).
- **Lowest per-user monthly price** compared to Flexible Plan.
- Cancelling before end of term → **full remaining contract value** is charged.

---

## Cancellation Charges

| **Plan**              | **Charge after Cancellation**                                                                                                 |
|-----------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Flexible Plan**     | Charged for days used in the month (e.g., cancel on 15th → half a month of service).                                         |
| **Annual/Fixed-Term** | **Full remaining contract balance** is charged. You remain responsible for the total commitment even if cancelled early.     |

---

## Threshold Billing

- If you **exceed your monthly billing threshold**, the primary payment method is **charged immediately** to reduce the unpaid balance.  
  _(Refer to [Understand threshold billing](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/billing/understand-google-workspace-bills-and-charges#threshold_billing) for details.)_

---

## Important Note for New Workspace Customers

- Purchases of **AI Ultra Access directly online** are billed on the **Flexible Plan** automatically.
- If the account has **never paid for Google services**, a **$100 early payment** may be required before purchase.  
  Learn how to [remove restrictions on new Google Workspace accounts](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/billing/remove-restrictions-on-new-google-workspace-accounts).

## Key Takeaways

- All charges are **monthly**; no upfront annual payment even for Annual/Fixed-Term – it’s a commitment, not a lump sum.
- Proration applies **only** to the Flexible Plan when licenses change mid‑month.
- Annual/Fixed-Term saves money **per license**, but **locks you in** – early exit incurs full remaining liability.
- Immediate threshold billing can trigger if unpaid balance exceeds the account’s threshold.
- Educational institution pricing is slightly lower through the Annual/Fixed-Term plan.

For questions not covered, the page suggests contacting a reseller or using the provided feedback options.
