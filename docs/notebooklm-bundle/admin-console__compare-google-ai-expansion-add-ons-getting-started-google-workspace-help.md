## Purpose

Google AI expansion add-ons let organizations get **higher access to advanced AI capabilities in Google Workspace**, including advanced video/image generation, Workspace Studio, NotebookLM, Google Flow, and other Gemini-powered features.

---

## Key Excerpts

> Google AI expansion add-ons enable organizations to get higher access to advanced AI capabilities in Workspace.

> Your organization's use of advanced AI features in Google Workspace is subject to usage limits. These limits are based on which Google Workspace edition you're using and any AI expansion licenses that you've assigned to users.

> Usage limits don't roll over, and they can't be shared with other people.

> **Important:** Some of the products below are not part of Google Workspace. They are additional services and are subject to their own terms of service. Interactions with these tools may be used to train models.

> \* AI Ultra Access is no longer available for purchase.

> † As of May 5, 2026, new AI Expanded Access subscriptions are not eligible to receive credits for Google Flow.

# Usage Limits

## Reset Cadence

AI usage limits reset either **daily** or **monthly**, depending on the feature.

- **Daily limits** reset at **12:00 a.m. PT each day**
- **Monthly limits** reset at **12:00 a.m. PT on the first day of each month**
- Limits:
  - **Do not roll over**
  - **Cannot be shared** with other users

## Actions That Count Against AI Usage Limits

Examples of usage that consumes limits include:

- Asking AI to perform work, such as:
  - Generating videos in **Google Flow** or **Google Vids**
  - Generating images with **Nano Banana** in Slides or Vids
  - Using the **AI function in Google Sheets**
  - Generating **audio overviews** in Gemini, NotebookLM, and Drive
- Creating and running flows in **Workspace Studio**

# Business / Enterprise: Advanced AI Feature Limits

The comparison covers:

- **Business Starter**
- **Business Standard / Business Plus**
- **Enterprise Standard / Enterprise Plus**
- **AI Expanded Access**
- **AI Ultra Access**

## Add-on Descriptions

### AI Expanded Access

Higher access to advanced AI capabilities in Workspace, including:

- Workspace Studio
- Advanced image generation
- Advanced video generation
- Other advanced AI capabilities

### AI Ultra Access

Highest access tier for advanced AI capabilities, intended for specialists working on ambitious creative and research projects.

> **Note:** AI Ultra Access is no longer available for purchase.
