A concise summary of the key information from the official FAQ, preserving all important facts, quotes, and details.

## 1. What is the Gemini App?

> The Gemini app includes:
> - The Gemini web app at gemini.google.com
> - Gemini mobile app on Android and iOS
> - Gemini in Chrome
> - Gemini on Mac

Also referred to as "Gemini Apps," "Gemini app," or simply "Gemini." Using it with a work or school account may involve different data handling terms.

---

## 2. Who Can Use the Gemini App?

The Gemini app is available as a **core service with most editions of Google Workspace**.

---

## 3. Data Protection & Security

Enterprise-grade protections apply when users have a **Google Workspace with Gemini license**:

- **No training or human review**: Submissions are not used to train models and are never reviewed by humans. (Unlicensed users have different handling.)
- **Data stays within your organization**: Prompts and generated content are stored alongside your Workspace content and not shared outside.
- **Existing Workspace protections apply automatically**: Data-region policies, Data Loss Prevention, etc.
- **No cross-customer use**: Your content is never used for model training outside your domain without permission.

> **Quote**: *“When Google Workspace commercial customers adopt Google Workspace with Gemini, they get the same robust data protection and security standards that come with all Google Workspace services, with specific protections for businesses, education, and public-sector customers.”*

More details: [Generative AI in Google Workspace Privacy Hub](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/generative-ai-in-google-workspace-privacy-hub)

---

## 4. Protection Against Generative AI Security Threats

- Gemini analyzes email and document content for suspicious URLs and malicious prompts before summarizing.
- When a threat is identified, the content is **excluded from the response** and the user is notified.

> **Quote**: *“When users ask Gemini to summarize content such as email messages or documents, Gemini analyzes the content for threats before taking action. When Gemini identifies a threat, it notifies the user and excludes that content from its response.”*

Learn more: [Protecting gen AI users from threats](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/security/how-google-helps-protect-gemini-users-from-malicious-content-and-prompt-injections)

## 7. Age Restriction

- Users must be **18 years or older** to use the add-on.
- Admins are responsible for assigning the add-on only to eligible users.

## 8. HIPAA Compliance

- **Google Workspace with Gemini can support HIPAA workloads**.
- Updated documents: [HIPAA Included Functionality](https://workspace.google.com/terms/2015/1/hipaa_functionality/) and [Implementation Guide](https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/gsuite_cloud_identity_hipaa_implementation_guide.pdf).
- The **Gemini app** also supports HIPAA, but some features may be blocked for customers who have signed a HIPAA Business Associate Amendment (BAA).

## 10. Client-Side Encryption Incompatibility

- Google Workspace with Gemini **cannot act on email and files encrypted with customer-controlled keys** (client-side encryption).

> **Quote**: *“No. Google Workspace with Gemini is unable to act on email and files that are encrypted with customer-controlled keys.”*

## 11. Availability & Usage Limitations

### Resource Usage and Performance
- Every generative AI request consumes significant data center resources.
- Google has planned for increased demand, but **transformative adoption may cause slower responses or wait times**.
- Usage limits are **monitored and may change** to manage capacity.

> **Quote**: *“Each time you submit a generative AI request, it starts a process that uses a tremendous amount of data center resources. … Consequently there may be times when your use and experimentation may cause Google Workspace with Gemini to be slower or delays in wait time.”*

If you experience slow response times, contact your account representative or Google Support.
