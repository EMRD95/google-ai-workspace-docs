## Overview

Gemini in Gmail lets eligible users use AI directly inside Gmail through the **Ask Gemini** side panel. It can help summarize emails, draft and suggest replies, search prior emails, reference Google Drive files, interact with Google Calendar, generate images, and use Gems for repetitive tasks.

---

## Feature Availability

- Requires an eligible **Google Workspace** or **Google AI** plan.
- Supported languages vary by feature.
- Google notes a change for some subscribers:

> “We're working to make it easier to use AI directly in Gmail. As such, Google AI Pro and Google AI Ultra subscribers in the US may no longer see Ask Gemini in Gmail or be able to open the Gemini in Gmail side panel. Powerful AI experiences in Gmail, such as AI Overviews and ‘Help me write,’ continue to be available directly in the product.”

## What Gemini in Gmail Can Do

With Gemini in Gmail, you can:

- Summarize an email thread.
- Suggest responses to an email thread.
- Draft an email.
- Find information from previous emails.
- Find information from your Google Drive files.
- Get information about Google Calendar events.
- Create events in Google Calendar.
- Generate images.
- Use Gems for repetitive or specialized tasks.

---

## Get Started with Gemini in Gmail

1. On your computer, open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/).
2. At the top right, click **Ask Gemini**.
3. In the side panel, either:
   - Select a suggested prompt, or
   - Write your own prompt.
4. Press **Enter**.

### Use a Suggested Prompt

1. Select a suggestion from the list.
   - To see more options, select **More suggestions**.
2. At the bottom, in the prompt box, replace the example text with your own information.
3. Press **Enter**.

### Enter Your Own Prompt

1. At the bottom, in the prompt box, enter a prompt.
2. Press **Enter**.

### Clear Gemini History

To remove your history:

1. Click **More options**.
2. Select **Clear history**.

**Important tip:**

> “To avoid losing your history, copy a suggestion into your email.”

You lose your Gemini conversation history when:

- You refresh your browser.
- You close and reopen Gmail.

## Gemini in Gmail Controls and Actions

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Ask Gemini** | Opens Gemini. |
| **More options** | Clears recent Gemini history and asks for more suggestions. |
| **Expand** | Makes Gemini larger. |
| **Collapse** | Returns Gemini to its original size. |
| **Close** | Closes Gemini. |
| **Clear history** | Removes generated text and images not yet inserted into the document/email. |
| **More suggestions** | Shows more Gemini suggestions. |
| **Copy** | Copies a suggestion. |
| **Retry with Google Search** | Searches Google for more information about your question or request. |
| **View more** | Shows more of Gemini’s response. |
| **View less** | Minimizes Gemini’s response. |
| **Good suggestion** | Sends positive feedback about a Gemini response. |
| **Bad suggestion** | Reports an issue with a Gemini response. |
| **Gem** | Lets you chat with or create a Gem to save time on repetitive tasks. |

## Things You Can Ask Gemini in Gmail

### 1. Summarize an Email Thread

Gemini can summarize email conversations and extract key points or action items.

Example prompts:

> “Create a list of action items for me based on this email.”

> “Explain this email to me like I’m 5 years old.”

---

### 2. Suggest Reply Options or Draft Emails

Gemini can draft new emails or replies.

#### To get suggested replies

1. On your computer, open an email thread.
2. Click **Ask Gemini**.
3. At the bottom of the side panel, click **Suggest a reply**.

#### To draft a new email

Ask Gemini to create the draft.

Example prompts:

> “Write an announcement email for the launch of Project Clover.”

> “Create a thank you note to a colleague for their work on Project Clover.”

---

### 3. Find Information from Previous Emails

Gemini can retrieve information from emails in your inbox, including reservations, flights, packages, and similar messages.

Example prompts:

> “When is my package arriving?”

> “What time is my next flight?”

---

### 4. Search for Certain Types of Emails

Gemini can search Gmail for messages based on criteria such as unread status, date range, or sender. Results load as a Gmail search.

Example prompts:

> “Show my unread emails.”

> “Emails from [person] sent last week.”

> “Show unread emails from this week.”

---

### 5. Find Information from Google Drive Files

Gemini can reference files and documents stored in Google Drive.

Example prompts:

> “What are the main points of the weekly team meeting notes from my documents?”

> “Summarize my document for Project Clover.”

**Tip:** To check which files Gemini used in its response, click **Sources**.
