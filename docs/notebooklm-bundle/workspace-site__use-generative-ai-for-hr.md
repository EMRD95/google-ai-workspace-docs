## How AI tools in Google Workspace benefit human resources

HR teams can use Gemini to save time on a variety of tasks, including recruiting efforts, onboarding new hires, updating training materials for existing staff, drafting company policies and workforce planning.

## Save time on recruiting

Prompt Gemini's Help me write to create job descriptions for open roles within your organisation. Gemini can also help streamline your hiring process by quickly analysing CVs, providing summaries to help you pinpoint qualified candidates and keep track of your interview pipeline.

## Automate the onboarding process

Use Gemini to create onboarding checklists to get new hires up to speed quickly. Use [Gemini in Sheets](https://workspace.google.com/intl/en_uk/resources/spreadsheet-ai/) to create a first draft of training tasks, then refine the list accordingly.

## Customise training

Use Gemini to update training materials or customise training documentation for specific roles, making them more engaging and relevant. In addition to updating training materials, Gemini can help you with original images to illustrate information.
