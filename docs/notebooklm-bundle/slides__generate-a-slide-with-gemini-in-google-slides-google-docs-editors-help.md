# Generate a Slide with Gemini in Google Slides — Summary

**Source:** Google Docs Editors Help  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/docs/answer/16961475?hl=en

## Overview

Gemini in Google Slides lets eligible users **generate and edit slides using prompts**. It can help build presentations, reuse the visual style of existing presentations, adjust slide images/content, and reference Drive files to create or refine slide content.

---

## Feature Availability

Key requirements and limitations:

- Requires an **eligible Google Workspace or Google AI plan**.
- Available in **Google Workspace Experiments**, a trusted tester program for users on personal accounts.
- Currently available:
  - **Desktop only**
  - **English only**

> “With Gemini in Google Slides, you can generate and edit slides with a prompt. It helps you build your presentation and lets you adjust the images and content on the slide at any time.”

---

## Generate a Slide with Gemini

You can create a new slide from scratch or reuse the visual style of an existing presentation.

### Steps

1. On your computer, open a presentation in [Google Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation).
2. At the top right, click **Ask Gemini**.
3. In the prompt bar, enter a prompt describing the slide you want to create.
4. Optional:
   - To match another presentation’s visual style:
     1. Click **Match a presentation style** → **Add from Drive**.
     2. Select a presentation.
     3. Click **Add**.
   - To start from scratch:
     - Click **More options** → **Clear history**.
5. When the preview appears:
   - To insert the generated slide as a new slide:
     - Beside **Replace**, click the **Down arrow** → **Insert**.
   - To replace the selected slide:
     - Click **Replace**.

### Important Tips

- **You can only generate one slide at a time.**
- Generated slides can be edited after creation.

## Edit Slides with Gemini

Gemini can directly revise the **design or content** of the current slide based on a prompt.

### Example Prompts

> “Simplify this slide with less text and add an image.”

> “Convert to two columns.”

### Steps

1. Open a presentation in [Google Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation).
2. Click **Ask Gemini** in the top-right corner.
3. In the prompt bar, describe the changes you want.
4. When the updated slide preview appears:
   - To insert it as a new slide:
     - Beside **Replace**, click the **Down arrow** → **Insert**.
   - To replace the selected slide:
     - Click **Replace**.
   - If you don’t want to apply the changes:
     - Click **Close** to close the preview.
     - Or click **Ask Gemini** again to refine your prompt or clarify the requested changes.

## Reference Other Files to Create Content

Gemini can use specific files from Drive to write, edit, summarize, or refine slide content.

### Example Prompt

> “Create a summary slide based on @ProjectNotes.”

### Steps

1. In the side panel, click **Add sources** → **Add from Drive**.
   - Alternatively, type `@` and search/select a file from the menu.
2. Select one or more files.
3. Click **Add**.
4. To review files Gemini used:
   - In the right side panel, click **Sources**.

### Source Search Settings

You can choose where Gemini is allowed to proactively search for information related to your prompt. Google links to more information about **source settings** for Gemini in Workspace.
