## 1. What is the Gemini App?

The Gemini app includes:

- Web app at [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/)
- Mobile apps for Android and iOS
- Gemini in Chrome
- Gemini on Mac

It helps users brainstorm, summarize complex topics, create first drafts of outlines/emails/posts, and more.

> **Note:** “Gemini Apps” are also referred to as “Gemini app” or “Gemini.” When used with a work or school Google Account, different data handling terms may apply.

---

## 2. How to Get the Gemini App

### 2.1 As a Core Service (Enterprise‑grade data protections)

Available with most Google Workspace editions:

- **AI Expanded Access** / **AI Ultra Access**
- Business Starter\*, Standard\*, Plus\*
- Enterprise Starter, Standard, Plus
- Essentials, Enterprise Essentials, Enterprise Essentials Plus
- Frontline Starter, Standard, Plus
- Nonprofits

Users under these editions are subject to the **Google Workspace Terms of Service** – content is **not** human‑reviewed and **not** used for generative AI model training outside the domain without permission.

> \* Business edition users must sign up with a business email address or verify their domain to get the Gemini mobile app.

### 2.2 As an Additional Google Service

Users **without** a qualifying Workspace edition are subject to the **Google Terms of Service** and the **Gemini Apps Privacy Notice**.  
Their chats **may be reviewed by human reviewers** and used to improve Google’s products, services, and machine‑learning technologies.

> **Important:** Tell these users to avoid entering confidential or sensitive information in the Gemini app.
