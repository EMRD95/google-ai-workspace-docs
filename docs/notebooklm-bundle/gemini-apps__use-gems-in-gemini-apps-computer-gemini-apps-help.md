## Overview

Gems let you **create and save customized versions of Gemini** so Gemini Apps respond according to your specific needs, instructions, and optional reference files.

> “You can create and save Gems to customize responses in Gemini Apps to your specific needs.”

Gems can be created and managed in the Gemini web app and used across supported Gemini surfaces.

---

## Requirements

To create and use Gems:

- You must be signed in to Gemini Apps.
- With a **personal Google Account**, you must be:
  - **13 or older**, or
  - the applicable minimum age in your country.
- You must follow Google’s Terms of Service and Prohibited Use Policy.

**Important notice from Google:**

> **Important:** When you create or chat with a Gem, remember that you agreed to Google's Terms of Service, including the Prohibited Use Policy. Be sure not to violate others' rights. Use your discretion before you rely on, publish, or use content that Gemini Apps generate. Users with a work or school account may be subject to different terms.

Work or school accounts may have different terms and controls.

---

## Key Limitations

**Important:**

- Gems **can’t currently be used with Gemini Live**.
- The **Learning coach** premade Gem **doesn’t currently support language learning**.

## Create, Use & Manage Gems

### Create a Gem

**Important:**

> When you create a Gem in the Gemini web app, it will appear in the Gemini mobile app and the Gemini side panel in Google Workspace.

#### Steps

1. On your computer, go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Open Sidebar**.
3. Click **Gems**.
   - If you’ve never made a Gem before, first click **Settings and help** → **Gems**.
4. Click **New Gem**.
5. Enter:
   - a **name**
   - **instructions** for your Gem
6. Optional: under **Knowledge**, click **Add files** to add sources.
7. Click **Save**.

## Optional Ways to Improve a Gem

### Use Gemini to Rewrite Instructions

After typing a few words of instructions, you can use Gemini to make them more comprehensive.

> After a few words, you can click **Use Gemini to re-write instructions** to have Gemini Apps create more comprehensive instructions for most topics. It may take a few seconds for the generated instructions to appear.

---

### Upload Files as Gem Knowledge

You can upload files so the Gem has more context or can reference specific documents in chats.

> You can add files to give your Gem more context or to reference specific docs in your chats with the Gem.

To add files:

1. Add instructions to the Gem.
2. Under **Knowledge**, click **Add files**.

#### File Upload Options

- **Upload from your device:** click **Upload files**.
- **Add from Google Drive:** click **Add from Drive**.
- **Add a NotebookLM notebook:** click **More uploads** → **Notebooks**.

#### Google Drive Requirements

To add files from Google Drive:

- **Keep Activity** must be turned on.
- Google Workspace must be connected to Gemini Apps.
- If Google Workspace isn’t connected, Gemini will prompt you to connect it.

#### Google Drive File Version Behavior

> If you add a file from your Drive, Gemini will use the most recent version of the file. If you make changes to the file, these changes will be reflected in your Gem.

---

### Knowledge File Citations

By default, Gem responses may cite uploaded knowledge files.

To turn citations off:

> If you don’t want the Gem’s responses to include citations to your knowledge files, select **Disable knowledge citations**. This also disables citations for files uploaded in your Gem chats.

Additional tips:

- You may need to scroll down to find the **Knowledge** section.
- You can edit a Gem later to remove files or add more files.

## Preview a Gem

Before saving or finalizing edits, you can test the Gem.

1. In the text box on the right, enter a prompt to preview the Gem.
2. Review the response.
3. Edit the Gem instructions as needed.
4. Click **Submit**.

---

## Use a Gem in a Chat

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Open Sidebar**.
3. Click **Gems**.
   - If you’ve never made a Gem before, first click **Settings and help** → **Gems**.
4. Under **My Gems**, select the Gem you want to use.
5. Enter your question or prompt in the text box.
6. Click **Submit**.

---

## Pin and Unpin Gems

Recently used or created Gems appear near the top of the Gemini Apps menu. You can pin specific Gems to keep them at the top.

### Steps

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. On the left under **Gems**, find the Gem you want to pin or unpin.
3. Next to the Gem, click **More**.
4. Select **Pin** or **Unpin**.
