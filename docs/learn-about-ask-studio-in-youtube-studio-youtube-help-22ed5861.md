# Learn about Ask Studio in YouTube Studio — Summary

**Source:** YouTube Help — https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/16291691  
**Topic:** Ask Studio, an AI assistant inside YouTube Studio for creators.

---

## Overview

**Ask Studio** is an AI creative partner integrated into **YouTube Studio**. It helps creators:

- Summarize comments and audience feedback
- Understand channel and video performance stats
- Brainstorm video ideas, concepts, and outlines
- Get creative feedback on scripts and unpublished videos

> Ask Studio is an AI creative partner integrated into YouTube Studio that helps you summarize comments and feedback from your videos, understand your video channel stats, and brainstorm ideas and outlines for your next videos.

---

## Eligibility & Availability

- Available to **most YouTube creators globally**.
- Available **only on web browsers on a computer**.
- If the **Ask Studio** icon does not appear in the top corner of YouTube Studio, the creator does **not** currently have access.

### Important Disclaimer

> **Note:** Responses provided by Ask Studio do not reflect YouTube’s views. Quality and accuracy of responses may vary. Use discretion when using Ask Studio and do not rely on it for professional advice, including medical, legal, or financial advice.

---

## How to Get Started

1. Sign in to [YouTube Studio](https://studio.youtube.com/) or access [Ask Studio](https://studio.youtube.com/?d=as) directly.
2. In the **top-right corner**, select **Ask Studio**.
3. Choose a suggested prompt or type your own question.

### Ask Studio in Analytics

Ask Studio can also be used from the **Analytics** page.

- Select suggested prompts to explore channel metrics in more detail.

---

## Best Practices for Using Ask Studio

### 1. Begin with a Clear Objective

Decide what you want help with, such as:

- Community insights
- Comment summaries
- Video analytics
- Performance comparisons
- New video ideas

### 2. Ask Specific Questions

More precise prompts produce better responses.

Instead of:

> "tell me about my channel"

Try:

> "What are the common themes in comments on my recent video about \[topic\]?"

Or:

> "How did my \[video title\] perform recently?"

### 3. Add Videos to the Chat

You can add a video by:

- Typing it into the chat
- Clicking the **Add** button in the chat box

### 4. Use Suggested Prompts

Suggested prompts can be used as starting points, such as:

> "Summarize comments”

You can also use the built-in guide:

> “How can Ask Studio help me?”

### 5. Experiment and Refine

If the first response is not what you need:

- Rephrase the prompt
- Ask follow-up questions
- Narrow the scope of your request

## Create and Develop Video Concepts

Ask Studio can help generate, refine, and save video ideas.

## Save an Idea Card

When Ask Studio generates a suggestion worth keeping, you can save it as an **Idea card**.

### Steps to Save an Idea Card

1. In your Ask Studio conversation, find the suggestion you want to save.
2. On the Idea card, click the save icon.
3. To find it later, go to the **Inspiration** tab and select **Saved Ideas**.

### Refining Saved Ideas

- Open any saved card from the **Inspiration** tab.
- This launches a new Ask Studio interaction so you can continue developing the idea.

---

## Get Feedback on Your Videos

Ask Studio can provide creative best practices and suggestions for your content, including unpublished videos.

### Script Feedback

- Copy and paste your script directly into Ask Studio.
- Ask for feedback based on creative best practices.

### Feedback Before Publishing

You can select videos from your channel that are:

- Draft
- Private
- Unlisted

Ask Studio can then provide creative feedback before publication.

### Note on Suggestions

Ask Studio’s recommendations are optional.

> These ideas are suggestions. You can always choose if you want to incorporate feedback into your final video.

---

## Data Collection & Use

When you interact with Ask Studio, YouTube collects data related to use of the feature, including:

- Conversations
- Feedback submitted
- Usage data

This information is used to:

- Develop Ask Studio
- Improve the product experience
- Improve YouTube products, services, and machine-learning technologies

### Conversation Retention

There are two retention-related details in the article:

- Conversations connected with your channel as part of Ask Studio interactions are **deleted automatically after 45 days**.
- Ask Studio conversation history is saved in recent conversations for **30 days** before automatic deletion.

### Human Review

To improve quality, human reviewers may read and process conversations.

YouTube says it protects privacy by:

- Disconnecting conversations from your Google Account before reviewers see them
- Using automated tools to recognize and remove personal information, such as:
  - Email addresses
  - Phone numbers
