# YouTube Help: Conversational AI Tool When Watching Videos — Summary

**Source:** https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/14110396  
**Topic:** YouTube’s conversational AI tool for asking questions and getting suggestions while watching videos.

---

## Overview

YouTube’s conversational AI tool lets users interact with AI while watching select videos. Users can:

- Ask questions about the video they’re watching.
- Choose suggested prompts, such as **“recommend related content.”**
- Get answers or recommendations without leaving the video.

The feature uses generative AI and large language models (LLMs), and its availability depends on language, region, device, account type, and video eligibility.

---

## Key Excerpts

> “While you’re watching a video on YouTube, the conversational AI tool lets you interact with AI to learn more about the content.”

> “For example, you can ask questions about the video you’re watching or choose a suggested prompt like ‘recommend related content.’”

> “AI-generated responses do not reflect the views of YouTube. Quality and accuracy may vary.”

> “The conversational AI tool is for informational purposes only. Don’t rely on AI-generated responses as medical, legal, financial, or other professional advice.”

> “Conversations connected with your Google Account will be deleted automatically after 45 days.”

> “Please don’t give confidential info, or anything you wouldn’t want a reviewer to see, in your conversations.”

> “No, your conversations aren’t being used to show you ads. If this changes, we will clearly communicate the change to you.”

## Supported Devices and Platforms

The tool is available on:

- Android
- iOS
- Web browsers on computers
- Smart TVs
- Game consoles
- Streaming devices, where supported

It appears on the **watch page** for select videos.

---

## Eligibility

The conversational AI tool is available to:

- Signed-in users
- Users who meet the minimum age requirement:
  - **13+**, or
  - the applicable age in the user’s country
- Users in supported countries and languages

The tool is **not available** for:

- Supervised kid accounts
- The YouTube Kids app

---

## How to Use the Tool on Computer / Mobile

To get started:

1. Tap **Ask** below the video.
2. Select one of the suggested prompts, or type your own question.

**Note:** Responses are AI-generated and may vary in quality or accuracy.
