Generative AI is evolving at an incredible pace and it seems like there’s something new to learn every day. The good news is: [You don’t have to be an engineer to write great Gen AI prompts](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-3-rethinking-prompt-engineering?e=48754805). That's why we created our *Beyond the prompt* blog series. Across the posts, we share hands-on, practical tips, in-depth use cases, and examples from Googlers and customers. Use this actionable advice to help you get the best out of generative AI in your own work.

### Here’s what we’ve covered so far:

**1. Effective prompt writing:** A deep dive into [the art of writing effective prompts](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/welcome-to-beyond-the-prompt?e=48754805). We broke down the 4 key elements of a good prompt — persona, task, context, and format — and showed you how to craft prompts that get results.

**2. Collaboration tips for Gemini:** [Four tips to boost your collaboration with Gemini](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-4-tips-boost-collaboration-gemini?e=48754805) emphasized the importance of using natural language, being specific and iterative, keeping prompts concise, and engaging in a conversation with [gemini.google.com](http://gemini.google.com).

**3. Use cases by role:**

- For **sales professionals**, we explored how [Gemini can become your research assistant](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-4-using-gemini-workspace-your-sales-research-assistant), helping you gather insights and close deals faster.
- For **marketers**, we shared how [marketers can conquer the blank page](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-5-marketers-conquer-blank-page) using Gemini for Google Workspace to generate ideas and create compelling content.
- For **customer service roles**, we looked at how Gemini can help [standardize and personalize customer service responses](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-6-personalize-customer-service-responses).
- And for **entrepreneurs**, we covered [how entrepreneurs can run competitive analysis with Gemini for Workspace](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-8-how-entrepreneurs-can-run-competitive-analysis-gemini-workspace).

**4. Inside Google:** How Googlers are using Gemini to transform their day-to-day work: Director of Product Management, Serge Lachapelle, shared [his "lightbulb" moments with generative AI](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-7-googler-serge-lachapelle-shares-his-lightbulb-generative-ai-moments) and how it's transformed his workflow. And we heard from Tony Majewski, Americas growth and solutions lead in the Workspace sales team, who revealed how he [uses Gemini to save 8-10 hours a week](https://workspace.google.com/blog/ai-and-machine-learning/beyond-prompt-part-9-googler-shares-how-he-saves-time-and-reduces-burnout-using-gemini-workspace).

In the weeks and months ahead, we’ll take a closer look at what our customers are accomplishing with generative AI, how executives can tap into the power of Gemini, and much more. In the meantime, consider taking a deep dive into prompting by downloading our [Prompting guide 101 ebook](https://inthecloud.withgoogle.com/gemini-for-google-workspace-prompt-guide/dl-cd.html).
