# Use Gemini — Android Help Summary

**Source:** Google Android Help — “Use Gemini”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/android/answer/15729107

## Key Excerpts

> You can use Gemini to:
>
> - Learn in new ways through text, voice, images, and your camera.
> - Write thank you notes, plan events, and more.
> - Connect with Google apps, like Gmail, Maps, and YouTube.
> - Create lists, set up reminders, manage phone settings, and create YouTube Music playlists.

> The Gemini mobile app may not be available for all languages, devices, accounts, or locations.

> You can conveniently get quick answers, contextual help, and assistance from Gemini without leaving the screen you were on.

> When you say “Hey Google” or activate Gemini by touch, like long pressing the power button, Gemini will start listening to you even if you have another app open.

## Chat with Gemini Live

Gemini Live supports real-time voice conversations with Gemini.

### Gemini Live Can Help You

- Start a conversation with **real-time spoken responses**
- Have more natural voice conversations that **ebb and flow at your own pace**
- Brainstorm ideas
- Rehearse for interviews
- Handle many other conversational tasks

### How to Start Gemini Live

1. On your Android device, open **Gemini**.
   - If available, you can also say: **“Hey Google.”**
2. At the bottom right, tap **Live**.
   - If this is your first time, follow the on-screen instructions.
3. Start talking.

### Microphone Control Tip

To turn off the microphone:

- Tap **Hold**
- Tap **End**
- Or say: **“stop”**

## Use Gemini as Your Built-in AI Assistant

Gemini can work as an integrated assistant on Android, allowing you to get help **without leaving your current screen**.

### Ways to Activate Gemini

You can activate Gemini by:

- Saying **“Hey Google”**
- Using a touch shortcut, such as **long pressing the power button**

Once activated, Gemini starts listening even if another app is currently open.

### Built-in Assistant Tips

- You can generate custom images and **drag and drop them directly into apps**, such as Gmail.
- In some apps, opening the on-screen overlay can help you find key information in content, such as **YouTube videos**.

## Use Gemini Extensions

Gemini extensions let Gemini interact with supported apps and services.

### Supported / Mentioned Apps and Services

Gemini can access or work with apps such as:

- Gmail
- Maps
- Tasks
- YouTube
- YouTube Music
- Phone settings and other supported services

### What Extensions Enable

With extensions, Gemini can:

- Create lists
- Set reminders
- Manage phone settings
- Create YouTube Music playlists matching a requested mood or “vibe”
- Use Google apps to complete relevant tasks

### How to Use Gemini Extensions

1. On your Android device, open **Gemini**.
   - If available, you can also say: **“Hey Google.”**
2. Enter your question or prompt.
3. Tap **Submit**.
   - If an extension is available, the Gemini app automatically uses it.
