To help your teams upskill and get more from their AI tools, we’re introducing [AI Boost Bootcamp](https://www.skills.google/course_templates/1674), a new training series available on [Google Skills](http://skills.google/) — our new home for AI learning and more. The training is designed to:

- Accelerate AI and agentic work transformation by training your teams in under 50 minutes.
- Make it easier to track your progress on Google Skills.

### **Closing the AI skills gap**

Buying AI tools is easy; integrating them into your teams’ daily work to fuel transformation is the real hurdle. According to a [recent Deloitte study](https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/what-we-do/capabilities/applied-artificial-intelligence/content/state-of-ai-in-the-enterprise.html), closing the AI skills gap is now a top priority, with 53% of companies investing in AI fluency and 48% on upskilling workers for applying AI in their roles.

We designed the AI Boost Bootcamp series to help close that gap, allowing teams to scale AI learning and adoption across their entire organizations. We’re kicking off the series with a course that’s designed for knowledge workers: [**How to Use Gemini at Work 101**](https://www.skills.google/course_templates/1674). Here’s what is covers:

- Overview and how to find Gemini across Google's tools for work
- Project management and planning with Gemini and NotebookLM
- Presentations and charts with Gemini in Slides, Sheets, and more
- Aligning stakeholders across meetings with AI notetaking, scheduling, and more
- Communicating with speed and precision using *Help Me Write* in Gmail, and Gemini Gems for repetitive tasks
- Getting started with AI agents and agentic work with Gemini Enterprise

### **How we designed the course**

AI Boost Bootcamp is based on Google’s own proven, internal training and builds on [AI Boost Bites](https://cloud.google.com/transform/introducing-ai-boost-bites-from-google?e=48754805), which offers quick, actionable tips on AI productivity. We heard from customers that they’re looking for AI best practices bundled as a concise, practical learning journey. AI Boost Bootcamp covers the most important knowledge you need to make the most of Google’s AI tools for work, such as Google Workspace with Gemini, NotebookLM, the Gemini app, and Gemini Enterprise.

### **Track progress and earn credentials**

For a structured learning experience, every learner can track their progress and test their knowledge on [**Google Skills**](https://www.skills.google/course_templates/1674). When they’ve successfully completed the series, they’ll earn an official skill badge to share on social media to showcase their proficiency.
