# Use AI to Search & Ask About Your Tabs — Google Chrome Help

**Source:** https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/17025061  
**Feature:** AI Mode side-by-side in Google Chrome

---

## Overview

Chrome’s **AI Mode side-by-side** lets you browse while asking AI questions about your **open tabs** or **files** from either:

- A **side panel**
- A **full tab view**

You can start AI Mode from the **New Tab page**, add context such as recent tabs, images, or files, and use tools like **Canvas**, **Create Images**, and **Deep Search**.

---

## Key Availability & Requirements

> **Important:** AI Mode in Chrome isn't available to everyone or on every device at this time. This feature is gradually coming to people outside the US and to non-English languages, so it might not be available to you right now.

To use AI Mode in Chrome, you must be:

- **Signed in to Chrome**
- **Not browsing in Incognito mode**

---

## Start a Thread from the New Tab Page

1. Open **Chrome** on your computer.
2. Open a **New Tab** page.
3. On the right side of the search box, select **AI Mode**.
   - Or use the keyboard shortcut: **Tab** + **Enter**
4. On the left side of the search box, select the add/context icon. You can:
   - Add recent tabs
   - Upload an image or file
   - Apply tools, such as:
     - **Canvas**
     - **Create Images**
     - **Deep Search**
   - Choose a model for your question
5. To select from previous conversations, choose **View your AI Mode history**.
6. Enter a question to be taken to the **AI Mode results page**.

### Tips

- To expand the AI Mode panel into a full tab, **close the link you opened**.
- When you select a link from a full-page search, it automatically opens in the panel and adds that tab to your follow-up question.

---

## Add Context and Tools in AI Mode

While in the AI Mode panel:

1. Below the **“Ask anything”** text box, select the add/context icon.
2. From the menu, you can choose:

### Available Context Options

- **Find most recent tabs to add to your search:** Find all tabs currently open in the window.
- **Show more:** Find more tabs to add to your search.
- **Add images:** Add images to your search.
- **Add file:** Attach files to your search.

### Available AI Tools

- **Create images:** Describe the image you want to create.
- **Deep search:** Research anything.
- **Canvas:** Build visual content.

---

## AI Mode Panel Toolbar Options

From the toolbar of the AI Mode panel, you can:

- **Search history or thread:** Select **AI Mode history**
- **Create a new thread:** Select **Create**

### Limitations

> Internal Chrome URLs, Gemini chats, or certain workspaces subject to controls will not work with AI Mode side-by-side.

## Data Privacy for AI Mode Side-by-Side

Google describes several privacy and storage behaviors for AI Mode side-by-side.

### Temporary Data

Temporary data such as:

- Screenshots
- Lens selections
- PDF captures

stays available only during your session and **won’t be saved**.

### Saved Tab Information

To help users revisit conversations later:

- The **titles and URLs** of tabs added to a thread are saved for a specific number of months.
- These sources can still be referenced even if:
  - The original tabs are closed
  - Temporary screenshots have expired

### Confidentiality of Raw Data

> All raw data from both public and private URLs are treated with strict confidentiality.

This raw data is **not used for**:

- Human review
- Personalizing the experience
- Ad targeting
- Training AI models
