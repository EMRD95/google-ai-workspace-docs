## What AI Mode Can Do

AI Mode in Google Search can help with time-consuming local tasks by gathering information from the web, filling out forms, or calling businesses directly when details are not available online.

### Supported use cases include:

- Finding local appointments, such as:
  - Haircuts
  - Auto repair
- Researching home services, such as:
  - Electricians
  - Tree cutting
  - Exterior cleaning
- Checking:
  - Real-time pricing
  - Shopping inventory
  - Dining reservations

AI Mode is designed to help users get specific details such as service costs, appointment availability, local inventory, and reservation options.

## How to Use AI Mode on a Computer

### 1. Open AI Mode

Go to Google Search and select **AI Mode**, or visit:

> [google.com/ai](https://google.com/ai)

---

### 2. Enter a Request

Type your question or task in the **Ask anything** bar near the bottom of the screen.

Example prompts from the article:

> “Deep cleaning appointment for an apartment move out.”

> “Reservation for 6 on Friday with vegetarian options”

---

### 3. Select “Check for me”

Choose **“Check for me”** to let AI Mode help complete the request.

---

### 4. Answer Follow-Up Questions if Needed

AI Mode may ask clarifying questions to better understand the request.

> **Note:** If your initial request has enough detail, this step will be skipped automatically.

---

### 5. Google May Call Businesses on Your Behalf

If a business has limited information online, Google may call the business to gather the details you requested.

Important call-related details:

- Only the details you shared and confirmed are provided to businesses.
- Calls are used to get information not available online, such as:
  - Earliest appointment times
  - Current inventory
  - Pricing or availability

If you do **not** want Google to call businesses:

> type “don’t call” into the prompt box and then select **Enter**.

---

### 6. Review the Request

Before AI Mode proceeds:

- Review the request details.
- Choose whether to receive an email notification.
- If calls are made, the email will notify you when results are ready.

---

### 7. Confirm the Request

Select:

> **Looks good**

This allows AI Mode to proceed.

---

### 8. View Results

Timing depends on the type of request:

- **Web results:** usually take a few minutes
- **Call results:** can take up to **30 minutes**

You can leave AI Mode and return later.

A **blue dot** appears on the thread when results are ready in AI Mode history.

---

### 9. Email Notifications

If you opted into email notifications and calls were made, you’ll receive an email when your results are ready.

---

### 10. Stop the Request

To end all work on a request, select:

> **Stop**

---

## Important Behavior Details

> **Important:**
>
> - AI Mode is designed to provide the most accurate and up-to-date information. If a business does not have its information listed online, such as the earliest available appointment or current inventory in stock, Google will make a call to get those answers for you.
> - AI Mode can process multiple requests simultaneously. If you provide unrelated details during an active search, AI Mode will suggest starting a new thread so each task is handled independently.
> - Google only calls for the specific information you've requested, keeping interactions brief, professional, and focused. Business owners can learn more about managing [these automated calls](https://support.google.com/business/answer/16190256).
