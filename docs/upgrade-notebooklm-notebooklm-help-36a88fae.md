# Upgrade NotebookLM — Comprehensive Summary

Source: [NotebookLM Help: Upgrade NotebookLM](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16206866?hl=en)

## Overview

NotebookLM can be upgraded for **higher usage limits** and **premium features** through:

- [Google AI Plans](https://one.google.com/about/ai-premium/)
- [Google Cloud / NotebookLM for Enterprise](https://cloud.google.com/resources/notebooklm-enterprise?sjid=4840678451071865222-NC&hl=en)
- Qualifying [Google Workspace plans](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16337734)

---

## Key Excerpts

> You can upgrade NotebookLM to get additional capabilities, including higher limits and more features through Google AI Plans, Google Cloud, or a qualifying Google Workspace plan.

> Your data is protected, and is not used to train NotebookLM unless you provide feedback.

> Google Workspace or Google Workspace for Education users. Your uploads, queries and the model's responses in NotebookLM will not be reviewed by human reviewers even when you provide thumbs up or down feedback, and will not be used to train AI models.

> Sharing a notebook does not change the source limit for any collaborator.

> Daily quotas are reset after 24 hours; monthly quotas are reset after 30 days.

> Some users may see a report, flashcards, infographic, slide deck, audio overview, or a video overview created automatically, when sources are first added to a notebook. These are generated once and do not contribute to limits.

> To confirm if you're using NotebookLM with upgraded plans, check to see if you have a distinct badge next to your user profile image in the user interface.

# Google AI Plans: NotebookLM Tiers

NotebookLM is available in several tiers:

1. **NotebookLM Standard**
2. **NotebookLM in Plus**
3. **NotebookLM in Pro**
4. **NotebookLM in Ultra — 20 TB Plan**
5. **NotebookLM in Ultra — 30 TB Plan**

---

## How to Upgrade

| Tier | How to Upgrade |
|---|---|
| **NotebookLM Standard** | Sign up free using a Gmail account, or use a qualifying Workspace / Workspace for Education license. |
| **NotebookLM in Plus** | Sign up for a [Google AI Plus](https://one.google.com/ai?utm_source=notebooklm&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=upgrade_page) plan. |
| **NotebookLM in Pro** | Sign up for a [Google AI Pro](https://one.google.com/ai?utm_source=notebooklm&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=upgrade_page) plan, or use a qualifying Workspace / Workspace for Education license. |
| **NotebookLM in Ultra** | Sign up for a [Google AI Ultra](https://one.google.com/ai?utm_source=notebooklm&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=upgrade_page) plan, or use a qualifying Workspace / Workspace for Education license. |

## Supported Countries & Regions

### Standard

NotebookLM is available in browser to users above the age of consent, or the applicable age in their country, in [supported countries & territories](https://support.google.com/notebooklm/answer/16164461#notebooklmcountries).

Available in browsers with Plus access where [Google AI Plus is available](https://support.google.com/googleone/answer/16548195?hl=en#zippy=).
