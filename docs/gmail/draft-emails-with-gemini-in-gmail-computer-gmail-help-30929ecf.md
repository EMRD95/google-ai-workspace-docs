# Draft Emails with Gemini in Gmail — Summary

**Source:** [Gmail Help: Draft emails with Gemini in Gmail](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/13955415?hl=en)  
**Platform covered:** Computer

---

## Overview

In Gmail, Gemini’s **“Help me write”** feature can help you draft or revise emails directly in the compose window.

You can use it to:

- **Generate a new email draft**, such as:
  - A birthday invitation
  - An introduction to a potential business contact
- **Refine existing text** for tone and clarity, such as turning a rough outline into a formal email.

---

## Requirements

You can use **“Help me write”** with:

- **An eligible Google Workspace or Google AI plan**
  - Available globally in multiple supported languages.
- **A personal Google Account**
  - Available in supported languages.
  - Restricted to the **US only**.

> Learn more: [Supported languages for Google Workspace with Gemini](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/14925782#zippy=%2Cgemini-summary-cards)

---

## How to Draft an Email with “Help me write”

1. On your computer, open [Gmail](https://mail.google.com/).
2. At the top left, click **Compose**.
   - Alternatively, you can **reply to an email** or **open an existing draft**.
3. In the compose window:
   - Enter your message, **or**
   - Leave the field blank.
4. At the bottom, in the prompt bar, enter your prompt.
   - If the prompt bar is not visible, click **Help me write**.
5. Click **Create**.

---

## How to Refine Your Draft

To polish or revise a draft, you can either enter a new instruction in the prompt bar or use preset refinement options.

### Example refinement prompts

> "Make it more professional."

> "Mention the Friday deadline."

### Built-in refinement options

Use these options in the prompt bar:

- **Formalize**
- **Friendly**
- **Shorten**

### Switching between drafts

**Tip:** To switch between different generated drafts, use:

- **Undo**
- **Redo**

These buttons appear at the right side of the prompt bar.

---

## How to Improve Gemini Draft Results

For the most relevant and helpful drafts, make prompts **specific and detailed**.

| What to include in your prompt | Example |
|---|---|
| Mention the person or group you are writing to. | “Write an apology to **my child's teacher** for their absence.” |
| Clarify the subject and required action. | “ **Decline the meeting** on Thursday.” |
| Use tone words like “formal,” “casual,” or other adjectives. | “Write a **formal** invitation to a business partner.” |
| To improve factual accuracy, mention a specific email or file. | “Write a summary of **the Q4 earnings email** I received last week.” |

## Personalization: How “Help me write” Uses Your Data

**Important:** To have “Help me write” match your tone and writing style, your drafts must be in **English**.

When you use **“Help me write,”** Gemini may proactively use relevant information from:

- Other emails
- Google Drive files

Examples of details it may reference include:

- Flight times
- Soccer schedules
- Hotel booking codes

This allows Gemini to draft a personalized response that includes relevant details and better matches your tone and style **without requiring explicit instructions**.

Google states that this information is only used to improve drafts when you use **“Help me write.”**

### View sources used by Gemini

**Tip:** To see which emails and documents Gemini used to personalize a response, click **Sources**.

## Giving Feedback About a Draft

Google Workspace with Gemini is still learning and may not always support your request correctly.

If a generated draft is inaccurate or appears unsafe, you can submit feedback.

### Steps to submit feedback

1. Below the generated text, click:
   - **Good suggestion**, or
   - **Bad suggestion**
2. Enter your feedback.
3. Click **Submit**.

### Feedback with screenshots

To send feedback with screenshots:

1. Click **Help** in the top right of Gmail.
2. Select **Send feedback to Google**.

Learn more: [Send feedback in Gmail](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/9212652)

### Reporting legal issues

To report a legal issue, [create a request](https://support.google.com/legal/answer/3110420).

## Important Notes About Gemini Suggestions

Google provides several cautions about Gemini-generated suggestions:

- Gemini feature suggestions **don’t represent Google’s views** and should not be attributed to Google.
- Do **not** rely on Gemini features as:
  - Medical advice
  - Legal advice
  - Financial advice
  - Other professional advice
- Gemini features may suggest **inaccurate or inappropriate information**.
- User feedback helps make Gemini more helpful and safe.
- Enterprise end users can submit feedback about their experience.
  - They are informed before submitting that feedback data should not contain **personal, sensitive, or confidential information**.
