## Business / Enterprise Editions

### Features Available Today

#### 1. Google Workspace with Gemini (included in plans)
Core AI features embedded directly in Workspace apps:

- **Gemini side panel** in Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Drive, Chat  
  ↳ AI assistance in the flow of work.
- **Help me write** in Gmail and Docs  
  ↳ Quickly compose professional emails and documents.
- **Take notes for me** in Meet  
  ↳ Automatic meeting notes capture.
- **Vids** – Create & collaborate on videos for storytelling at work.
- **Gemini app** – Chat with your AI assistant on laptop/mobile.  
  ↳ Use *Gems* to build a team of AI experts for repeatable or complex tasks (coding, deep research, data analysis).
- **NotebookLM** – Upload sources → get instant insights, Audio Overviews, Mind Maps, Infographics.  
  ↳ Enhanced with notebook customization, shared notebooks, usage analytics, increased outputs.
- **Workspace Studio** – Automate work from simple tasks to complex processes, no coding required.
- **Help me visualize in Slides** – Create infographics, images, and slides with *Nano Banana Pro*.
- **Google Flow** – Generate high-quality, cinematic video quickly and cost-effectively.
- **Gemini in AppSheet**  
  – *Gemini for App Creation*: build apps by describing business processes in natural language.  
  – *Gemini in AppSheet Solutions*: add AI tasks to extract/categorize information from images and documents.

#### 2. AI Expanded Access (add-on)
> “Get higher access to advanced AI capabilities in Workspace to scale your impact.”

- Supplement existing plan with higher access to the Gemini app, NotebookLM, advanced image/video generation, and more.  
[Learn more about AI Expanded Access](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/workspace-with-gemini/ai-expanded-access)

### Features Available Today

#### 1. Gemini app
- **Most capable Google AI models** widely available – superior reasoning, instruction following, coding, creative collaboration.
- **Natural exploration** – brainstorm, refine thinking, discover perspectives by chatting.
- **Deep research** – explore complex topics, get a comprehensive, easy-to-read report.
- **Seamless workflow integration** – bring insights and creations directly into Workspace apps.
- **Handles highly complex tasks** – inputs can be text, images, code.
- **Enterprise-grade data protection** – your submissions aren't used to train models and are not reviewed by humans. [Learn more](https://knowledge.workspace.google.com/admin/generative-ai/gemini-app/turn-the-gemini-app-on-or-off#gemini_editions).
- **Expanded language support** – available in [45+ languages](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/14517446#languages).
- Try **[sample prompts](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13275745#advanced_examples)**.

#### 2. NotebookLM
NotebookLM grounds AI responses in the information you provide.
> “supercharges productivity and collaboration through AI that's grounded in the information you provide”

Depending on your edition you can:
- Get 5× more Audio Overviews, queries, notebooks, and sources per notebook.
- Customize style and length of notebooks.
- Create shared notebooks for teams + usage analytics.
- All sources, queries, responses stay within your organization.
