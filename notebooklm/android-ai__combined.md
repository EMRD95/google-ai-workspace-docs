# Get Started with the Gemini Mobile App — Android Summary

**Source:** Google Android Help — “Get started with the Gemini mobile app”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/android/answer/14554984

---

## Overview

The **Gemini mobile app** lets users chat with Gemini using **text, voice, images, and camera input** to get help with tasks like learning, writing, planning, and more. Gemini can also work with **connected apps** such as **Google Workspace** and **YouTube Music**.

Your Gemini mobile app may be:

- **On Android:** The Gemini app, including as your mobile assistant.
- **On iOS:** The Gemini app.

> **Important:**  
> The Gemini mobile app may not be available for all languages, devices, accounts, or locations.

> **Important:**  
> If you have access to Gemini Apps with a work or school account, some Gemini mobile app features aren’t available. This includes features that are powered by Google Assistant, such quick voice action features.

You can also manage activations or remove Gemini through Google’s guide to managing or deleting the Gemini app.

---

## Gemini as Your Primary Mobile Assistant

On Android, if the **Google app** is set as your device’s **default assist app**, you can choose **Gemini** as your mobile assistant.

When Gemini is your primary assistant:

- Gemini responds to assistant activations on your phone or tablet.
- Other Google Assistant built-in devices — such as **Smart Displays, smart speakers, TVs, cars, and Pixel Tablets** — continue to be directly powered by **Google Assistant**.

> **Note:**  
> “Hey Google” and some features previously available in Google Assistant are not available in all languages and countries, or for supervised accounts.

---

## Requirements

To use Gemini mobile app, you need:

- A **personal Google Account** or a **work/school Google Account** with access to Gemini Apps.
  - Work/school users should check whether their plan or license includes Gemini Apps access.
  - Family Link managers can turn off Gemini Apps access for users under 13 or the applicable age in their country.
- You must be **signed in** to your Google Account.
- An **eligible device** in an available **language, country, or territory**.

> **Important:**  
> To use Gemini as your mobile assistant, make sure the Google app is set as your device's default assist app.

---

## Check Whether Google App Is the Default Assist App

1. On your Android device, open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Apps** → **Default apps**.
3. Check whether the app listed under **Digital assistant app** is the **Google app**.
4. If not:
   - Tap **Digital assistant app** → **Default digital assistant app**.
   - Select the **Google app** from the available options.

---

## Set Up the Gemini Mobile App

Some Android devices come with Gemini as the primary assistant by default. On those devices, start Gemini by:

- Opening the **Gemini app**, or
- Activating Gemini by touch, such as:
  - Long pressing the power button
  - Swiping up from the corner of the screen

On other eligible Android devices using Google Assistant, you can get started with Gemini by:

- Receiving a notification from Google Assistant to switch to Gemini, or
- Downloading the **Gemini app** from the Google Play Store.

If you switch to Gemini:

- Gemini replaces Google Assistant as the primary assistant on your device.
- You can access Gemini with **“Hey Google”** if enabled.
- You can use the same touch methods previously used for Google Assistant.

> **Note:**  
> Gemini will replace Google Assistant on your other paired mobile devices, like smart watches, headphones, and earbuds with Google Assistant built in. On other non-mobile devices, including Smart Displays, smart speakers, TVs, cars, and Pixel Tablets that are locked, including in Hub Mode, Google Assistant will continue to respond to “Hey Google.”

> **Tip:**  
> If you previously chose not to try Gemini, you can revisit this choice later and switch to Gemini.

## Start a Conversation with Gemini

You can chat with Gemini in several ways depending on your device and settings.

---

### Chat by Typing

To type to Gemini:

1. Open the Gemini mobile app.
2. Use the on-screen keyboard.
3. Tap **Send**.

You can also type in Gemini’s **conversational overlay**, which appears when you say **“Hey Google”** or activate Gemini by touch.

---

### Chat with Your Voice

To speak to Gemini inside the app:

1. Open Gemini and tap **Microphone**.
2. Say your question or prompt.
3. The microphone stays open for up to **5 minutes**.
   - Tap **Stop** to pause.
   - Tap **Microphone** to resume.
4. Tap **Send** to share your prompt with Gemini.

If the Google app is your default Android assist app, you can also activate Gemini by:

- Saying **“Hey Google”**, if enabled.
  - After you speak your query, it updates automatically.
- Using device-specific touch activation:
  - Press and hold the power button.
  - Touch and hold Home.
  - Swipe up from the bottom corner of the screen.

# Use Gemini Apps — Computer (Android Help)

**Source:** <https://support.google.com/android/answer/13275745>

## Overview

Gemini Apps help users “supercharge” ideas by assisting with tasks such as:

- Brainstorming ideas, developing plans, and finding ways to get things done
- Summarizing complex topics in an easy-to-understand way
- Creating first drafts of outlines, emails, blog posts, poems, and more

> “Gemini Apps can make mistakes. When using Gemini Apps, double-check responses and don’t rely on responses from Gemini Apps for professional advice.”

---

## Requirements

You can use some Gemini Apps features without signing in, but signing in is required for additional features and to save Gemini Apps activity.

### Sign-in options

To sign in to Gemini Apps, you need one of the following:

- **A personal Google Account**
  - Family Link managers can turn off Gemini Apps access for users under 13, or the applicable age in their country, in **Google Family Link** settings.
- **A work Google Account with a qualifying Workspace edition**
  - You must be **18 or over**.
- **A school Google Account**
  - The service must be enabled by your institution’s administrator.

### Availability and browser support

- Supported languages and countries vary for:
  - Gemini web app
  - Gemini mobile app
- Work or school Google Account usage has additional guidance.
- To use **gemini.google.com**, you need a supported browser:
  - Chrome
  - Safari
  - Firefox
  - Opera
  - Edgium

---

## Start a Conversation in Chat

1. On your computer, go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. In the text box at the bottom, enter your question or prompt.
3. Optionally, add a file or image by clicking **Add files**.
   - Follow the on-screen instructions.
4. Click **Submit**.

### Tips

- To start a new chat:
  - Click **New chat** at the top left.
  - If you don’t see it, click **Menu** first.
- Depending on your location and device type, some features may be available without signing in.
- To access additional features or save activity, sign in with a Google Account.

---

## Start a Temporary Chat

A **temporary chat** does not appear in:

- Your Gemini Apps Activity
- Your recent chats

### Requirements

To start a temporary chat, you need:

- To be signed in to Gemini Apps
- A **personal Google Account**
  - Not available for work or school Google Accounts
- Access to the Gemini web or mobile app
  - Not available for Gemini in Chrome or Gemini in Google Messages

### How to start a temporary chat

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Next to **New chat**, click **Temporary chat**.
3. Enter your question or prompt in the text box.
4. Click **Submit**.

### Temporary chat tips

- Once you leave a temporary chat, you no longer have access to it.
- It won’t appear in recent chats.
- When using a temporary chat, the temporary chat icon appears at the top of the screen.

### What you can’t do in a temporary chat

Temporary chats do **not** support:

- Gems
- Connected Apps and integrated services that require **Keep Activity** to be turned on
- Personalized responses
- Saving information for future personalization
- Giving feedback about the response

---

## Choose the Gemini Model

> **Important:** To switch between models in Gemini Apps, you must be signed in.

### How to switch models

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. At the bottom, inside the text box, click the model name.
3. Select the model you want to use.

### Available models

- **Gemini Flash-Lite**
  - Efficient workhorse model designed for speed
  - Ideal for everyday tasks like summarization and brainstorming

- **Gemini Flash**
  - More powerful model that balances speed and reasoning
  - Suitable for a wide range of problems, from simple to complex

- **Gemini Pro**
  - Most advanced model
  - Excels at complex math and coding prompts
  - Offers high performance and reasoning
  - Has deeper understanding across text, files, images, and videos
  - Includes major upgrades for coding and studying
  - Responses generally take longer than other models

### Subscription note

If you have a Google AI subscription plan and hit your limit, you can continue chats with **Flash-Lite**.
