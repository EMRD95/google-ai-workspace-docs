# Connect the Google Workspace App to Gemini Apps — Summary

**Source:** Google Gemini Apps Help — “Connect the Google Workspace app to Gemini Apps”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/15229592

---

## Overview

Gemini Apps can connect to your Google Workspace data to help you work across Google services. Once connected, Gemini can retrieve, summarize, organize, and act on information from supported Workspace apps.

With the **Google Workspace app**, Gemini Apps can help you:

- Summarize, answer questions, and find information from services like **Gmail, Docs, and Drive**
- Add, edit, and retrieve **Google Tasks** and reminders
- Create and retrieve **Google Keep** notes and lists
- Create and manage **Google Calendar** events

> **Tip:** You can also get personalized Gemini experiences based on your content in Gmail when you connect the Google Workspace app.

---

## Requirements

To connect Google Workspace to Gemini Apps, you must:

- **Be signed in to the Gemini app**
  - Use the **same account** you use with Google Workspace.
- **Have Keep Activity turned on**
  - Gemini can’t connect to Google Workspace if this setting is off.

### Important Notes

- Only **some Google Workspace services** are currently available in **Live chats**.
- Gemini may provide incorrect or outdated information.

> **Important:** Gemini Apps can hallucinate responses or provide outdated information, like from an older email when there’s a more recent one. To check the information, select and review the sources listed after the response.

---

## How to Use the Google Workspace App in Gemini Apps

1. Sign in to Gemini with the same account you use for Google Workspace.
2. Ask Gemini to get information from Google Workspace.
3. If Google Workspace is not yet connected, Gemini will offer the option to connect it.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions.

You can disconnect the Google Workspace app later from **Connected Apps settings** at:  
https://gemini.google.com/apps

### Prompting Tip

Do **not** paste the URL of a Google document or email into your prompt. Instead:

- Use keywords from the content
- Specify the relevant service or content type
- Use `@` to select a Google Workspace service

> **Tip:** Don’t paste the URL of a document or email in your prompt. To reference content in Google Workspace, use keywords from the content and specify the service or content type. To specify a Google Workspace service, enter `@` and select the service.

- “What dates did Erik propose in the email about the Grand Canyon hiking trip?”
- “Find the most recent lease agreement from my Drive and check how much the security deposit was”

- “Find my resume titled June 2023 from my drive and summarize it to a short paragraph personal statement”

### Simplify and Organize Information

- “Find the doc from Ashley about the neighborhood clean-up project and summarize the proposal for me in 5 bullets”

### Capture Tasks in Google Tasks

- “Help me plan a camping trip and create a packing list for me.”

- “Recommend some places to visit in Kyoto and save them to Keep.”

### Check Schedule and Manage Calendar Events

- “What’s on my calendar today?”
- “Check the email that Alice sent about our trip to Tahoe this weekend, and add it to my calendar.”

## Why You Might Not Be Able to Connect Google Workspace

### Work or School Account Restrictions

For work and school accounts, a Google Workspace administrator must enable app connections first.

> **For work and school accounts, the Google Workspace administrator must first enable the ability to connect with apps.**

### Gmail Smart Features May Be Off

Gemini Apps require **Smart features in other Google products** to be enabled in Gmail settings.

If you turn this setting on:

1. Reload the Gemini app.
2. Try connecting Google Workspace again.

### You Must Mention Workspace Explicitly in New Chats

For every new conversation, explicitly ask Gemini to get or add something from your personal Google Workspace.

You can mention:

- **Gmail** or email
- **Docs** or documents
- **Drive** or PDFs
- **Calendar** or events
- **Tasks** or to-dos
- **Keep** or notes

You do **not** need to repeat this in follow-up questions within the same chat.

Example:

- **First prompt:** “Summarize my recent emails from Harper elementary school”
- **Next prompt:** “When is pajama day?”

# Create docs, apps & more with Canvas — Gemini Apps Help

**Source:** https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16047321  
**Platform covered:** Computer  
**Product:** Gemini Apps / Canvas

---

## Overview

Canvas in Gemini lets you collaborate with Gemini to **create or edit docs, apps, slides, code, quizzes, Audio Overviews, infographics, web pages, and other formats**.

> **Important:** To use this feature, you must be signed in to Gemini Apps.

Canvas supports:
- Creating new docs, slide decks, quizzes, apps, and other content
- Editing generated docs and apps
- Direct code editing
- Adding Gemini-powered AI features to apps
- Exporting to Google Docs, Google Slides, PDFs, Colab, or copying content
- Sharing Canvas apps/content through public links
- Copying and editing shared Canvas content, with age and platform restrictions

---

## Start a New Chat with Canvas

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Below the text box, click **Add Files** → **Canvas**.
3. Enter your prompt in the text box.
   - **Tip:** If you want a specific format or document type, such as a slide deck, include that in your prompt.
4. Optional: Click **Add files** to upload files or an image with your prompt.
5. Click **Submit**.

### Example prompts

> Create a slide presentation about…

> Create a quiz to test me on…

---

## Apps in Canvas

### Edit Apps & Code

You can edit an app either by:
- Asking Gemini to make changes using the prompt box
- Directly editing the app’s code

**Changes in Canvas are auto-saved.**

#### Open code, errors, logs, and changes

- To open and edit code: click **Code** in the top-right of the Canvas panel.
- To view preview errors and logs: click **Show console**.
- To view recent code changes: click **Code** → **Show recent changes**.

---

### Ask Gemini to Edit Selected Parts of an App

1. Open the chat with the app you want to edit.
2. In the bottom-right of the Canvas panel, click **Select & ask**.
3. Use the cursor to select the section you want to edit.
4. Enter a prompt describing the update you want.
5. Press **Enter**.

---

### Add AI to an App

> **Important:** To add AI to your app, you must be signed in to Gemini Apps and be **18 or over**.

You can add Gemini-powered features such as:
- Text generation
- Image generation

Steps:

1. Open the chat with the app you want to edit.
2. In the bottom-right of the Canvas panel, click **Add Gemini features**.
3. Gemini will provide an overview of the AI feature added to the app.
4. You can reply in the text box and ask Gemini to make additional changes.

---

## Docs in Canvas

### Edit Docs in Canvas

You can edit docs directly in Canvas or ask Gemini to help via the prompt box.

**Changes are auto-saved.**

#### Ask Gemini to edit selected text

1. Open the chat you want to edit.
2. In the Canvas panel, select the text you want to edit.
3. Enter a prompt explaining what you want updated.
4. Press **Enter**.

**Tip:** After selecting text, you can also click **Suggest edits** to have Gemini recommend changes.

---

### Ask Gemini to Make Quick Edits

1. Open the chat you want to edit.
2. In the Canvas panel, choose a quick-edit option:
   - **Change length** — adjust the length of text.
   - **Change tone** — make the text more casual or formal.

---

### Edit Text Style & Formatting in a Doc

1. Open the chat you want to edit.
2. Select the text in the Canvas panel.
3. Use the toolbar to format the text.

Available formatting options include:
- Stylize headings
- **Bold text**
- *Italicize text*
- Create bulleted lists
- Create numbered lists

**Tips:**
- If the full toolbar is not visible, click **More**.
- To view saved versions, click **Previous Version** or **Next Version** at the top.

---

LaTeX is described as:

> LaTeX is a document preparation system widely used in academic, scientific, and technical fields to create high-quality documents with complex formatting, such as mathematical equations, tables, and multi-column layouts.

### Exporting or Sharing Docs with LaTeX

- You can export a doc containing LaTeX as a **PDF**, with preview before download.
- You can copy any Gemini response that includes LaTeX; the copied content will include the **unrendered LaTeX code**.

Available options:

  Enter a prompt describing what you want to turn the report or doc into.

# Create & Manage AI Mini-Apps / Custom Workflows as **Gems from Google Labs**

**Source:** [Gemini Apps Help](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16802014?hl=en)  
**Product area:** Gemini web app, Gems, Google Labs / Opal

---

## Overview

**Gems from Google Labs** are custom AI mini-apps or workflows you can create, edit, remix, and share in the Gemini web app. They are powered by **Opal**, a Google Labs experiment, and let users build app-like AI workflows using natural language.

> You can create, edit, and share custom workflows bundled into AI mini-apps. These AI mini-apps are called Gems from Google Labs in the Gemini web app. They’re powered by Opal, a Google Labs experiment.

Users can:

- Create AI mini-apps from a text description.
- Edit app workflows by describing desired changes.
- Remix premade AI mini-apps from Google Labs.
- Share AI mini-apps with links, specific people, or public access.
- Use multiple AI models for workflows, including text, image, video, and music models.

> **Important:** This is an experimental feature and part of ongoing work to enhance Gemini experiences. This experiment will help us gather your feedback and refine the experience further.

---

## Requirements & Availability

To use Gems from Google Labs, you must:

- Be **18 or over**.
- Sign in to the Gemini web app with a **personal Google Account**.
- Use the same personal Google Account when signing in to **Opal**.

### Current limitations

> **Important:**
>
> - For now, this feature is available in the Gemini web app and in English only.
> - For now, you can create and edit Gems from Google Labs on a computer only.
> - For now, this feature isn’t available in the Gemini mobile app, Gemini in Google Messages, or Gemini in Chrome.

Additional limitation:

- Not available to users with **work or school Google Accounts**.
- Classic Gems **cannot currently be converted** into Gems from Google Labs.

---

## Create an AI Mini-App

You create AI mini-apps from the **Gem manager** in Gemini.

### Steps

1. On your computer, go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. On the left, click **Gems**.
3. Next to **“My Gems from Labs,”** click **New Gem**.
   - First-time users must review disclosures and follow on-screen instructions to sign in to **Opal**.
   - Use the same personal Google Account as Gemini.
4. In the text box at the bottom, describe the AI mini-app or workflow you want to create.
   - You can also click an example prompt for inspiration.
5. Click **Submit**.
   - Complex apps may take a few minutes to create and run.
   - Keep the chat open until the app is ready.
   - You can open another browser window or tab to start another chat.
6. When ready, Gemini shows:
   - The app workflow as steps.
   - A preview of the app.
7. Review the steps and click **Start app** to try it.
8. To add or change steps, describe your edits in the text box below the workflow.

### Tips

- You can remix premade AI mini-apps and customize them.
- Classic Gems cannot currently be converted into Gems from Google Labs.

---

## Find & Use an AI Mini-App

You can find both premade and personally created AI mini-apps in the **Gem manager**.

### Steps

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/) on a computer.
2. Click **Gems** on the left.
3. Browse the available AI mini-apps in two sections:
   - **Gems made by Labs:** Premade AI mini-apps provided by Google Labs.
   - **My Gems from Labs:** AI mini-apps you created or remixed.
4. Click the AI mini-app you want to use.
5. Click **Start**.

Related data information: [Learn about how your data from My Gems from Labs is handled](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13594961#gems_from_labs_data).

---

## Remix a Premade AI Mini-App

Premade apps from Google Labs can be edited and saved as your own.

### Steps

1. On your computer, go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Gems** on the left.
3. Under **“Gems made by Labs,”** select the premade AI mini-app you want to edit.
4. In the text box below the steps, describe your edits.

---

## Share an AI Mini-App

You can share AI mini-apps you create or remix.

### Steps

1. On your computer, go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click **Gems** on the left.
3. Click the AI mini-app you want to share.
4. Above the AI mini-app preview, click **Share**.
5. Choose how to share:
   - **Share a link:** Click **Copy link**, then paste it in a messaging app, email, forum thread, etc.
   - **Share with specific people by email:** Enter the email addresses of the people you want to share with.
   - **Manage general access:** Under **“General access,”** click the down arrow and choose who can access the app.
6. Click **Save**.

### General access options

- **Public:** Anyone can search on Google and access your AI mini-app without signing in to a Google Account.
- **Anyone with the link:** Anyone with the link can use the AI mini-app without signing in to a Google A

# Schedule Actions in Gemini Apps — Summary

**Source:** [Google Gemini Apps Help — Schedule actions in Gemini Apps](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/16316416)  
**Platform covered:** Computer / web app

## Overview

Gemini Apps can schedule recurring actions as part of daily, weekly, or monthly routines. Scheduled actions let Gemini generate content automatically at a chosen time, such as news digests, creative inspiration, skill-learning suggestions, calendar summaries, weather forecasts, and more.

Gemini prepares scheduled content in the background so it is ready by the delivery time.

---

## Key Excerpts

> “You can make Gemini a part of your daily, weekly, and monthly routines by scheduling recurring actions.”

> “You can set a delivery time that works for your schedule. Gemini will prepare your content in the background so it’s ready by the scheduled time.”

> **Important:** You can have up to 10 active scheduled actions at a time.

> “Gemini will prepare your content within the hour leading up to your delivery time to help ensure you get the latest information.”

> “Responses for scheduled actions are prepared in advance to guarantee timely delivery. As a result, rapidly changing data, like stock prices, won’t be the latest when you receive them.”

> “Scheduled actions work best for daily summaries and wrap-ups.”

---

## Requirements

To schedule actions in Gemini Apps, you must meet **both** of the following requirements:

### 1. Sign in with an eligible account

You need one of these account types:

- A **personal Google Account** with a **Google AI Pro or Ultra subscription**
- A **work or school Google Account** with access to a **qualifying edition of Google Workspace**

Relevant links from the article:

- [Learn how to upgrade](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/14517446)
- [Qualifying Google Workspace editions](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/14620100)
- [Learn how to sign in to Gemini Apps](https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13278668)

# Use & Manage Connected Apps in Gemini — Summary

**Source:** Google Gemini Apps Help — “Use & manage Connected Apps in Gemini”  
**URL:** https://support.google.com/gemini/answer/13695044  
**Platform focus:** Computer / Gemini web app, with notes for Android, iOS, and other devices.

---

## Core Idea

Gemini Apps can connect to other apps and services to complete user requests and provide more useful, personalized responses. With permission, Gemini can access information and content from connected apps, such as Gmail, Google Calendar, Spotify, Google Photos, WhatsApp, smart home tools, and more.

> “Gemini Apps can connect to other apps to complete your request and give you more helpful responses. With your permission, Gemini can also help you with your info and content in other apps.”

---

## What You Can Ask Gemini to Do with Connected Apps

Gemini can help across several categories:

### Productivity

- Summarize emails from Gmail.
- Create events in Google Calendar or third-party calendar apps.
- Help with code from GitHub.
- Interact with Google Workspace apps such as Docs, Drive, Keep, and Tasks.

### Media

- Play music from YouTube Music or Spotify.
- Search Google Photos or Samsung Gallery.

### Communication

- Make calls and send messages using default Android apps.
- Send messages through WhatsApp on Android devices.
- Find and manage details about people using Contacts.

### Device Control

- Manage smart home devices through Google Home.
- Use Android Utilities to:
  - Set alarms and timers.
  - Open websites, apps, and settings.
  - Check and control device features.

## Important Account and Availability Notes

### Personal vs. Work or School Accounts

This help page is for users accessing Gemini Apps with a **personal Google Account**.

> **Important for users with a work or school account:** The info below is for users who access Gemini Apps with a personal account. If you’re using a work or school account, learn how to use Connected Apps in Gemini with a work and school Google Account.

### Google Messages Limitation

> **Important:** For now, Gemini can’t use Connected Apps in Gemini in Google Messages.

### Availability Varies

Connected Apps availability depends on:

- Location
- Language
- Device
- Gemini app or platform
- Country
- Specific app support

> **Important:** Availability of Connected Apps vary by location, language, device, and Gemini app.

Apps available only on Android may not appear in Connected Apps settings on iOS or the Gemini web app.

## Getting Started with Connected Apps

To use Connected Apps:

1. **Sign in to Gemini Apps.**
   - You must be signed in to use Connected Apps.

2. **Check your Keep Activity setting.**
   - Connected Apps depend heavily on whether Gemini’s **Keep Activity** setting is on.

3. **Choose which apps Gemini connects to.**
   - Apps can be connected or disconnected anytime in Connected Apps settings.

### Keep Activity Behavior

> “When Keep Activity is off, Connected Apps won’t be available on gemini.google.com, iOS devices, or smart watches.”

On Android devices, if Keep Activity is off, only these apps remain available:

- Utilities
- Phone
- Messages
- WhatsApp

## How to Use Connected Apps on Computer

To use Connected Apps in Gemini web:

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Enter your question or prompt in the text box.
3. To specify an app, type:

```text
@
```

Then select the app.

4. If the selected app is not connected:
   - Gemini may connect it automatically, or
   - Gemini may ask for permission.
5. Click Submit.
6. Follow any on-screen instructions.

> “If an app is available, Gemini automatically uses it.”

- [Browse Connected Apps](https://gemini.google.com/apps)

## Managing Which Apps Gemini Works With

You can connect or disconnect apps anytime.

### Steps on Computer

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/).
2. Click:
   - **Settings & help**
   - **Connected Apps**

   If not visible:
   - Click **Personal Intelligence**
   - Then **Connected Apps**

3. Find the app you want to manage.
4. Toggle the app:
   - Turn it on to connect.
   - Turn it off to disconnect.
5. Follow any on-screen instructions.

### Data and Privacy Notes

Google points users to the Gemini Apps Privacy Hub for:

- What happens when an app is disconnected.
- How data is handled when using Connected Apps.
