# Create a List with AI in Google Keep — Summary

**Source:** [Google Keep Help](https://support.google.com/keep/answer/14626262?hl=en)  
**Feature:** AI-generated lists in Google Keep

---

## Availability

> **Important:** This feature is available to users with Google AI Pro and Google AI Ultra, or a Pixel mobile device.

- The AI list creation feature is available in **Google Keep**.
- It lets users create a new list using the **“Help me create a list”** prompt.
- The feature is currently available on **Android**.

---

## How to Use AI to Create a List in Google Keep

1. Open the **Google Keep** app on your Android device.
2. Tap **Create**.
3. In the bottom-right corner, tap **Help me create a list**.
4. Enter a prompt.

   Example prompts:

   - `"Packing list for a camping trip with 2 kids in summer"`
   - `"Classic movies to watch on Halloween"`
   - `"Spring cleaning checklist for a 2-bedroom apartment"`

   > **Tip:** For better results, write specific instructions. For example: `"Groceries for a week for a vegetarian family of 3."`

5. Tap **Create**.
6. Optional actions after the list is generated:
   - **Send feedback about the suggestion:** Tap **Good suggestion** or **Bad suggestion**.
   - **Edit your prompt:** Tap the prompt at the top of the window, revise it, then tap **Refresh**.
7. Tap **Insert** to add the generated list to your note.

---

### Feedback Steps

2. If selecting **Bad suggestion**, choose the issue found and optionally enter additional feedback.
3. Optional: Select **What data will be attached?** to review data included with the feedback.
   - To exclude collected data, uncheck:
     > `"Attach collected data to your feedback to help us improve the product experience"`
4. Select **Submit**.
